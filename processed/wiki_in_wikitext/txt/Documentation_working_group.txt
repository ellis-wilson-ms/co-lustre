The [http://www.opensfs.org OpenSFS] Lustre Documentation Working Group (DWG) is the place the where the participants of OpenSFS participate in Lustre documentation creation and improvement.

== Goals ==

A non-exhaustive list of goals for this community include:

* Review of extant Lustre documentation for errors, staleness, and missing content
* Creation of presently non-existent Lustre documentation
* Revision of Lustre documentation that includes stale information or errors
* Prioritization of Lustre documentation efforts
* Streamlining Lustre uptake with beginner administrator and developer information

== Email List ==

Please join the [http://lists.opensfs.org/listinfo.cgi/dwg-opensfs.org DWG Email List] to stay up-to-date on the progress and on-goings of the DWG.
