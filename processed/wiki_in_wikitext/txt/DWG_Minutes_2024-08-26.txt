== Minutes ==

Introductory meeting.  Meet and greet with all members present, and basic ideation and goals for the Lustre Documentation Working Group were laid out.

All attendees were solicited for their interest areas with respect to documentation, and the following resulted:

* Ellis – Improve HSM material in the Lustre Manual
* Tom – HSM docs in the Lustre Manual – potentially reorg manual into more manageable components
* Arshad – LU-9636 (in-kernel documentation of man pages), Review of man pages and improving the volume of examples present therein
* Cyrus – Developer documentation (lfsck/lnet)
* Xiaolin - Improve HSM Docs
* Jason - Potential reorganization of the Lustre manual into more defined sections; improve docs for individuals to build Lustre locally (Mac)
* Anjus – Lustre Internals Doc continuation; updating Wiki surrounding the source code
* Peter – How to guides for various tasks
* Shane – Best practices for physical components of cluster geometry

Initial effort is to be focused on the Lustre Wiki.  Ellis to follow-up with an organized list of pages that have not been updated in more than a few years.
