OpenSFS hosted a Lustre Developer Day in Portland, Oregon on April 4, 2016.  The meeting was held the day before [[Lustre_User_Group_2016|Lustre User Group 2016]] began.

The agenda was as follows:

* Welcome and Logistics - Peter Jones, Intel
* [[Media:DevelopersDay2016.pptx|Various Topics]] - James Simmons, ORNL
* [[Media:LUG2016-File_Level_Redundancy.pdf|File Level Redundancy]] - Andreas Dilger, Intel
* [[Media:ASCAR-ldd16-yanli-public.pdf|ASCAR: Increasing Performance Through Automated Contention Management]] - Yan Li, UCSC
* [[Media:BenEvans-LUGDevDay2016.pptx|Identification and Elimination of Technical Debt]] - Ben Evans, Cray
* [[Media:Lustre-Developer-day-2016-4.pdf|Upstreaming of Features from FEFS]] - Shinji Sumimoto, Fujitsu
* Coverage Analysis Automation in Lustre Testing - Alexander Lezhoev, Seagate
* Bulk MDT Scans/Replacing KUC - Henri Doreau, CEA
* [[Media:Hsm-agent-rread.pdf|OpenSource HSM Agent and Movers]] - Robert Read, Intel
* Wrap up - Peter Jones, Intel
