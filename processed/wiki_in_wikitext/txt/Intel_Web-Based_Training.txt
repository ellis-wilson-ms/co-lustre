Intel has created free training for Lustre at their [http://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/software/web-based-training.html Web-Based Training Portal]. This training applies to users of Intel's Enterprise Edition as well as users without Intel support. For users of community releases, Intel has created a breakdown of which modules apply, available [http://pages.intel.com/a0300Z02YP0000CYVWv36Xl here] under "Lustre Users without Intel Support".
