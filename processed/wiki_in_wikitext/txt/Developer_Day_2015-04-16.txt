The agenda was as follows:

* [[Media:LUG2015-toward-patchless-ldiskfs.pdf|Patchless ldiskfs servers]] - Andreas Dilger
* [[Media:Lock_Ahead_-_Technical_Discussion.pptx|Lock Ahead and Strided Locking]] – Patrick Farrell
* Security – Stephen Simms
* [[Media:Sebastien_Buisson_Security_DevDay_v1.pdf|Kerberos]] – Sebastien Buisson
* [[Media:OpenZFS_Performance_Improvements.pdf|ZFS and Lustre]] – Brian Behlendorf
* Low level MDT scans – Henri Doreau
* Move lustre tests to external repository – Elena Grayznova
