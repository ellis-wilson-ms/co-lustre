[https://www.rc.ufl.edu/lug-2021-agenda/ LUG 2021] was to be held in Gainesville, FL on May 19th-20th at the [https://www.rc.ufl.edu/ University of Florida Research Computing].

The event was instead held virtually due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic.
