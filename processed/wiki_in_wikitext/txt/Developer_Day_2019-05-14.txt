OpenSFS hosted a Lustre Developer Day in Houston, TX on May 14th, 2019. The meeting was held the day before [[Lustre User Group 2019]].

The agenda was as follows:

* [[Media:LUG2019-Developer_Day-OST_Pool_Quotas-Cray.pdf|OST Pool Quotas]] (Cray)
* [[Media:LUG2019-Developer_Day-ls_Long_Term_Storage-Rutman.pdf|Efficient 'ls -l', Lustre for long-term storage]] (Nathan Rutman)
* [[Media:LUG2019-Developer_Day-Lustre_Upstreaming-Brown.pdf|Upstreaming: buy-in, process, pain points]] (Neil Brown)
* [[Media:LUG2019-Developer_Day-Leveraging_Newer_Kernels-Simmons.pdf|Leveraging options in newer Linux kernels]] (James Simmons)
* OpenSFS Requirement Gathering Discussion (Ken Rawlings)
* LNet Network Namespace (Aurélien Degremont)
* [[Media:LUG2019-Developer_Day-ldiskfs_Status_Foreign_Layouts-Dilger.pdf|Ldiskfs Status, Foreign Layouts]] (Andreas Dilger)
* [[Media:LUG2019-Developer_Day-Security_Encryption-Buisson.pdf|Security and Encryption]] (Sebastien Buisson)
* [[Media:LUG2019-Developer_Day-Fujitsu_Development_Plans-Sumimoto.pdf|Fujitsu Development Plans]] (Shinji Sumimoto)
