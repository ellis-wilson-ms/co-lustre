!Note: Despite the page title referencing IRC, the Lustre community has largely moved from IRC to '''Slack'''. The primary Slack workspace is [https://lustre.slack.com lustre.slack.com]. See the [[#Slack|Slack]] section below for join links.

=== Mailing Lists ===

General
* [http://lists.lustre.org/listinfo.cgi/lustre-announce-lustre.org lustre-announce] A low traffic announcements-only mailing list.  Keep up to date with the latest Lustre news and events.
** [http://lists.lustre.org/pipermail/lustre-announce-lustre.org/ lustre-announce archives]
* [http://lists.lustre.org/listinfo.cgi/lustre-discuss-lustre.org lustre-discuss] The main mailing list for Lustre users and administrators. Discuss all aspects of designing, installing, configuring, using, and maintaining a Lustre filesystem.
** [http://lists.lustre.org/pipermail/lustre-discuss-lustre.org/ lustre-discuss archives]
* [http://lists.lustre.org/listinfo.cgi/lustre-devel-lustre.org lustre-devel] The primary mailing list for discussing Lustre software development.
** [http://lists.lustre.org/pipermail/lustre-devel-lustre.org/ lustre-devel archives]

Working Groups
* [http://lists.opensfs.org/listinfo.cgi/lwg-opensfs.org lwg] The [http://wiki.opensfs.org/Lustre_Working_Group OpenSFS Lustre Working Group] (LWG) is the place the where the participants of OpenSFS come together to coordinate their software development efforts for the Lustre high-performance, Open Source, parallel filesystem.
* [http://lists.opensfs.org/listinfo.cgi/dwg-opensfs.org dwg] The [https://wiki.opensfs.org/Documentation_Working_Group OpenSFS Documentation Working Group] (DWG) allows collaboration to improve the [https://doc.lustre.org/lustre_manual.xhtml Lustre Operations Manual] and other Lustre-related documentation (this wiki, man pages, etc.) to help new and experienced Lustre users find the information they need.
* [http://lists.opensfs.org/listinfo.cgi/lustre.org-opensfs.org lustre-org] The [http://wiki.lustre.org/Lustre.org_Working_Group Lustre.org Working Group] (LOWG) manages the lustre.org web domain and its associated content.
