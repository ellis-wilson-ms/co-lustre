OpenSFS hosted a Lustre Developer Day in Bloomington, Indiana on May 30th, 2017, along with the first ever Lustre Hackathon.  The meeting was held the day before [[Lustre_User_Group_2017|Lustre User Group 2017]] began.

The agenda was as follows:

*  Developer Day Welcome and Logistics - Peter Jones, Intel
*  [[Media:LUG2017-Intel-Developer-Day-Update-Dilger.pdf|Intel Development Update]] - Andreas Dilger, Intel
*  [[Media:LUG2017-ORNL-Tracepoints-Simmons.pdf|Tracepoints]] - James Simmons, ORNL
*  [[Media:LUG2017-DDN-Lustre-QOS-LiXi.pdf|Lustre QOS]] - Li Xi, DDN
*  [[Media:LUG2017-DDN-Lustre-Security-Buisson.pdf|Lustre Security]] - Sebastien Buisson, DDN
*  [[Media:LUG2017-LLNL-ZFS-MMP.pdf|MMP for Lustre on ZFS]] - Olaf Faaland, LLNL
*  [[Media:LUG2017-LLNL-OpenZFS-for-Lustre.pdf|OpenZFS Developments Impacting Lustre]] - Brian Behlendorf, LLNL
*  [[Media:LUG2017-ORNL-Upstream-Client-Simmons.pdf|Upstream Lustre Client]] - James Simmons, ORNL
*  [[Media:LUG2017-Fujitsu-Development-Update-Sumimoto.pdf|Fujitsu Development Update]] - Shinji Sumimoto, Fujitsu
*  Developer Day Wrap up - Peter Jones, Intel
*  [[Media:LUG2017-Hackathon-Projects.pdf|Hackathon Project Ideas]] - Andreas Dilger, Intel
