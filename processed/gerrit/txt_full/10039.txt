Patch Set 2: Code-Review-1

as I understand this reprocess is needed for 2 things:

1. replayed waiting locks are just placed to waiting queue without extra check conflict exists, no new bl ast is sent and (!!!) locks are not placed to waiting callback list. if the conflicting client has been evicted, they could be granted, but nobody checks it and locks may be left in the queue forever.

2. an error occurs after the lock is placed to the waiting list and cancelled  without granting, next waiting lock could be granted - a reprocess is needed. e.g. ldlm_handle_enqueue0 checks if export is still valid, and cancel lock if so.
