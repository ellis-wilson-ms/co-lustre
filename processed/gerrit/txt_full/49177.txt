--- Commit Message ---
LU-16319 osd: Allow fiemap on user and kernel buffers

Set the otherwise unused BIT(31) on fiemap_extent_info fi_flags
to indicate the fiemap extent buffer is allocated in kernel space.

In ll_fiemap perform in kernel memcpy or user copy based on
fi_flags BIT(31).
