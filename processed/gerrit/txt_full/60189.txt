--- Commit Message ---
LU-19173 build: memcg tasks should restrict dirty pages

Dirty page accounting should be aware of memcg limit when
the struct mem_cgroup is available and the memory limit
for the cgroup can be determined.

This makes lustre aware of memcg limit and restricts dirty pages
accounting to both system memory and memcg limits.

Fixes: c413d2ede5 ("LU-19014 memcg: fix client hang in balance_dirty_pages()")
