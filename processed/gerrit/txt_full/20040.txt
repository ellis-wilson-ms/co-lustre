Patch Set 5: Code-Review-1

I'm not sure this is correct for ldiskfs.  According to the ldev.conf.5 man page, the only allowed prefixes before the device-path are md: and zfs:.  Your new test seems to assume that ldiskfs: needs to be allowed, which may be new, undocumented functionality.

It was assumed in the past that a lack of zfs: label meant it was an ldiskfs filesystem.  I is not obvious to me that the default is currently coded in correctly.

If that has all been considered, at minimum this requires an update to ldev.conf.5.
