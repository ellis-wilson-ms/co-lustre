LU-2396 ofd: Remove unmaintained brw_stats

/proc/fs/lustre/obdfilter/*/{brw_stats,exports/*/brw_stats}.  If the
corresponding osd has a brw_stats proc entry then add a symlink from
the ofd proc dir.

> technically the patch looks good, but i'm not sure we actually want to get rid of the stats.

We (meaning you ;)) got rid of the stats during OSD restructuring. This just removes some pointless proc files and struct fields. There is also very little chance of us restoring these stats to their former functionality. In osd-ldiskfs we are not passing exp down to osd_do_bio().
