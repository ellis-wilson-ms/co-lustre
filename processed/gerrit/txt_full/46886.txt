LU-15621 wbc: free buffer when batched RPC is committed

Add test srcipt sanity-wbc.sh test_106 for replay recovery of the
batched RPC.

It should not free the registered buffer for bulk I/O upon the
reply of the batched RPC. It should be in the commit callback,
called when request is committed and about to be freed.
Otherwise, it will cause the replay recovery failure.
