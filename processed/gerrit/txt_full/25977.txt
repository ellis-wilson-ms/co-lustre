I noticed there was a bug in my awk script.  I was checking for (NR>1) instead of (NR>0).  I've corrected the bug, and added the while loop as suggested.

I tried several different ways to search for the newline on the final line, but most utilities strip off the newline characters while processing, making it impossible to tell if it was actually present in the input.  The awk script I provided is the only way I found that was capable of both skipping empty files and testing for a terminating newline on the final line of input.
