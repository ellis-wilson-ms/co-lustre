LU-17583 llite: getattr/open should not revalidate dentry

ll_getattr() and ll_intent_file_open() do not perform a lookup, it
get the attr and ldlm locks by FID (inode). So this should not
revalidate the dentry, otherwise it may produce dir cache
inconsistencies (e.g: with cwd fd).

Add a regression test: sanityn 31s, 31t

Lustre-change: https://review.whamcloud.com/54354
Lustre-commit: TBD (831126a8f4c6da367a8e1e1c14c33671e06c7667)

This backport needs to update dir depth in ll_revalidate_dentry(),
revalidate_statahead_dentry() and ll_lookup_it_finish() as this is
no longer done in ll_inode_revalidate().
On master, those were brought by:
388a185 ("LU-15971 llite: implicit default LMV inherit")
