LU-1842 ptlrpc: clean up ptlrpc_client setup

Remove client-specific information from client_obd_setup() and
instead have the caller pass in their client-specific information
when setting up a new connection.  This removes a lot of special-case
logic from the ptlrpc code that adds complexity for no benefit.
