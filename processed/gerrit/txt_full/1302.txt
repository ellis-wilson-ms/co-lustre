--- Commit Message ---
TT-148 test: workaround for negative time shifts

Try to workaround negative test timestamps being seen in Maloo.
It appears that the negative time shifts are always even multiples
of 7h (= -25200s) so try to compensate for this when printing out
test durations and the test completion summary line.

The fix doesn't hard-code 25200s offset, since I suspect this is
somehow related to confusion by the client/host between UTC and
PDT (where the test clusters are hosted) and this offset will change
as daylight savings time changes.
