--- Commit Message ---
LU-16540 clio: Don't count owned DIO pages

Because the clio is accessed in parallel in parallel DIO
submission, accesses must be read only or atomic.

The ci_owned_nr is a sanity check and would be costly to
do with an atomic, so we just turn it off for dio pages.
