--- Message (Andreas Dilger, 2011-09-09 10:33:39.000000000) ---
Patch Set 1: I would prefer that you didn't submit this

(55 inline comments)

Lai, I know that you didn't request an inspection of your patch, but a lot of the changes that you are making here are useful if they would land on master for 2.2, instead of being kept in a pnfs patch that may not land for a long time.  It would be unfortunate if others spend time fixing these problems again for 2.6.38 or later kernels, or if such changes are back-ported to one of the vendor kernels.

It would be better if you could rebase your patch against Yang Sheng's LU-506 patch series, and then add a series of incremental patches that fix the various problems that are in this patch.  Each patch should focus on fixing a single problem (as the LU-506 patch series does).

Having a series of separate patches will not only make patch inspection easier, it will also make it easier to land those patches onto master, and hopefully will reduce the overall maintenance effort you have as those patches are landed into master.
