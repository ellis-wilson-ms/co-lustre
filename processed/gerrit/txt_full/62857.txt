LU-19654 tests: increase SHUTDOWN_ATTEMPTS to 5

While running failover tests with FAILURE_MODE=HARD,
sometimes the test node was not powered off after
attempting 3 times. This patch increase the attempts
to try to avoid the failures.

Test-Parameters: trivial \
  env=SLOW=yes,ENABLE_QUOTA=yes,FAILURE_MODE=HARD \
  clientcount=4 mdtcount=1 mdscount=2 osscount=2 \
  austeroptions=-R failover=true iscsi=1 \
  clientdistro=el8.10 serverdistro=el8.10 \
  fstype=ldiskfs testgroup=failover-part-2
