LU-252 2.6.37 removes bp arg from dump_trace()

combination of task and regs is sufficient to determine an appropriate
value for bp.  Red Hat backported this to 2.6.32-107.el6 so it has hit
current CHAOS kernels and will show up in RHEL 6.1.
