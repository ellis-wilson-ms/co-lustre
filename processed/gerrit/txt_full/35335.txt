For $MOUNT_OPTS, the "-o" is assumed to be there. So, for this test, we could:
mount_client $MOUNT "$MOUNT_OPTS,ro"
or
MOUNT_OPTS=$(csa_add "-o $MOUNT_OPTS" -o ro)
or
