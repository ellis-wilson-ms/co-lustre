LU-1206 mdt: Call mdt_write_allow on mo_open error

In the case that mdt_mfd_open is called with the MDS_FMODE_EXEC flag
set, but mo_open returns an error, the mdt_write_deny call is not
properly balanced with a mdt_write_allow call. This patch adds the
needed invocation of mdt_write_allow.
