--- Commit Message ---
LU-12678 lnet: discard lnet_sock_accept()

The only advantage of lnet_sock_accept() over kernel_accept()
appears to be that it can be interrupted by calling
  sk->sk_state_change()

kernel_accept() can be interrupted by sending a signal
to the process, and as it is only used from a dedicated process
we can easily use a signal to interrupt.

So discard lnet_sock_accept(), use kernel_accept(), and send
a signal.
