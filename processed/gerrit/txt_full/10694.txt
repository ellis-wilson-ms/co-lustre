LU-1717 ldlm: Fix rs_opc initialization

By the time target_send_reply() initializes rs_opc, rs_msg has not
been filled with a valid opc yet.  Following Oleg's suggestion on the
Jira ticket, this patch changes target_send_reply() to initialize
rs_opc with rq_reqmsg instead and silences a couple of related
warnings that are of only informative nature.
