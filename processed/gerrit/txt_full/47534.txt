LU-15912 config: support autoconf 2.71

autoconf 2.71 causes lots of deprecation warning and some errors.
"autoupdate" can be used to update the files.

This patch is the result of running autoupdate, then cleaning up a
bit.  It allows "./configure" to work on openSUSE Tumbleweed which
has 2.71
