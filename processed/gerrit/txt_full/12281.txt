LU-2675 libcfs: ignore CDEBUG_ENTRY_EXIT for userspace

In userspace define GOTO() and RETURN() to not call libcfs_log_goto()
or libcfs_log_return(). Remove the definition of uses of the obsolete
__arch_lib__ symbol.
