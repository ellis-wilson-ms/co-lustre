LU-19469 llite: add hole pages into client LRU cache list

It should add hole pages into client LRU cache list and manage
them by client-side page cache LRU mechanism.
Thus they can be reclaimed under the memory pressure or moved into
unevictable list when they are mlocked.

Otherwise, it failed sanity/test_600b.
