--- Commit Message ---
LU-18998 lnet: match size of LNet fault handling

Smatch reports in lnet_fault_attr_to_fault4()

__builtin_memcpy() '&attr->u' too small (16 vs 64)

This is due to struct lnet_fault_large_attr missing some
padding that is in struct lnet_fault_attr. Add the missing
padding. In the future as more rules are added to
struct lnet_fault_large_attr this padding will not matter.
