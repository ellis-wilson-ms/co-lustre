LU-398 nrs: Do not include NRS in client-only builds

Do not include NRS in client only builds, and also include only a
minimal feature set for liblustre, i.e. the framework and the FIFO
policy, as since liblustre is single-threaded, it does not make much
sense for it to make use of more elaborate policies.
