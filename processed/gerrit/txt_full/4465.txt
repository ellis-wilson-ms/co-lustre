--- Commit Message ---
LU-XXXX o2iblnd: Add histograms in /proc

This change is intended to increase visibility into the o2iblnd layer by
exporting certain information to /proc. Two histograms are exported by
this change.

The first shows the time spent on the ibc_active_txs queue by transmit
packets. This tells us how long it took the IB layer to actually send a
message once Lustre handed it off.

The second shows the time that the kib_conn_t sat on the kib_sched_list
queue.
