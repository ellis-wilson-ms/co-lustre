--- Commit Message ---
LU-18022 lmv: quota-aware MDT selection on -EDQUOT

When mkdir hits -EDQUOT on an MDT, instead of failing immediately,
retry the operation on another active MDT.

The client walks the MDT list to find the next usable target. if
suitable target is found, the mkdir is retried there, otherwise the
original -EDQUOT is returned.

This improves user experience by automatically selecting an MDT with
available quota space.
