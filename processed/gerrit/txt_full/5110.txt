LU-2641 llite: avoid warning in simple_setattr with reg inodes

Commit eef2380c (v2.6.35) added a warning to simple_setattr to
be issued if the function was called with an inode with a
->truncate operation.

Assuming the comment is correct above the existing simple_setattr
call, ATTR_SIZE won't be set and we can use our own copy of
simple_setattr that doesn't warn or call vmtruncate at all.
