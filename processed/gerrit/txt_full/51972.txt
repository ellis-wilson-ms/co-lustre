--- Commit Message ---
LU-14950 osc: use list_splice_in

osc_io_submit is now one of the main consumers of time in
DIO submission.  This can be improved by switching from a
per-page list move to a batch splice of part of the list.

This is straightforward for DIO pages, because most of the
operations on them cannot fail.  This is trickier and gives
less benefit for buffered IO, so we don't bother.

This hasn't had full performance testing but local testing
suggests an improvement in best DIO performance of around
10% is possible(!).  The real number will likely be smaller
but still noticeable.  (Will update with that if possible.)
