--- Commit Message ---
LU-18687 build: refactor the top-level Makefile

Lustre uses automake far more than is strictly needed.
Limit the top-level autoMakefile to only creating dist
tarballs and handling install/deinstall. The remaining
of make targets are transfered to a series of plain
Makefiles. This completes our goal of building the
Lustre kernel modules without using automake.
