--- Commit Message ---
LU-14590 utils: duplicate param_dirs are collapsed

Instead of adding multiple copies of the same dir deep into
the tree, once the furthest match has been found if the path lens
match the new param_dir will instead be pointed to the existing one.

When adding a param to the tree, it will first look for an existing
match, if found the current param is freed and not printed to console.

This is not only good to reduce how much space is being used but it is
also crutial for dshbak to work.
