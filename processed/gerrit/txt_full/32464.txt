LU-11039 hsm: Wrong HSMTOOL_BASE name when kill copytools

When running HSM sanity testing locally, it wrongly gets the
HSMTOOL_BASE with "lhsmtool_posix", not "lt-hsmtool_posix".
Thus during the cleanup of HSM testing when call kill_copytools,
The command "killall -q $HSMTOOL_BASE" will kill nothing, and
execute the wrong cleanup action.
This patch parses the HSMTOOL_BASE from the output of helper
command of "lhsmtool_posix", fixes this problem.
