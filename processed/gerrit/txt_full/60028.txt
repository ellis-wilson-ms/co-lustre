--- Commit Message ---
LU-19157 nodemap: add banlist property

Add a new 'banlist' property to nodemaps. Its syntax is similar to the
'ranges' property, and can be managed from userspace thanks to new
commands:
lctl nodemap_banlist_add --name NODEMAP_NAME --range NID_RANGE
lctl nodemap_banlist_del --name NODEMAP_NAME --range NID_RANGE
NID ranges for the 'banlist' of a nodemap have to be included in the
'ranges' of the nodemap: only clients that belong to this nodemap can
be added to the ban list.
