> I just noticed another problem - ONLY_REPEAT doesn't work with this patch.

Yes, that is intentional until the patches that split up the subtests have all been landed. Otherwise, autotest will run all of the modified subtests in a loop, and many of them do not work properly in this case (they predate the review-subtest-change auto-loop), and I don't want to have to fix every subtest while copying them over to new files.  

It should still be *possible* to run with ONLY_REPEAT explicitly, but not ONLY_MINUTES, which is what review-subtest-change sessions are using.
