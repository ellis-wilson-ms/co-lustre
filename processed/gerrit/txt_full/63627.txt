LU-19539 osd-zfs: use osd_dmu_write() wrapper for xattrs

When setting xattrs use the osd_dmu_write() wrapper which
internally calls the _by_dnode() version of dmu_write().  This
both avoids an extra lookup and hold on the dnode.  Furthermore,
it removes the osd-zfs dependency on the dmu_write() symbol
which will add an flags argument in OpenZFS 2.5.
