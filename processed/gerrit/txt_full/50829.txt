--- Commit Message ---
LU-6142 libcfs: use list_first_entry() where appropriate.

This patch changes
   list_entry(foo.next, ...)
to
   list_first_entry(&foo, ...)

in cases where 'foo' is a list head, rather than a list member.
It also re-indents following lines where that is needed.
