  fs: provide accessors for ->i_state
Provide a compatible inode_state_read() for older kernels

Linux commit v6.18-rc1-10-gc826229c6a82f
  vfs: make vfs_create break delegations on parent directory
Provide a compatible vfs_create for older kernels

  interval_tree: Fix ITSTATIC usage for *_subtree_search()

  fs: rework I_NEW handling to operate without fences

  treewide: Remove in_irq()

  net: Add struct sockaddr_unsized for sockaddr of unknown length
Provide a mapping of sockaddr_unsized to sockaddr for older kernels

  get rid of kill_litter_super()
Use kill_anon_super() when kill_litter_super() not available
