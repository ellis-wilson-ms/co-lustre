LU-278 build: Only warn for tag/version mismatch

The configure process should NOT abort just because the most
recent tag is not of the form that upstream uses to tag Lustre.
Downstream developers may use their own tags, or just add
extensions to upsteam's version tags.

Chris: bz 22281 was created exactly for you to designate something like -1chaos in your version with the --downstream-release configure switch.

I agree however with the code that (now) warns of the mismatch, you will always see that warning if your tags have the -1chaos in them also.
