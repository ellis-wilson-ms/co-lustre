--- Commit Message ---
LU-10066 osp: error overflow handling for llog id

In osp_sync_correct_id(), it should be a normal case for the lrh_id
to be greater than committed_id but it was recognized as overflow,
this will cause correct_id to become a negative number.

This patch makes it correct.
