LU-19028 nodemap: do not squash trusted ids

When a root access is made with the admin property set, or when a
regular user access is made with the trusted property set, id should
not be squashed, even if it matches the defined squash_id on the
nodemap.

Add sanity-sec test_84 to verify trusted ids are not squashed.
