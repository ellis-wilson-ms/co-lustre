This is a great start, despite my numerous comments.  In general:

* descriptions that start with "This method..." should be revised.  For example, "This method changes the specified extended attribute." can just be "Change the specified extended attribute."
* Use a consistent style throughout the file.
* Replace terms like "should" and "is supposed to" with definite terms like "may optionally" and "must".
* Specify success and failure cases separately in \retval lines, and use correct grammar, i.e. "0 if the declaration succeeded" not "0 if succeed"
