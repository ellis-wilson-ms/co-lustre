--- Commit Message ---
LU-13380 clio: Explicitly copy "setattr" attribute to sub_io

For calls like falloc(), or any call which piggy backs
behind SETTER interface and depends upon these attributes.
It was noticed that the attributes was not explicitly
copied to sub_io under lov_io_sub_init(). This was resulting
in attributes under cl_io.ci_setattr coming out as 0. This
patch fixes the above bug by explicitly copying the
"ci_setattr" field to sub_io before calling cl_io_sub_init()
