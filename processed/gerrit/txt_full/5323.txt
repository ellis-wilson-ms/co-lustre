LU-2748 Allocate struct osd_thread_info with vmalloc

Need to allocate struct osd_thread_info in osd_key_init with
OBD_ALLOC_LARGE, since otherwise we get order 5 allocation which
is hard to fulfil.
This is mostly a bandaid before we get around to actually splitting
that allocation into better sized smaller pieces on as needed basis.
