LU-13814 clio: note if cdp is initialized

Once the cl_page allocation is removed for DIO, it becomes
possible (in a few FLR cases) to get a not-initialized lov
object freed before we get to cl_dio_pages_free.

We can recognize this case by marking whether or not we
really initialized the cdp at the lower layers, because
those not-initialized LOV objects don't have operations for
us to call.

This is required for the next patch, which removes cl_page
allocations for DIO.
