LU-17069 osc: check for invalid import before osc_lru_reserve

osc_lru_reserve() should not be called when OSC/import is disable in
configuration.

If the OSC is disables on the client at mount time, the LRU cache is
not initialized for the OSC.
This could lead to the following crash for a write IO:

BUG: unable to handle kernel NULL pointer dereference at (null)
