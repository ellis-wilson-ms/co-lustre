LU-18876 build: remove Lustre header autoMakefiles

The Lustre header autoMakefiles just explicitly list
out the name of each distributed header. We can detect
the headers automatically in the top-level autoMakefile.
So remove the explicit autoMakefiles in lustre/include/.
