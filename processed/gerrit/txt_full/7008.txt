LU-3390 iokit: Interop failure in mds-survey, strip_count=1

The information returned from "lctl device_list" changed between
b2_3 and b2_4 causing interop failures.

In b2_3, "lctl device_list" on the MDS returns lines containing
"... UP osc lscratch-OST0000-osc-MDT0000 ..." for each OST.
In b2_4, the OST information looks like
"... UP osp lscratch-OST0000-osc-MDT0000 ...".

A patch for LU-1303 (http://review.whamcloud.com/4087) fixes this
by modifying mds-survey to look for and parse lines with "osc" or
"osp".
