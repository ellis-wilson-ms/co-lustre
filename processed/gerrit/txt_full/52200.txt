--- Commit Message ---
LU-16355 osc: batch small writes based on small object count

This patch adds accounting for small write objects on OSC layer.
Once the accumulated small write objects with dirty pages exceeds
a threshold (a tunable "osc.*.batch_write_objs_thrsh"), it will
try to batch small writes into a batched bulk RPC and send to
OSTs.
