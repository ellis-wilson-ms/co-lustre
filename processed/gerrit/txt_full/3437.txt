LU-1158 general: remove obd_time typedef in lieu of __s64

As it became clear with the addition of separate nanosecond time
fields that the old obd_time-type fields were to be used exclusively
for storing time in seconds, the obd_time type was removed in lieu of
simply explicitly using signed 64-bit integers for these fields.
