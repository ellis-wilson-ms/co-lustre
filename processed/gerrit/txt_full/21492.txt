--- Commit Message ---
LU-6756 osd-zfs: not hold rwlock to do blocked operation

For osd_object_sa_dirty_rele(), the caller may holds the rwlock
obj::oo_attr_lock to do get another rw_semaphore and do blocked
xattr update. If it is blocked, then will tigger soft lockup.

So the caller should release obj::oo_attr_lock just after unlink
from the transaction delay EA update list osd_thandle::ot_sa_list.
