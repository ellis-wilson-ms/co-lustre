LU-7287 clio: osc_cache_shrink_scan() unsafe

In osc_cache_shrink_scan(), the stop_anchor could be removed from
the list, and results in a infinite scan loop.

This patch adds shrink osc device counter and makes
osc_cache_shrink_scan() stops when enough osc device has been
scanned.
