LU-2959 mgc: allow mgc_copy_llog to fail early in OST startup

If a lock is canceled before mgc_fs_setup has completed, it's possible
to call mgc_copy_llog with cli->cl_mgc_configs_dir not set, in this
case, fail gracefully instead of ASSERTing and dieing.
