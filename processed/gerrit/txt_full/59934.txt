LU-16738 utils: add mgsname mount option for custom hostname display

Add support for mgsname= mount option that allows specifying a custom
MGS hostname to display in /proc/mounts instead of the resolved NID
list.

Example usage:
mount -t lustre -o mgsname=my-mgs server@tcp:/lustre /mnt/lustre shows
"my-mgs@tcp:/lustre" in /proc/mounts instead of "1.2.3.4@tcp:/lustre".
