--- Commit Message ---
LU-20102 o2iblnd: do not call rtnl_lock() in RCU read section

Change-Id #63038 ("aebedbe750 LU-19707 lnet: fix RTNL assert in
LNet on v6.15") has introduced a regression allowing rtnl_lock()
from inside a RCU read-side critical section.

To avoid this wrong behaviour, now only a reference will be taken on
net device inside the RCU section, and all others actions/locks will
be done outside.

Fixes: aebedbe750 ("LU-19707 lnet: fix RTNL assert in LNet on v6.15")
