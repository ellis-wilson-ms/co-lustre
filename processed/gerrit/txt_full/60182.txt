--- Commit Message ---
LU-9 lod: Do not use qos alloc when stripe_count == OSTs

If an object is being stripped across all OSTs and the qos allocation
method is being used, it could potentially waste significant time
trying to hit OSTs with small weight.

Since each OST will be hit anyways it makes sense to just use
round-robin allocation.
