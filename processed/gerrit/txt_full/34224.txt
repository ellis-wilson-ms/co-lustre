LU-11953 tests: conf-sanity cleanup

Most conf-sanity tests assume cleanup has been run before
they start, but many tests also do not do this correctly on
error.

Rather than adding a stack_trap to almost every test, let's
put cleanup() in $CLEANUP so it's run after every test.
