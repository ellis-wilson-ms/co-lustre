--- Commit Message ---
LU-18813 osd-wbcfs: fix coverity warnings

osd_mksym() is not implemented yet, so remove the dead code.

The CDEBUG() in osd_index_dir_insert() can incorrectly
dereference a NULL dentry. Also, it accesses d_flags without
holding a lock. Move the CDEBUG() after out_dput and remove
the d_flags access (rather than do expensive locking).

CoverityID: 463351 ("Data race condition")
CoverityID: 463341 ("Structurally dead code")
CoverityID: 463344 ("Dereference after null check")
