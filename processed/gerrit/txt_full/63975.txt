--- Commit Message ---
LU-19865 lov: Bypass LU device free before disconnect

lov_cleanup() included by lov_device_free() should be called after
disconnect so avoid calling lov_device_free() before disconnect.
Try to fix the following dmesg error:

    # mount.lustre XXX.XXX.XXX.XXX@tcp:/test /mnt/test
    # umount /mnt/test
    # dmesg
     ...
     LustreError: 281843:0:(lov_obd.c:780:lov_cleanup())
      test-clilov-ff45ff4eca88c800: lov tgt 0 not cleaned!
      deathrow=0, lovrc=1

Fixes: 6a06834a3f2b ("LU-18162 lov: convert Logical Object Volume to LU device")
