Here is what happened during test_17m.

1. the MDT target is in recovery
2. the target received a MDS_CONNECT RPC that sets the export last xid to value A
3. the target received a LDLM_ENQUEUE RPC with an xid lower than export last xid value A
4. this triggers LBUG

How is it possible that the client sends the LDLM_ENQUEUE RPC (without RESEND and REPLAY flag) after the MDS_CONNECT RPC and that the MDS_CONNECT RPC was not aware of the LDLM_ENQUEUE RPC when it computed its last_xid ?

Need to check if the strategy implemented in patch http://review.whamcloud.com/#/c/15473/ better handle this situation.
