--- Commit Message ---
LU-19996 utils: Make l_getidentity produce proper errno

l_getidentity called by call_usermodehelper() always throws EBADF(9)
errno when the specified UID doesn't exist on mds. We found that
logging messages into invalid stderr triggers EBADF(9) which makes
people confused. In this case, l_getidentity should avoid using
invalid stderr and throw EIDRM(43) errno instead.

Also add a test to verify l_getidentity with a nonexistent user
on the MDT.
