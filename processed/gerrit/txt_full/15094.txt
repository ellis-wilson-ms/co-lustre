SGI-133 mdt: remove cdt_llog_lock mutex

During early HSM development, issues had been encountered
when using LLOG layer during concurrent hsm_actions usage.
To avoid this, cdt_llog_lock mutex defensive protection had
been introduced to serialize hsm_actions LLOG usages.
As LLOG layer has been reworked and is guaranteed to be
thread-safe, this protection use is no longer required.
This may help to have CDT's performance better scale when
handling thousands of requests.

Additional change has been to rewrite all hsm_actions record
update cases to switch from non-atomical "add new, then
remove old" current way to an in-place update of existing record.
