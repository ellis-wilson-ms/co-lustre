LU-16405 tests: a reproducer

a reproducer for the problem where create/mkdir can result in
a duplicated name.
the root cause is in ext4_find_dest_de() which supposes that
the caller ensures a correct non-racy check for duplicates -
ext4_find_dest_de() doesn't scan the whole block, only upto
a record with free space enough to save the new record.
