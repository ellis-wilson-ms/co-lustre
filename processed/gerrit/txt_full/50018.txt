--- Commit Message ---
LU-16538 mdt: reserve connect flag for open lock modes

Reserve OBD_CONNECT2_OPEN_LOCK_MODE connection flag for OPEN
ibit lock.
It is required to return various OPEN ibits lock modes for
different open flags to a client. And the lock mode for an OPEN
ibits lock is not mandatorily bound according to the specific
open flags.
