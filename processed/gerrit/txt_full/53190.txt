LU-15372 mdd: Add projid in Changelog

This patch adds projid support into Changelog.
Projid would be printed under section "projid="
When projid is not applicable it will show 0.

"projid=<projid>" will be followed by "attrs=ATTRS".
Where ATTRS are file attribute flags (e.g. LUSTRE_PROJINHERIT_FL,
but others attrs may be included. "attrs" are direct copy of
lu_attr->la_flags. These may include on disk inode flags and
Lustre specific (LMA) flags.

    # touch /mnt/lustre/f1

    Before change:
    01CREAT 0x0 t=[0x200000401:0x7:0x0] j=touch.0 ef=0x33 u=0:0 \
      nid=0@lo p=[0x200000401:0x5:0x0] f1

    After change:
    01CREAT 0x0 t=[0x200000402:0x4:0x0] j=touch.0 ef=0x33 u=0:0 \
      nid=0@lo projid=7200 attrs=20000000 p=[0x200000402:0x3:0x0] f1
