--- Commit Message ---
LU-1114 ptlrpc: synchronize ptlrpcd partner threads at start

The ptlrpcd threads can take some work from their partner threads,
see ptlrpcd_check() routine. However, the start of all the ptlrcpd
threads is not synchronized wich can lead to access the ptlrcpd_ctl
structure of a partner thread that has not been initialized.

This patch ensures partner thread control structure is accessed
only when it is completely initialized.
