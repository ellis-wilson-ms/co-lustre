>  Should we pin the pages?

No.  The simplest reason is that we weren't pinning the pages before so we shouldn't do it now.

I think that as a general rule, when the IO request will be synchronous, you use the nopin version.  When the IO could be async, the pin version is more likely to be useful - as the memory will be freed once the IO completes.
