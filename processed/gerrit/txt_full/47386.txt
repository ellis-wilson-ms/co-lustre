--- Commit Message ---
LU-13011 wbc: obtain attr from PCC once data on PCC

After a regular file has assimilated into PCC, all subsequent I/O
will direct into PCC with read-write PCC mode.
As the writes will only update the size of the corresponding PCC
copy, not the Lustre inode, thus it still needs to acquire the
attributes (especially for size attr) from PCC copy and update
the attributes of the corresponding Lustre inode during the stat()
call.

Add test case sanity-wbc test_33.
