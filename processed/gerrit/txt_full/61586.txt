LU-19102 llog: update llh_cat_idx on disk with llh_count

lh_cat_idx may be not written to disk, because order of operations
is changed by "LU-19102 llog: enospc catalog fix", and might be lost
after server restart.
Let's call llog_cat_set_first_idx() inside llog_cancel_rec(),
rigth after llh_count modification. And make an attempt to fix
llh_cat_idx during llog handle initialization.
