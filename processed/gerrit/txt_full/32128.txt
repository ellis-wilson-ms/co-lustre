LU-10938 wbc: Assimilate cache pages in MemFS into Lustre clio

When WBC EX lock is revoking on a client, it needs to flush the
cached directories and file data properly.
When flush a regular file under the protection of WBC EX lock,
it first creates the metadata on MDT and return the instantiated
layout. And then, the client needs to acquire all necessary extent
locks for file data and assimilate the cache pages in MemFS into
Lustre clio.
