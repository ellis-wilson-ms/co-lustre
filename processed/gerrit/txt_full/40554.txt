--- Commit Message ---
LU-14116 autoconf: check if DES3 enctype is supported

krb5 releases 1.18 and later completely remove support for
all DES3 enctypes (des3-cbc-raw, des3-hmac-sha1, des3-cbc-sha1-kd).

This patch adds HAVE_DES3_SUPPORT to check if DES3 enctype
is supported.

I would be happy to remove DES support from the client completely at this point, because DES encryption is no longer secure at all. I had wanted to do this previously, but was convinced several years ago to keep it for compatibility reasons. +1 because it is partly removing this support, but +2 if is removed entirely.
