LU-19241 o2iblnd: clean up conn on CM address change

In RoCEv2 bonding scenario, link failover causes
RDMA_CM_EVENT_ADDR_CHANGE event. If connection is not
cleaned up at this point, it gets stuck and prevents
clean module unload.

Disconnect on RDMA_CM_EVENT_ADDR_CHANGE.
