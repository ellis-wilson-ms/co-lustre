LU-16179 llite: race in ll_splice_alias()

ll_splice_alias() calls ll_find_alias() before calling d_add(), if
there are concurrent lookups, both may not find alias dentry, and then
add dentry, therefore there are more than one valid dentries for the
same file, which will cause deadlock in rename().

To avoid this race, use lli_rwlock to serialize ll_splice_alias().

Signed-off-by: Lai Siyao <lai.siyao@whamcloud.com>
