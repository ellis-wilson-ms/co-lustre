--- Commit Message ---
LU-9066 ldlm: NOT evict client when target stopping

The inflight LDLM AST RPC will get failure when the current target
is stopping for failover or pure umount. Originally, the AST error
handler will call ldlm_failed_ast() to evict related lock holder.
Such process is improper under the race case, it will cause client
error after the service re-mounted.

This patch checks the obd device status, if it is in stopping, then
NOT evict the client for the LDLM AST RPC error.
