LU-14055 obdclass: move obd_debugfs_vars to end obd_device

Move the obd_debugfs_vars field to the end of struct obd_device.
The addition of this field causes a significant performance loss
on the client, likely due to alignment or cacheline contention
issues of some field between its current position and the end of
the struct.  Moving it to the end should avoid this problem.
