LU-12268 osd-ldiskfs: zero buffer in iam_new_node

osd_ldiskfs_append does not guarantee a newly allocated
block is zeroed. Explicitly reset the buffer to zero
to avoid corrupting the IAM tree and writing garbage data
back to disk.
