LU-10391 ptlrpc: pass lnet_nid for self to ptl_send_buf()

The 'self' arg to ptl_send_buf() is now a pointer to a
'struct lnet_nid', and can be NULL meaning "ANY NID".

LNetPut() already accepts NULL as the self pointer.
