LU-10938 wbc: Basic symlink support

This compiles and works, but not ideal, due to some
hacks around rpc formats on the server and we don't want
to return locks for symlinks after creates and such.

There's unknown error coming from lmv for who knows why,
when we do async symlink create:
"(lmv_obd.c:275:lmv_init_ea_size())
lustre-clilmv-ffff8800cd4ee800: NULL export for 0"

Down not appear to be breaking anything in a visible way,
though.

Test-Parameters: trivial
