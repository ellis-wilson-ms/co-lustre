LU-12669 llite: constrain EC recovery read to component range

During EC recovery (CIT_EC_RD), vvp_io_ec_rd_end() calls
generic_file_read_iter() to deliver recovered data to
userspace.  Without any constraint, this read can extend
into pages belonging to subsequent PFL components that
have not been processed by the current cl_io iteration.
Those pages have no DLM locks, causing LBUG in
osc_req_attr_set().

Fix by truncating the iov_iter to the current component's
range (ec_inner.crw_pos + ec_inner.crw_bytes), capped at
i_size.  The sum ec_inner.crw_pos + ec_inner.crw_bytes is
invariant across the RG loop in lov_io_ec_rd_start(),
which advances crw_pos and decrements crw_bytes equally.

Also refresh lcc_end_index before generic_file_read_iter()
so that ll_readpage() rejects pages beyond the constrained
component range.

Resolves LBUG in tests 42d and 47a through
47d where reads extended into the next PFL
component which had no DLM locks.
