LU-12021 mdt: make useless error message a debug one

When there are unknown flags in mdt_attr_valid_xlate(),
there are simply ignored but an error message is printed.
As they are ignored and nothing is wrong most of time, avoid
filling the kernel log with these useless warnings.
Replace CERROR with a CDEBUG.
