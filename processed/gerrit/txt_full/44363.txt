LU-14093 gss: gcc10 fixes for GSS

GSS:

gss_util.h:37: multiple definition of `this_realm';
gssd.h:73: multiple definition of `clnt_list';
svcgssd.h:38: multiple definition of `krb_enabled';

Properly scope these variables.
