LU-9121 lnet: Preferred gateway selection 2

Refine the selection of the gateway to take into consideration
the preferred list assigned by the src->rte UDSP which adds
gateway nids on the preferred list for a local ni.
If there are multiple preferred gateways defined, then given
the same route priority, gateway from a local net preferred
list is selected. Otherwise usual selection criteria are
applied.
