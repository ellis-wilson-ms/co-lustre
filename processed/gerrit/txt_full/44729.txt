LU-14942 lnet: Update last alive for message source

In lnet_parse() we update the lpni_last_alive field for the
"from_nid". In a routed network, the from_nid is the NID of the
router that forwarded the message to the recipient. In this case,
we need to update the lpni_last_alive field for the message
originator which is the "src_nid". Otherwise, that last alive
timestamp is never updated and the remote peer NIs will not be
eligible for recovery pings.
