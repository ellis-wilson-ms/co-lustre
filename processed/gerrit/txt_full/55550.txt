--- Commit Message ---
LU-17982 ldlm: lfs migrate changing stripe locking

Avoid deadlock race when modifying stripe on a file:
  lfs migrate -o <stripe_pattern> <file>

When multiple clients attempt to modify the stripe on
a file an LDLM deadlock on competing blocking ast calls
result in both clients locking up in an uninterruptible
state.

This avoids a deadlock however clients are blocked for
~300 seconds waiting for the LDLM lock to timeout.
