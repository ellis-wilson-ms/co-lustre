LU-6142 lustre: use list_first_entry() in lustre subdirectory.

Convert
  list_entry(foo->next .....)
to
  list_first_entry(foo, ....)

in 'lustre'

In several cases the call is combined with
a list_empty() test and list_first_entry_or_null() is used
