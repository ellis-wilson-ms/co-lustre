--- Commit Message ---
LU-2789 mdt: take lov_mutex in mdt_attr_set to avoid race

There is a race condition between setstripe and chown.  Conditionally
taking lov_mutex before calling mo_attr_set avoids this race
condition.
The lock condition is having the LA_UID or LA_GID flags set, which is
always true when doing a chown.

Also corrected two instances of an incorrect comment
(unpack_setattr should be mdt_setattr_unpack).
