--- Commit Message ---
LU-11931 lnd: relax throttling limit

On large system it is better to allow head room for outstanding
transmits beyond the negotiated queue depth. It appears that this
restriction causes RDMA timeout, since tx stay on the tx_queue
for a long period of time.

Previously, there was concurrent_sends module parameter which
could be set to a maximum of queue_depth * 2. With the removal
of concurrent_sends, simply have a ceiling of queue_depth * 2
concurrent sends.
