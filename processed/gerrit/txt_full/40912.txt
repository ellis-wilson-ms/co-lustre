LU-14195 ldiskfs: update osd-ldiskfs for Linux 5.10

Linux 5.10 removes EXT4_GET_BLOCKS_KEEP_SIZE and
LDISKFS_INODE_EOFBLOCKS.

LDISKFS_INODE_EOFBLOCKS isn't a #define so we cannot test is directly,
but we can test the related LDISKFS_EOFBLOCKS_FL
