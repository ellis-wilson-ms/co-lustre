--- Commit Message ---
LU-19921 nodemap: expose fileset operations

Make nodemap_fileset_add/del/clear/modify non-static so they can be
called without going through cfg_nodemap_fileset_cmd(). The existing
implementations are renamed with a "_locked" suffix, making it
explicit that the caller must hold active_config_lock, and new
name-based wrappers handle the lookup, locking and refcount on their
behalf.

In this context, the same lookup_locked + active_config_lock + putref
pattern was duplicated in several other places. Those were refactored
to use "nodemap_lookup_and_lock()" and "nodemap_unlock_and_put()"
instead for cleanup.
