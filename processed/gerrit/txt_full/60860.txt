LU-19285 utils: Move yaml_parser_delete to yaml_parser_cleanup

This is a followup patch after commit ca6fdf73528a0 (LU-19234)
This patch moves direct calls from yaml_parser_delete() to
yaml_parser_cleanup() and direct calls from yaml_emitter_delete()
to yaml_emitter_cleanup() wherever it is required.

This patch also removes warnings added under spelling.txt
which was added under patch commit ca6fdf73528a0. The reason
being, not all calls must call *_cleanup(). So, direct call
to yaml_emitter_delete or yaml_parser_delect is still valid.

Fixes: ca6fdf73528a ("U-19234 lst: Fix leak under emitter/parser delete (1)")
