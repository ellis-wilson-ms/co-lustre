LU-12668 tests: add EC stress and admin tests

Add tests 73a-75f exercising EC stress and admin
operations in degraded mode:

- test_73a-e: advanced I/O patterns -- fallocate,
  truncate, append, and mixed operations on EC
  files with deactivated OSTs
- test_74a-c: device failure during reads -- spam
  reads racing with OST deactivation, readahead
  under failure, and rapid failure/recovery cycling
- test_75a-f: degraded admin and stress -- stat,
  getstripe, 50-iteration stress loops, lfs mirror
  verify with SEEK_DATA validation, directory-
  inherited layout recovery, and stripe rotation
  across multiple files
