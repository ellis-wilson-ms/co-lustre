--- Commit Message ---
LU-19780 layout: restore locking

cl_prune_object might hit infinity loop without proper
locking.
Several processes can block each other while trying to apply new
layout. One of them starts object prune and thus increases
lo_active_ios the second is forced to wait. After prune is finished
they exchange.
