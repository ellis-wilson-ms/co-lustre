--- Commit Message ---
LU-15069 llite: remove cra_contention

cra_contention attempts to add a heuristic to make readahead
less aggressive when there is lock contention, but all it
does is prevent readahead expanding when there *might* be
lock contention.

The normal readahead lock boundary checking functions just
fine to handle lock contention - it will not do readahead
if there is no lock.  The rest is just an unnecessary and
confusing extra.
