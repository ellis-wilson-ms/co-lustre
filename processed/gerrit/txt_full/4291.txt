--- Commit Message ---
LU-2177 ptlrpc: flock/posix lock cause LBUGs because of a race

The below races may happen when evicting clients

- LBUG in ldlm_flock_decref_internal_nolock()
1) A therad calls ldlm_flock_completion_ast()
2) Another thread is dealing with client eviction and sets some flags
   to the same flock as (2), and calls ldlm_flock_completion_ast()

Because of the flags which've been set at (2), the both completion
ast call ldlm_lock_decref_internal() but it's more than necessary

----------------------------------------------------------------------
- LBUG in ldlm_lock_destroy()
1) a flock has been granted
2) the client which (1)'s flock belongs to is evicted and the flock
   gets LDLM_FL_FAILED flag
3) (1)'s thread goes ahead and calls ldlm_process_flock_lock()

Because of (2), an error is detected and ldlm_flock_destroy() is
called in ldlm_process_flock_lock(). But LDLM_FL_FAILED has already
been set, So ldlm_flock_destroy() never call ldlm_lock_decref_internal
_nolock() thought it's needed
