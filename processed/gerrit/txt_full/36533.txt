LU-12756 lnet: Drop addref in lnet_get_peer_ni_locked

lnet_get_peer_ni_locked is only called by lnet_find_peer_ni_locked.
Most callers of lnet_find_peer_ni_locked() do not need the ref on
the peer_ni object returned by this function. Remove the addref
from lnet_get_peer_ni_locked, and adjust callers of
lnet_find_peer_ni_locked accordingly.
