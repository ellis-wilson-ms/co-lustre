--- Commit Message ---
LU-10486 osp: fix request leak on error in osp_sync

osp_sync_new_unlink_job() and osp_sync_new_unlink64_job() do not
call ptlrpc_req_put() when returning early on error after
osp_sync_new_job() has allocated the request. This leaks the
ptlrpc request.

Add ptlrpc_req_put(req) to three error paths, matching the
pattern used in osp_sync_new_err_job().
