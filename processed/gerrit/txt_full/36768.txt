LU-12975 lmv: ltd_tgts_size used instead of lmv_mdt_count

reistered MDTs number causing following errors when
"lfs quota" is used with -v:

...
quotactl mdt60 failed.
quotactl mdt61 failed.
quotactl mdt62 failed.
quotactl mdt63 failed.

Issue was introduced in "LU-12624 obdclass: lu_tgt_descs cleanup".
