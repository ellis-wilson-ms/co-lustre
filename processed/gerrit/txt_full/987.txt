--- Commit Message ---
LU-414 initialized the unused area in MDT's CATALOGS

during connecting OSTs in MDT, it will call mds_lov_update_desc/obd_llog_init
to initialize the llog for this OST, and if an OST with big index is connected
before these with small index, an area in MDT's CATALOGS file will be left to
be uninitialized, which will cause various problems.
