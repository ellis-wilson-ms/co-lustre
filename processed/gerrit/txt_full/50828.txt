--- Commit Message ---
LU-6142 lustre: use list_first/last_entry() for list heads

This patch changes
    list_entry(foo.next, ...)
to
    list_first_entry(&foo, ...)
and
    list_entry(foo.prev, ...)
to
    list_last_entry(&foo, ...)

in cases where 'foo' is a list head - not a list member.
