--- Commit Message ---
LU-4053 clio: don't cache inode of unlinked files

If file is known to be destroyed, the inode on the client side
shouldn't be cached on the client side. MDT uses OBD_MD_FLREMOVED
flag in various RPC reply to tell client this information.
