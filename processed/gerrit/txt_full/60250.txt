LU-18135 scripts: bad iproute rt_tables path

ksocklnd-config fails in some distros where there is no config in
/etc/iproute2, but in /usr/share/iproute2.
To fix that and keep the distro files untouched, we copy rt_tables
into /etc/iproute2 and modify it.
