--- Commit Message ---
LU-11088 ptlrpc: handle invalid opcodes in ll_opcode2str()

When an unknown opcode is received from the wire, opcode_offset()
returns (u32)-1, and then one of the assertions in ll_opcode2str()
fails. This can happen in at least two places where this function
is called with opcodes received from the network.

This patch modifies ll_opcode2str() to handle invalid opcodes
gracefully by returning a descriptive string like "unknown-opcode-NNN"
instead of asserting. This prevents kernel panics when invalid
opcodes are received.
