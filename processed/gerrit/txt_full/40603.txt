--- Commit Message ---
LU-13500 ldlm: Do not LRU-cancel "expensive" locks for in bl-ast

When we got a blocking ast that results in a lock cancel, we definitely
should avoid canceling lru locks that results in extra RPCs like
PW locks with dirty data or OPEN locks that must send a CLOSE RPC
first. Those RPCs might take a long time and what's worse, they might
end up not being sent out at all because of lack of request slots.
