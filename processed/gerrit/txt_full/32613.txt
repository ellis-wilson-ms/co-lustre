Hello guys,
Thanks for the ping ...

I see one thing that is broken : the famous "CONFIG_LDISKFS_FS_ENCRYPTION".
I have tried to take time to understand what is going on with latest kernel.
Since 4.11, CONFIG_EXT4_FS_ENCRYPTION exists in the kernel, it means when you apply ldiskfs patches, you enable ldiskfs fs encryption but you don't verify that the "module" is built.
In the case where you kernel has no "ext4 fs encryption enabled" you will have unknow symbols on lustre modules loading.
I have found a way to check that with the macro LB_CHECK_CONFIG_IM but i have no time yet to upload these modifications (i hope next week plus Andreas remarks). Can you add this check either in lustre-build-ldiskfs.m4 or in lustre_core.m4 ? (i have no preference)

Good job !
