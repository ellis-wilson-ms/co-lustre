LU-19143 tcu: test Trash Can with various UID/GID/PROJID setting

Add a test case sanityn/test_117v for various Trash Can UID/GID/
PROJID setting: "default"/"-2", "original"/"-1" and other value
(i.e. id=10):
	lctl set_param mdd.*.trash_can_uid=$id
	lctl set_param mdd.*.trash_can_gid=$id
	lctl set_param mdd.*.trash_can_projid=$id

Add a test case sanityn/test_117w to verify that under the
original UID/GID/PROJID setting, the UID/GID/PROJID of the files
or directories moving into Trash Can does not change and keep its
original value.

Test-Parameters: trivial
