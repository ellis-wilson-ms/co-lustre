**The Fix:**
Added a comma after the format string:
```c
CWARN("%s: unexpected notification from %s %s: %d\n",
      host->obd_name, watched->obd_type->typ_name,
```

**Rationale:**
The `CWARN` macro (and similar logging macros in Lustre) follows the `printf`-style format, where the format string must be followed by a comma before the variadic arguments. The format string expects 4 arguments (`%s`, `%s`, `%s`, `%d`), and these are provided as: `host->obd_name`, `watched->obd_type->typ_name`, `watched->obd_name`, and `result`.
