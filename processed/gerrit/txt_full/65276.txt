LU-10482 tests: renumber sanity/56ea to avoid conflict

There are two sanity test_56ca() tests, which exposed issues with
the naming schema in this set of subtests - that some test_56XX
subtests are in the "abc" sequence of tests, and others are in
the "post-xyz" sequence of tests and using "aa, ab, ca, ...".

Rename test_56ea*() and test_56Ea*() tests to avoid future collisions.
