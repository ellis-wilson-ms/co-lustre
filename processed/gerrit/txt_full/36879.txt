LU-12955 lnet: Fix dest NID if sending from non-multi-rail

If the originator of a message is not multi-rail capable then we
need to fix the destination NID of the message in order to ensure
that we receive a reply from a known NID. Otherwise, it is possible
that messages being forwarded by MR capable routers to MR capable
peers will have a new destination NID (lnet_peer_ni) chosen by the
router. Certain RPC replies will then be sent over this interface
which the NMR originator may not know about. This can result in
dropped messages.
