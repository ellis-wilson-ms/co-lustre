LU-19992 connect: reserve OBD_CONNECT2_FLR_EC_WR flag

Reserve OBD_CONNECT2_FLR_EC_WR at 0x80000000000ULL
for immediate write erasure coding support.

This follows OBD_CONNECT2_FLR_EC (read-only EC)
and OBD_CONNECT2_FLR_IMMED_MIRROR (immediate write
mirroring). No functional changes -- flag reservation
only. Generated with Claude Code + Tools.
