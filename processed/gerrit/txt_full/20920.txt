--- Commit Message ---
LU-7842 nfs: don't drop cap for getattr too

This is a follow up of LU-6528: ll_iget_for_nfs() ->
search_inode_for_lustre() -> md_getattr() -> mdt_getattr() is a
possible call trace in NFS export too, so mdt_getattr() should
not drop capability just like mdt_getattr_name() and
mdt_intent_getattr().

Also rename mdt_init_ucred_intent_getattr() to
mdt_init_ucred_getattr() to be more accurate.
