LU-15595 lnet: Do not calculate route aliveness on send

Route aliveness is now updated when the health or status of a router's
peer NI is modified. As such, we do not need to calculate route
aliveness in lnet_is_route_alive() (called on the send path). Instead,
we simply check the current value of lnet_route::lr_alive.

sanity-lnet test 227 and 228 are refactored since now the behavior
should be the same between the DD enabled and disabled cases.
