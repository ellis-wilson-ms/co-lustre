--- Message (Andreas Dilger, 2017-03-24 20:50:17.000000000) ---
Patch Set 1: Code-Review-1

Besides the small reduction of code, is there any benefit to this change?

There may be block mapped files existing on older filesystems for some reason, and MDTs are usually formatted without extents (because that is bad for directory allocation and random IOPS performance of things like llog files that are allocated over time).  Also, with DoM it means the MDT would have bulk IO to block-mapped files.
