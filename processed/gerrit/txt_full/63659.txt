--- Commit Message ---
LU-16245 osd-ldiskfs: ensure ordering of dr_elapsed_valid

After waiting for iobuf->dr_numreqs to become zero, we must ensure
that the read of iobuf->dr_elapsed_valid in osd_fini_iobuf() sees
the correct and up-to-date value. Without a read memory barrier, the
CPU or compiler may reorder these reads, leading to incorrect
behavior. This patch inserts smp_rmb() after the wait_event to
prevent such reordering and ensure proper memory visibility.
