--- Commit Message ---
LU-8339 lnet: lock improvement for ko2iblnd

kiblnd_check_sends() takes conn::ibc_lock at the beginning
and releases this lock at the end. This is inefficient because
most use-cases need to explicitly release ibc_lock before caling
this function.

This patch changes it to kiblnd_check_sends_locked() and avoids
unnecessary lock dances.
