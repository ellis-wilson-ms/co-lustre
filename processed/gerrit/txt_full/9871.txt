LU-5319 proto: multi-slot last_rcvd

THIS IS A PROTO TO PLAY WITH IDEA.

basically the idea is:
 - to use additional log to store transno/xid/vbr/rc records
   (struct lsd_reply_data)
 - in-core bitmap is used to find free slots in this additional log
 - reint/enqueue are tagged: tag to be re-used once reply is received
 - rep-ack is used to free the slots earlier

sanity-benchmark gives the following:
	lustre-MDT0000: 231776 acked, 131239 reused
231776 slots were released by REP-ACK, while 131239 via tag.

a silly local benchmark creating 10K special files in 12 directories
with 12 threads (but limited by 6 slots on the server):
before: 21s
after:  13s

TODO:
 - release slot by lowest unreplied XID
 - error handlign
 - optimizations to bitmap scanning
 - 2way compatibility?
