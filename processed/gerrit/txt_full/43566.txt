--- Commit Message ---
LU-14648 osd: osd-ldiskfs should serialize destroy vs. others

MDD takes order of declaration, start transaction and then take locks,
so we need to check object's existence in OSD declaration phase as
other threads could be destroying the objects.

So we'd serialize destroy vs. others even in declaation phase.
