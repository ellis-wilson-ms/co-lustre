LU-10927 osc: do not spend too much time in osc_lru_reclaim

When a IO calls osc_lru_reclaim(), it needs the LRU slots immediately.
So if the thread is asking for less than max_pages_per_rpc slots,
do not waste time to shrink max_pages_per_rpc slots. The overhead
will become much more significant when memory gets bigger.
