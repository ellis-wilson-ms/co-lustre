I think checkpatch is wrong here. The alternative is to write:

case MY_VERY_COOL_ENUM:
   return "MY_VERY_COOL_ENUM";

which is worse than the macro, since the two can be out-of-sync if someone does a typo for the string. With the macro, the compiler enforces that the correct string gets output.
