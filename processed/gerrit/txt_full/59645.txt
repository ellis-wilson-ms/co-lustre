LU-0000 llite: remove LDLM_FL_NO_LRU from ACTIVE_WRITER locks

Remove the LDLM_FL_NO_LRU flag from ACTIVE_WRITER lock acquisition
to allow proper lock cancellation and LRU management.

Previously, ACTIVE_WRITER locks were created with LDLM_FL_NO_LRU
flag, which prevented them from being added to the LRU list and
cancelled normally. This caused the server-side active writer
count to remain elevated instead of being decremented when locks
were released.

The LDLM_FL_NO_LRU flag is generally undesired for ACTIVE_WRITER
locks as it prevents normal lock lifecycle management. With this
change, ACTIVE_WRITER locks can now be properly cancelled through
the normal LRU mechanism and ldlm_lock_decref_and_cancel() calls.

This fix works in conjunction with the previous server-side fix
in mdt_intent_lock_replace() that ensures proper l_conn_export
setup for cancellation RPCs.
