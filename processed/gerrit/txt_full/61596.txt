LU-19437 lnet: re-create missed Lustre peers

LNet peers added by Lustre via LNetAddPeer() can be
deleted by LNet discovery if local and remote peers have
different 'discovery' or MR settings. Such peer is
deleted by LNet and it is expected to be re-created as
simple non-MR peer when Lustre try to access it for
network transfer. However, after commit ee977b9039 Lustre
doesn't try to use such peer.

Patch restores expected behavior and re-creates peer
during call to LNetPeerDiscovered() if peer is missing

Fixes: ee977b9039 ("LU-17906 pltrpc: don't use non-uptodate peer at connect")
