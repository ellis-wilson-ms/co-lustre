LU-19469 osc: detect holes in read RPCs

Extend OST_READ RPC to detect holes and populate the hole extent
cache on the client.
And then the subseqeent read can zero the buffere direclty when
hit the cached hole and avoid unnecessary RPCs.

A new feild @o_holemap_size is added into the wire data structure
obdo to inform the server that the size of hole_map the client
prepared.

Add sanity/test_856g.
