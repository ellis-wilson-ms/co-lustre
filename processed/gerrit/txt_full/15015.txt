--- Commit Message ---
LU-6194 tests: Skip sanity 162c for MDS older than 2.6.92

Sanity test 162c tests that a path can have more than 100
subdirectories. The limit of only 100 subdirectories in a
path was removed by a patch for LU-4239 and landed in Lustre
master tag 2.6.92.

Skip sanity test 162c for MDSs with version less than 2.6.92.

Test-Parameters: alwaysuploadlogs envdefinitions=ONLY=162c \ 
clientdistro=el6.6 mdsdistro=el6.6 ossdistro=el6.6 clientarch=x86_64 \
mdsarch=x86_64 ossarch=x86_64 clientjob=lustre-b2_6 mdsjob=lustre-b2_6 \
ossjob=lustre-b2_6 clientbuildno=19 mdsbuildno=19 ossbuildno=19 \
testlist=sanity
