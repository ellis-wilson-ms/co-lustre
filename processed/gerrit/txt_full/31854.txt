--- Commit Message ---
LU-10873 lustre: Add support for used inode/space on MDT/OST proc

We have a monitoring system which can get the information from
the /proc entries of Lustre. However, the Lustre doesn't export the
used inode/space on MDT/OST, this patch adds used inode/space info
in ofd/lod layer:

/proc/fs/lustre/osd-*/testfs-OST0000/filesused
/proc/fs/lustre/lod/testfs-MDT0000-mdtlov/fileused
/proc/fs/lustre/osd-*/testfs-OST0000/kbytesused
/proc/fs/lustre/lod/testfs-MDT0000-mdtlov/kbytesused

I know it is a question whether it is really necessary to add used capacity/inode, but according to the requirement of our customers, it is.

The used capacity/inode can be calculated indeed from total - free.
