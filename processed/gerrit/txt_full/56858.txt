LU-17885 osc: convert cdp_page_count to int

As wild as it sounds, 2% of CPU time in DIO is spent in
handling a 32 bit sign extension for this value when used
in the loop check in osc_transfer_page_init.

There is no need for more than 32 bits of pages.
