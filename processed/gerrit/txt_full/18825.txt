LU-12531 osc: use enough LNET MDs for changing BRWs

An reconnection from OST could cause BRW size of the import
changing from a big number to a smaller one. Exsiting extents
with big max pages per rpc is not able to fit into current BRW
size. This patch always allocates enough LNET MDs even this
happens.
