LU-14950 llite: Remove page state for DIO

The page owner and page state tracking are unnecessary for
DIO pages, which have fairly simple lifecycles.  Also, it's
becoming expensive - Taking up >10% of CPU time in the
current DIO path.

This also moves us towards possibly removing the cl_page
abstraction for DIO.

This is a *VERY UGLY* first pass at this to see what blows
up, and it is badly in need of cleanup.

So: Let's see what blows up...
