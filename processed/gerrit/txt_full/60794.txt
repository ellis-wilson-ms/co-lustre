LU-19268 lmv: Add qos_exclude_patterns

Introduce qos_exclude_patterns to complement
qos_exclude_prefixes. qos_exclude_patterns should
allow shell-style pattern matching (e.g., "*.[ch]"),
providing greater flexibility than prefix-only
matching.
