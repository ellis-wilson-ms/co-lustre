--- Commit Message ---
LU-4271 mdd: do not start journal handle before quota ops

Before this patch, dead-lock situations have been encountered in
JBD2 layer, due to mdd_attr_set() routine calling heavy quota
processing routines, inside a journal handle reservation.
This bug only concerns mdd_attr_set() routine, and only in
b2_1 version.
