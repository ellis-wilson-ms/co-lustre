--- Commit Message ---
LU-3611 quota: Fix order of locking and allocating memory.

mgs_pool_cmd() locks fsdb_mutex and then allocates mti. It will
leave fsdb_mutex locked if it fails to allocate memory.

This patch fixes the problem.
