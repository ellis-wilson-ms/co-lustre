LU-3522 osd-zfs: Report ZFS blocksize as 4K

This will cause ofd to do rational grants instead of trying to
grant wildly too much space for each write.  Without this change
ofd assumed that all grants would take up 32x the amount of space
becuase zfs reported a block size of 128KB.

--- Message (Andreas Dilger, 2013-08-22 21:20:35.000000000) ---
Patch Set 1: I would prefer that you didn't submit this

To further clarify - the whole reason that there is grant inflation with ZFS is because old (and sadly current) clients will only consume grant in PAGE_SIZE chunks (i.e. typically 4kB units).

That means (in the worst case) if some client is writing sparse 4kB-chunk files that each 4kB written by the client might consume a full 128kB block of space on the OST.  Without the server-side grant inflation this could lead to the OST incorrectly running out of space before the client runs out of grant.
