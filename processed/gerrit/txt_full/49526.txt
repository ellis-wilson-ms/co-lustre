LU-16159 target: race in update log cancel

Update log cancel should wait for running transactions to commit to
avoid race in log file access, the former may remove empty logs, while
the latter may write them.

The running transactions are tracked by distribute_txn_commit_thread,
wait for all transactions to commit, but do not update batchid and
cancel records in update logs (because both are time consuming, and
update log canceling makes them not necessary), and then it's safe to
cancel update logs in recovery.

Fixes: b054fcd785 ("LU-16159 lod: cancel update llogs upon recovery abort")
