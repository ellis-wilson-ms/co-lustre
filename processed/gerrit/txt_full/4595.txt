LU-2306 obdclass: solve an unexpected behaviors of class_decref

When LOV obd_device on a client node is registered with target OSCs

- marge the two if-statements in class_decref() in order for
  class_decref() not to call obd_precleanup and unlink the same
  self export multiple times
- unlink a self export and wait for releasing it in class_cleanup()
  to let class_detach() to put the last obd_refcount and to detach
  obd_device from the obd_devs list
- marge OBD_CLEANUP_SELF_EXP into OBD_CLEANUP_EXPORTS because of
  the change in class_decref() in this patch
