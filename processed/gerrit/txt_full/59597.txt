LU-13643 flr: Add immediate mirror flag

Adding a layout flag to mark immediate mirrors.  This
requires fixing some of the layout flag handling, which
mostly masks off everything that isn't an flr flag.

The PCC_RDONLY flag snuck in by being inside the FLR
mask, but we have a full 16 bits for flags and should
use it.

Also reserve INCOMPRESSIBLE flag on master, so we do
not conflict.
