--- Commit Message ---
LU-6142 libcfs: discard PO2_ROUNDUP_TYPED, LOWEST_BIT_SET

LOWEST_BIT_SET() is never used.

PO2_ROUNDUP_TYPED() has the same function as 'round_up()'.

osd_roundup2blocksz() can be further simplified using
DIV_ROUND_UP_ULL().
