LU-3936 ldlm: prevent overflow when counting granded resources

When the total amount of resources we may release counted the
total value can overflow integer.

    int total = 0, cached = 0;

    for (nr_ns = ldlm_namespace_nr_read(client);
         nr_ns > 0; nr_ns--)
    {
        [...]
        total += ldlm_pool_shrink(&ns->ns_pool, 0, gfp_mask);
        [...]
        cached += ldlm_pool_granted(&ns->ns_pool);
        [...]
    }
