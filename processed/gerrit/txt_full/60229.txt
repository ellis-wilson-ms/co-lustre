LU-18756 tests: fix DoM ops for sanity-sec 75a interop

Sanity-sec 75a runs fallocate on a DoM file. In case the MDS uses an
older version where fallocate on DoM files isn't supported, the test
should skip the fallocate test. This patch implements this.
