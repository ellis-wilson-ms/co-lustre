INTL-15 ldiskfs: debug patch

lock the buffer containing the group descriptor while it is locked
by ldiskfs_lock_group to verify there is no IO during modifying the
content of the group descriptor.

this debug patch will affect the performance for it prevent others
from accessing the group descriptors contained in the same buffer.
