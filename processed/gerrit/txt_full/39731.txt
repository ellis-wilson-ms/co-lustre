LU-13923 lnet: Add missing lnet_peer_ni_decref call

The code added in LU-13606 was missing a refcount decrement.
The call to lnet_nid2peerni_locked is incrementing a refcount
on a peer_ni object which is never decremented. This results in LNet
being unable to cleanup properly on "unconfigure" or "net del"
operation, causing lnetctl to hang.

Fixes: b0e8ab1 ("LU-13606 lnet: Allow router to forward to healthier NID")
