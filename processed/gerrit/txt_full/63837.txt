LU-11030 fld: initialize index and req variables

Initialize `index` and `req` in fld_update_from_controller()
to prevent use of uninitialized variables. The `req` pointer
could be dereferenced in the cleanup path if fld_client_rpc()
returns early without setting *reqp.
