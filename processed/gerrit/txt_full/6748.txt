LU-3496 ost: various optimizations and cleanup for OSS

This patch does the following:
1) removes unnecessary multiplication in filter_do_bio
2) implements log2 with bitops in lprocfs_oh_tally_log2
3) converts devlock into rwlock for *rdonly*
4) cleans up f_e_m_ext_inode_pages
5) optimize lprocfs counters in filter_preprw_read
6) remove meaningless assert for dr_pages filter_do_bio
