LU-8968 osp: protect opd_pre_thread from early release

this approach doesn't look "normal", I'm still looking for
a more elegant solution in the kernel.

this is a short schema for the race:
th1, umount                th2,osp_pcrecreate_thread()
t_flags=SVC_STOPPING
wake_up()
<-- e.g. interrupt -->     t_flags=SVC_STOPPED
                           <-- e.g. interuupt -->
wait_event()
kfree(opd_pre_thread)
                           wake_up(opd_pre_thread.t_ctl_waitq)
			    --> use-after-free

the patch puts t_flags=SVC_STOPPED and wake_up() under
the spinlock and adds a barrier in form of lock+unlock
after wait_event(). this way thread1 is blocked after
wait_event() (and can't release the memory) until
wake_up() in thread2 is completed.
