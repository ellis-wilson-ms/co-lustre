> Can you explain what %private at the end means?  Just at the end of
 > the commit title or ?

This is explained in the Gerrit documentation:
https://review.whamcloud.com/Documentation/user-upload.html#_git_push

  Private Changes

  To push a private change or to turn a change private on push
  the private option can be specified:

  git push ssh://john.doe@git.example.com:29418/kernel/common HEAD:refs/for/master%private

  Omitting the private option when pushing updates to a private
  change doesn’t make change non-private again. To remove the
  private flag from a change on push, explicitly specify the
  remove-private option:

  git push ssh://john.doe@git.example.com:29418/kernel/common HEAD:refs/for/master%remove-private
