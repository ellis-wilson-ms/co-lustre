--- Commit Message ---
LU-4800 utils: do explicit modprobe when module autoload fails

Failure of the usual kernel module autoload mechanism has been
observed multiple times.  Frequently seen at mount time during
lustre client mounts with newer linux kernel versions.
This fix does an explict modprobe to force loading lustre kernel
modules.
