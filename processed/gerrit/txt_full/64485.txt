--- Commit Message ---
LU-19960 lnet: derive VLAN NI CPT from parent device

dev_to_node() may return NUMA_NO_NODE for VLAN devices, causing
li_cpt to be set to -1. NIs created from such interfaces inherit
ni_dev_cpt = -1 and are deprioritized by the NI selection algorithm.

Resolve VLAN devices to their underlying physical device and derive CPT
locality from the parent instead so stacked interfaces inherit the
correct NUMA locality.
