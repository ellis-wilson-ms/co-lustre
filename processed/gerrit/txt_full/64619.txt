LU-20003 tests: increase recovery-small/131 delay

In some cases, the OBD_FAIL_OSC_DELAY_IO delay for the 'dd'
expires before the client can be evicted, due to slow 'ssh'
operations in set_nodes_failloc().

Increase the delay by a few seconds to avoid occasional test
failures. Cancel fail_loc after eviction to avoid extra wait.
