LU-5735 mdt: add missing reconstructors for opcodes

Some opcodes (REINT_RMENTRY, REINT_MIGRATE) don't have
reconstructors, which might cause NUll pointer derefernce when
resent RPCs are beinh handled.

This patch adds the reconstructors, and also adds some checking
codes for it.
