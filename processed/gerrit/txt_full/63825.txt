--- Commit Message ---
LU-19856 ldiskfs: restore commit info on ldiskfs patches

Add and improve the provenance of ldiskfs patches that have
been ported to new kernels, but lost the original commit
information (LU ticket, Author, Change-Id, Reviewed-by, etc).

This is useful for understanding why each patch exists, and
providing references to find out more about the background.
It will also be useful when patches are submitted upstream.

In some cases this was partially available, but make it
more consistent between patches for ease of reference.

This information should be preserved when patches are
copied while porting to new kernels.
