--- Commit Message ---
LU-1032 build: Add DKMS support client kernel modules.

This patch permits the creation of a new RPM DKMS-aware.
It will allow for Lustre-Client modules re-build within DKMS
infrastructure.
Lustre-Server modules future re-build has been somewhat planned.
Further work may be needed (scripted?) to handle llite_lloop and
ptlrpd_gss special cases, due to their respective configure-time
and gss/krb5 dependencies on target platforms.

DKMS is a cross distro mechanism for the building and maintenance
of out-of-tree kernel modules. (http://linux.dell.com/dkms/)

Signed-off-by: Bruno Faccini <bruno.faccini@intel.com>
