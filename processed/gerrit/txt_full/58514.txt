Patch Set 1:

(4 comments)

The patch looks good functionally, but I'm not enthused at the function name.  I think there is a bit of a mismash of naming conventions, but I think "llapi_create_volatile_*" names are the worst of the bunch.

We need to keep them for API/ABI compatibility, but I'd prefer "open" instead of "create", and follow the "llapi_layout_*" convention (and in general "noun_verb" naming instead of "verb_noun").  We can have wrappers for compatibility with existing functions, but I'd rather not keep going further down the "llapi_create_volatile_*" road.
