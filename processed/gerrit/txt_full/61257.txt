LU-19222 llite: disable container 'unmount -f'

*** WIP ***

Do not disable the client imports when a container bind mount is
unmounted with the '--force' option. Otherwise, the 'umount -f'
will disable the imports for *all* mountpoints on that client and
they will become inoperative.
