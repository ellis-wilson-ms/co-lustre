--- Commit Message ---
LU-1415 tests: Handling of ZFS's delayed space updates

Space-related updates, such as changes of dn_used and of ds_used_bytes,
are invisible to opertions like dt_attr_get() and dt_statfs() until
after corresponding transaction groups start to be synchronized.  This
patch makes sure the tests do a dt_sync() for each OST before checking
space usage.
