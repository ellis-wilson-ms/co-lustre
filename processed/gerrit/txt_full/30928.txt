LU-10502 utils: redefine progname in lfs.c

This patch redefines progname in lfs.c to fix the issue of
address of a local variable is returned through global variable.

The patch also standardizes some error messages in lfs.c to
remove argv[0] because progname already contains the sub-command
name.

Maybe a better way to fix this is to move everything from lfs_project.[ch] to lfs.c.
