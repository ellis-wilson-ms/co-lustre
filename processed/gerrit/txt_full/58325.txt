LU-18778 lmv: lmv_remote_intent() doesn't copy suppgids

lmv_remote_intent() discards supplementary groups from
the MD op_data. Correct this error by adding two functions:
lmv_remote_prep_md_op() and lmv_remote_finish_md_op(), which
copy free the needed data.
