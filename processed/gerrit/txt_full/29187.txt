LU-10028 obdclass: Export Lustre special mount infomation

Some users need the information about Lustre mount, but the
output information exported by '/proc/mounts' is not enough
especially for some Lustre special mount flags and options
such as abort_recov, noir, etc.
This patch can help to export some Lustre special mount
information via /sys/kernel/debug/lustre/mounts.
