--- Commit Message ---
LU-19869 ptlrpc: move ptlrpc_free_request() out of imp_Lock

ptlrpc_free_request() may result in vfree() which must not
be called with a spinlock held as it can sleep.

let ptlrpc_free_committed() collect requests and return as
a list, so the caller can free requests with no spinlock held.
