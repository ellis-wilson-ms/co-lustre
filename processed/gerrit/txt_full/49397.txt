--- Commit Message ---
LU-16356 hsm: initialize cdt_restore_hash at MDT startup time

cdt_restore_hash is being initialized when the HSM coordinator thread
is started, but it needs to be used via mdt_hsm_restore_is_running()
even when HSM isn't enabled, otherwise the MDT can crash due to a NULL
pointer dereference; move its initialization at MDT startup time to
address this issue.
