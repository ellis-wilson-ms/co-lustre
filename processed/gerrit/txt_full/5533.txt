--- Commit Message ---
LU-2704 llite: GPF in __d_lookup called from ll_statahead_on

dentry_hashtable[] corruption suspected to occur due to a race
allowed with the lack of dcache_lock protection/lock before
calling d_lustre_invalidate() who will itself call directly
__d_drop(). Adding an Assert to early trigger in such case.

Signed-off-by: Bruno Faccini <bruno.faccini@intel.com>
