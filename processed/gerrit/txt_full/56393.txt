--- Commit Message ---
LU-18231 obd: protect all bitfield updates with a spinlock

Audit all the bitfields in struct obd_device and make sure they are
all updated under the obd_dev_lock spinlock.

This is not strictly required in cases where during early initialization
we know there can be no concurrent access or in late cases during
obd destruction where it doesn't matter if a bitfield becomes currupted.
But it is easier to ensure that all changes are done under a spinlock
than whether or not certain updates are safe at the current point in time.
