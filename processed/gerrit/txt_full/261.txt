--- Commit Message ---
Disable build of llite_lloop module with --disable-server.

Works around the fact that RHEL6 on ppc64 uses 64k pages by default,
and llite_lloop does not currently work with >= 64k pages. My immediate
need is client-only for ppc64, so skipping the llite_lloop build when
--disable-server is used effectively postpones the problem.
