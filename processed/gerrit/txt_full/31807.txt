LU-10856 utils: delay "lfs getstripe -M" deprecation

The "lfs getstripe -M" and "lfs mv -M" options were replaced by "-m"
in the 2.9.0 release and a deprecation warning added.

Delay the '-M' option deprecation until 2.12 to allow more time to
clean up the test scripts.
