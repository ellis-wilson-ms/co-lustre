LU-13012 wbc: customizable rule based auto WBC

Define various auto caching rule for WBC on a client similar to
PCC.
When a newly creating directory meets the rule condition, it can
try to obtain EX WBC lock from MDS and keep exclusive access on
the directory under the protection of the EX lock on the client.

The rule can be combination of uid/gid/projid/fname.
Moreover, users can specify a range of uid/gid/projid for auto
WBC cacahing when define a rule.

Add sanity-wbc test_113{a, b, c, d, e}.
