LU-17197 ptlrpc: Sort waiters on close_req completion

During scale testing a performance regression was encountered
and bisected to LU-15947.

Restore mdtest unique directory and file removal performance
for 0K:
  mdtest -v -i 5 -p 120 -C -E -T -r -n 3120 -u -d <dir>
and 32K:
  mdtest -v -i 5 -p 120 -C -w 32768 -E -e 32768 -T -r -n 3120
         -u -d <dir>
