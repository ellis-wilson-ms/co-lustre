LU-10048 ofd: take local locks within transaction

the patch (along with osd companion) addresses long outstanding
technical debt resulting in different locking order on MDT and
OST. with OUT introduction this mismatch began to result in
deadlocks as OUT is used by the both sides and can't easily
support two different locking models simultanously.
