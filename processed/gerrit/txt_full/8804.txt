--- Commit Message ---
LU-4371 tests: remove hard-coded UID values

To make testing more robust in different test configs, remove the
hard-coding of UID 500/501 in sanity-sec.sh and get this from the
environment if available.  Use getent instead of trying to grep it
out of /etc/passwd, in case it is stored in LDAP or similar.

Simplify check_runas_id() to assume the common default parameters
to avoid the common problem of not passing the runas GID correctly.

Consolidate the UID != RUNAS_ID check in check_runas_id().
