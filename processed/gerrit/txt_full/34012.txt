LU-11847 pcc: Add statistics for PCC

To evaluate the PCC prefetching and evicting algorithm, we need to
add statistics for PCC operations to calculate the Byte Hit Rate
(BHR) and File Hit Rate (FHR).
The following statistics for PCC on each client are added in
llite.*.stats:
LPROC_LL_PCC_READ_BYTES         "pcc_read_bytes"
LPROC_LL_PCC_WRITE_BYTES        "pcc_write_bytes"
LPROC_LL_PCC_ATTACH             "pcc_attach_bytes"
LRPOC_LL_PCC_RESTORE            "pcc_restore_bytes"
LPROC_LL_PCC_DETACH             "pcc_detach"
LRPOC_LL_PCC_OPEN               "pcc_open"
