--- Commit Message ---
LU-4463 ptlrpc: fix race in ptlrpc_set_wait

1. wakeup threads waiting on req->rq_set_wait

2. fixes the race between ptlrpc_set_wait/ptlrpcd_check and
ptlrpcd_add_req (called by ptlrpc_replay_req) during recovery,
ptlrpc_replay_req calls ptlrpcd_add_req to queue a request,
which is being processed in ptlrpc_set_wait or ptlrpcd_check,
to replay, then causes the race between the two request_set.
