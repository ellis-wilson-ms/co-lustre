LU-18569 tests: skip ls for ubuntu clients on sanity-sec/75a

There seem to be an unusual number of network timeouts when running
"ls -l" in sanity-sec/75a on Ubuntu clients only. This patch skips
"ls" on Ubuntu clients to investigate whether the frequency of
failures will be reduced on the test system for this test.

Test-Parameters: trivial
Test-Parameters: testlist=sanity-sec env=ONLY=75a,ONLY_REPEAT=100 clientdistro=ubuntu2404
