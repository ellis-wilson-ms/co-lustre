LU-12682 utils: foreign symlink format userhelper sample

This is a sample implementation of the foreign symlink
upcall, it is presently only adding an unnecessary but
harmless slash to the existing default defined rule to
interpret and translate LOV/LMV format ("<UUID>/<UUID>"
-> "<UUID>//<UUID>").
It is intended to serve as a sample for future format
change and interpretation/translation need.
