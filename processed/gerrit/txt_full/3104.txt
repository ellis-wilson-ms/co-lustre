Technically that still overrides DACs, but I don't feel strongly about it.  It may be helpful to discuss the use cases imagined for fid2path.  Setting aside admin type chores, are there any aside from imporving error reporting among application that use open-by-fid? But do note that on lustre/llite/file.c:1669 (the OBD_IOC_FID2PATH case of ll_file_ioctl()), inode may have nothing to do with the FID whose path is requested---rather inode is only used to get a handle on the sbi/mdexp for the FS.
