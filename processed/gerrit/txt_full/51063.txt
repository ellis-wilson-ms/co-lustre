LU-12916 osd-ldiskfs: detect good extent via extent tree

Scanning the page mapping for a good full extent write with
delayed allocation may be too expensive.
To reduce the CPU usage, we improve it by using Ext4 extent status
tree (@extent_status) to detect full extent write.
