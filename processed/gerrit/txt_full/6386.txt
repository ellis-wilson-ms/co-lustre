Looks pretty good, but some fixes are needed:

- (style) several places in patch with trailing whitespace that should be removed
- (minor) if there are many number of open files on one node  (more than PAGE_SIZE/sizeof(DFID) = 4096/26 = 157) then the list of open FIDs will not fit into a single page of proc output. - (minor) the processing of "$fid" and "$ ofid" is more complex than needed. It could be handled more simply by just comparing the values and not the actual parameter paths.
