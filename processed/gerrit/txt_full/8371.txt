Patch Set 7: Looks good to me, but someone else must approve

This is OK. I wish that the other callers of mdt_getattr_internal() were protected as well, since lu_object_attr() will assert on non-exiting objects.

I think that the checks for exists and !remote are getting a little unwieldy, not to mention unwieldy. Defining a mdt_object_find() variant that builds in checks for exists and !remote would be a good start. Second it would be worthwhile to refine HABEO_CORPUS in to one or more target handler flags that check some useful combinations of: that the a body was really passed, that body had a valid FID, that the object was found and that it existed locally.
