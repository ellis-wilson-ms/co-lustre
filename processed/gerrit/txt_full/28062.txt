--- Commit Message ---
LU-5170 lfs: Standardize error messages in lfs_find()

Error and warning messages in lfs_find() are updated to a standard
format.  Messages are prefixed with the name of the utility and the
command that caused the error.  User-provided values are delimited
with single quotes.

Messages that duplicate information provided by the llapi functions
have been removed.
