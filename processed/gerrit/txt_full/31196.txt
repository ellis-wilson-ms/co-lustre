LU-10628 tests: obdfilter-survey case=network fail

obdfilter-survey case=network always exits with non zero
exit status in case of no client mounted because the statement
   "lctl dl| grep osc | grep -v mdt | grep UP"
returns 1

Patch turns off 'set -e' to correctly proceed the case of
unmounted client.
