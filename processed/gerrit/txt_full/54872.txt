--- Commit Message ---
LU-17504 build: fix lock_handle array-index-out-of-bounds warning

On Linux kernel 6.5, due to commit 2d47c6956ab3
("ubsan: Tighten UBSAN_BOUNDS on GCC"), flexible
trailing arrays declared like 'lock_handle[2];'
will generate warnings when CONFIG_UBSAN & co. is
enabled:

  UBSAN: array-index-out-of-bounds in ldlm_request.c:1282:18
  index 2 is out of range for type 'lustre_handle [2]'

Since the lock_handle array was declared as
lock_handle[LDLM_LOCKREQ_HANDLES], the index of
the array should be less than LDLM_LOCKREQ_HANDLES.
