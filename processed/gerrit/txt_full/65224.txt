LU-19179 misc: cleanup checkpatch and spelling.txt

Remove a large number of obsolete checks in spelling.txt that were
added to notify in-flight patches of changes in APIs.  Those changes
are years old and any unlanded patches still using them cannot land
for many other reasons.

warnings are more visible.  Otherwise they are after dozens of lines
of text and hard to see.  With autotest running review-subtest-change
sessions automatically, ONLY_REPEAT is not critical to mention.
