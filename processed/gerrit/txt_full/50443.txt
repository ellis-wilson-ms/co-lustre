LU-16675 tests: add colors to test output

Make PASS green, FAIL red, and SKIP yellow in the test
output. This is much easier to read and looks cool.
The yellow looks fairly orange, so the colors should be
pretty close to Maloo.

Make the banners bold so they stand out more.

Fix style and spacing issues.

LUSTRE_TEST_COLORS will be disabled by default in auster.
Future testing wrappers can opt to enable colors if they
want. Developers can also set their environment.
