--- Message (Andreas Dilger, 2023-10-31 14:50:44.000000000) ---
Patch Set 1: Code-Review+1

> Please consider the race when two threads are reclaiming the slab,
 > the first thread hasn't yet managed to free anything, the second
 > thread immediately returns from slab reclaim. Is it possible to get
 > ENOMEM during this race window?

It seems possible, but unlikely?  If it became a real issue then we could have the other thread(s) busy-wait on the trylock until the holder had actually freed something.  Not sure it is worthwhile to do that yet.
