--- Commit Message ---
LU-19644 tests: make sanity/150ib robust on extent-based fs

On extent-backed filesystems, stat block counts can vary due to
extent-tree metadata, causing flaky equality checks in test_150ib.
Switch to SEEK_HOLE/SEEK_DATA lseek checks for punch validation,
while keeping strict block-count assertions for indirect mapping.
