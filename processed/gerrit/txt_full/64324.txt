--- Commit Message ---
LU-19740 utils: Restrict {tunefs,mkfs}.lustre to one device

It's confusing that tunefs.lustre and mkfs.lustre with ldiskfs
can accept multiple devices but only process a single device.
This patch makes the following changes:
1) tunefs.lustre only accepts a single device, regardless of
   whether the backend filesystem is ZFS or ldiskfs.
2) mkfs.lustre with ldiskfs only accepts a single device.
3) mkfs.lustre with ZFS remains unchanged.

Signed-off-by: Xiao Yang <xyang@ddn.com>
