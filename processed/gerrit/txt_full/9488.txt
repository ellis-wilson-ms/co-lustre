While looking at the code, i was surprised that non-O_CREAT open reply reconstruction goes through mdt_reint_open(). Here both the parent and child are looked up (by name for the child) again which seems really wrong to me in the case of reply reconstruction since both might have been unlinked between the first request processing and the resent. It is definitely not related to this patch, but i think we have a bug here which could cause mfd to be leaked and orphan files to stay around until next MDS reboot.
