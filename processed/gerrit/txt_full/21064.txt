--- Commit Message ---
LU-7117 mdt: mdt unlink should lock before lookup

MDT unlink should lock parent before lookup child, otherwise the
child may be stale in use later.

Also enable osd xattr cache for LMV, because it's fetched more than
one time in several reint operations (which is used to detect whether
object is striped).
