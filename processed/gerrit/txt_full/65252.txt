--- Commit Message ---
LU-20123 ldlm: fix lock_limit_mb cannot increase

lock_limit_mb_store() rejects any value larger than the
current lock_limit_mb due to a misplaced validation copied
from the reclaim_threshold side during the debugfs-to-sysfs
migration. This makes it impossible to increase lock_limit_mb
once lowered.

Move the check to lock_reclaim_threshold_mb_store() where it
belongs, preventing reclaim_threshold from exceeding
lock_limit, and remove it from lock_limit_mb_store(). The
existing check in lock_limit_mb_store() that validates
lock_limit_mb >= lock_reclaim_threshold_mb is retained.

Fixes: 319fe53bca ("LU-8066 ldlm: move debugfs entries to general sysfs")
