LU-1432 ptlrpc: LBUG in lprocfs_free_client_stats()

When target_handle_connect() does reconnection with an existing export
object obtained from lustre_hash_lookup(), the refcount of the export
object may reach zero while still being used there.
This case ends up in two phenomena, the one is causing OS-panic when
the export object has already been released, the other is the LBUG in
lprocfs_free_client_stats() when the export object has still been
listed in obd_zombie_exports and not released yet.

This patch adjusts the refcount of export in target_handle_connect()
properly in order not to cause the above phenomena and serialize
class_cleanup() and target_handle_connect() in order to remove the
race-condition of obd_nid_hash
