--- Commit Message ---
LU-17484 gss: do not resend SEC_CTX_INIT rpcs

SEC_CTX_INIT rpcs are initializing GSS security contexts between
peers. They cannot be resent to failover NIDs, as they contain
information specific to the targeted remote peer NID.
