--- Commit Message ---
LU-4009 osp: batch local cancels

every cancel updates a whole llog header (8k by default)
which is an expesive update from ZIL point of view.
OSP doesn't need to cancel the records immediately, instead
it can batch cancels to do more job with a 8k write.
the patch adds support for batched cancels to llog component.
