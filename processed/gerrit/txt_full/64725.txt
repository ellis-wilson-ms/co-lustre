LU-20024: tcu: fix the space accounting error with TCU enabled

The statfs accounts the space usages in block size unit. However,
the space usage of TCU via the project ID of Trash Can is
accounted in bytes.
This patch fixes the space accounting error by converting the
bytes of project to blocks.

Add sanityn/118u to verify it works as expected.
