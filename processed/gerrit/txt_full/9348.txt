James: size_t is implementation defined; it's guaranteed to be the type of a sizeof expression. Therefore we cannot put size_t in struct which go on the wire. But IIRC there are places where obd_count is used but doesn't go on the wire. There are also tons of places where int or unsigned int is used but we should be using size_t or ssize_t.
