--- Commit Message ---
LU-10438 mdt: layout truncate compatibility

FLR change the protocol to instantiate layout before truncate
request. This patch makes old server understand 2.11+ clients'
truncate write intent request.

In PFL design, client issues [0, size) intent to MDS to instantiate
objects in this extent. While in FLR design, the intent servers two
purposes: 1) make objects across [size, EOF) on other mirrors stale,
and 2) instantiate objects in the chosen write mirror. And FLR
chooses to use [size, EOF) as the extent of truncate write intent
request.
