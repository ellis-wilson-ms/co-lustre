--- Commit Message ---
LU-19732 ldiskfs: keep RHEL10.x not to update cmtime

RHEL10.x uses "inode_set_ctime_current" instead of "current_time"
to update the ctime/mtime of the inode, then it needs to modify
"inode_set_ctime_current" to support the check of the S_NOCMTIME
to ignore the update of ctime/mtime.
