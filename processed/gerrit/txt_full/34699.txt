Elena, you are correct that this patch removes the MDT randomization
added in your patch, however it leaves the (AFAIK) original functionality 
that you cared about, which is testing compatibility with older releases.

The other alternative is to revert the whole patch, because this change
is causing a large number of test failures, which is causing many other
patches to fail testing, and hurting patch landing as a result.  Since the
testing behavior is now random, it will take much longer to verify if a "fix"
is actually fixing a test failure or just randomly passing.

My preference is to land this patch to keep the other functionality intact,
but if you want to revert the whole patch until you fix it that is also possible.
