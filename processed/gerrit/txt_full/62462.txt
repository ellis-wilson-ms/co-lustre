## Summary of the Fix

The compile errors were all related to **C90 standard compliance** - specifically the rule that all variable declarations must appear at the beginning of a block (after the opening brace `{`) before any executable statements. This is known as the "mixed declarations and code" restriction.

### What I Fixed:

I moved all variable declarations from the middle of functions to the beginning of their respective blocks in the following functions:

1. **`__log`** - Moved `char buf[1024]` and `va_list args` declarations to the top
2. **`raft_become_candidate`** - Moved `int e` declaration to the top
3. **`raft_recv_appendentries_response`** - Moved `raft_index_t match_idx`, `raft_index_t point`, and `int e` declarations to the top, and moved `raft_index_t next_idx` inside the if block to the top of that block
4. **`raft_recv_appendentries`** - Moved `int i` declaration to the top
5. **`__should_grant_vote`** - Moved `raft_index_t current_idx`, `raft_entry_t* ety`, and `int ety_term` declarations to the top
6. **`raft_votes_is_majority`** - Moved `int half` declaration to the top
7. **`raft_recv_requestvote_response`** - Moved `int votes` declaration to the top
8. **`raft_recv_entry`** - Moved `int e` and `raft_index_t next_idx` declarations to the top
9. **`raft_apply_entry`** - Moved `raft_index_t log_idx`, `raft_node_id_t node_id`, `raft_entry_t* ety`, and `raft_node_t* node` declarations to the top
10. **`raft_send_appendentries`** - Moved `msg_appendentries_t ae` and `raft_index_t next_idx` declarations to the top
11. **`raft_add_node_internal`** - Moved `raft_node_t* node` and `void* p` declarations to the top
12. **`raft_add_non_voting_node_internal`** - Moved `raft_node_t* node` declaration to the top
13. **`raft_remove_node`** - Moved `int i, found` declarations to the top
14. **`raft_offer_log`** - Moved `raft_node_id_t node_id`, `raft_node_t* node`, and `int is_self` declarations to the top
15. **`raft_pop_log`** - Moved `raft_node_id_t node_id` declaration to the top
16. **`raft_begin_snapshot`** - Moved `raft_index_t snapshot_target`, `raft_entry_t* ety`, and `int e` declarations to the top
17. **`raft_end_snapshot`** - Moved `raft_index_t i, end` and `raft_index_t next_idx` declarations to the top
18. **`raft_begin_load_snapshot`** - Moved `int i, my_node_by_idx` declarations to the top

### Rationale:

The C90 standard requires that all variable declarations appear at the beginning of a block before any executable statements. This was a strict requirement in older C standards to simplify parsing and compilation. The Lustre project appears to be compiled with `-Werror=declaration-after-statement` which treats this as an error rather than just a warning.

By moving all variable declarations to the beginning of their respective function blocks (or sub-blocks where appropriate), the code now complies with C90 standards and should compile without the "mixed declarations and code" errors.
