--- Commit Message ---
LU-19548 lfs: updated mirror split for EC support

When splitting a data mirror that has an associated EC parity
mirror, automatically split (or destroy) the parity mirror as
well. Splitting a parity mirror directly prints a warning since
it removes EC protection from the data mirror.

Add --force-no-ec option to override this behavior and split
only the specified mirror without affecting its EC counterpart.
