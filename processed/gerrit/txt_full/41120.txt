LU-14287 tests: Improve sanity/150b test-case

On rare occasion when check_fallocate binary fails
it leaves large $DIR/$tfile under the mount. On
multiple runs check_fallocate subsequently fails
with "no space on device".

This patch adds a 'stack_trap' on exit to remove
file/s created by the testcase on success or failure.
