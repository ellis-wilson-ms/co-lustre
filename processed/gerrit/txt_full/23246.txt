LU-8728 tests: fix conf-sanity:88 for multiple MDS case

start_mds starts all MDSs. lctl clear_conf fails because
it expects only one mds combined with mgs started with
nosvc option. The fix is to force start_mdt to start only
needed mds.

Similarly for combined MDS1 and MGS force stop_mdt to
stop only needed mds.
