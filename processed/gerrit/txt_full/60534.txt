--- Commit Message ---
LU-19235 nodemap: add pool_quota_ops rbac role

The purpose of the new pool_quota_ops rbac role is to separate pool
specific quotas from UID/GID/ProjID regular quotas. When not set,
clients cannot set pool specific quotas ('lfs setquota --pool').

sanity-sec test_64c is updated to exercise the new pool_quota_ops rbac
role.
