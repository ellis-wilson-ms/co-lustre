LU-20103 tests: sanityn execvp fails on declare

sanityn test_77k, and other tests that use nrs_write_read encounter

running as uid/gid/euid/egid 500/500/500/500, groups: 500
[declare] [-a] [pids_r]
execvp fails running declare

issue. This is because execvp treats declare as a executable while
it is actually a bash builtin cmd.
