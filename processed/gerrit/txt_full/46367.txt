LU-15234 lnet: ref leak in lnet_ping_router_locked

If lnet_update_rc_data_locked() returns NULL then there's a peer_ni
reference leak in lnet_ping_router_locked(). Ensure the ref taken at
beginning of lnet_ping_router_locked() is dropped before the function
returns.
