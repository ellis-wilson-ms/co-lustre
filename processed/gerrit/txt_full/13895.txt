--- Commit Message ---
LU-5319 protocol: support for multislot feature

re-use ptlrpc_body's last_xid to transfer 16bit tag. the tag
will be used to index an virtual (per-export) slot in last_rcvd
file when many concurrent modifying RPCs are in flight.

OBD_CONNECT_MULTISLOT flag.

ocd_reqs_in_flight in obd_connect_data specifying the maximum
concurrent modifying RPCs.
