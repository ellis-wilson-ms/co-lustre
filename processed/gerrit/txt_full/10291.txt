Patch Set 2: Code-Review-1

Hi Dmitry,

In run_one(), tfile and tdir are defined as follows:

        export tfile=f${testnum}.${TESTSUITE}
        export tdir=d${testnum}.${TESTSUITE}

They are unique to each sub-test, and cleaned up at the end of sanity test suite in check_and_cleanup_lustre():

        [ -n "$DIR" ] && rm -rf $DIR/[Rdfs][0-9]* ||
                error "remove sub-test dirs failed"

So, we do not need cleanup them in each sub-test.
