--- Commit Message ---
LU-20082 lnet: fix buffer assignment in lnet_sock_setbuf

When setting tx/rx buffer sizes via lnet_sock_setbuf(), `rxbufsize`
was incorrectly assigned to `sk->sk_sndbuf`, causing both send and
receive buffers to behave unexpectedly.

Fixes: 99d9638d6c ("LU-13783 libcfs: support removal of kernel_setsockopt()")
