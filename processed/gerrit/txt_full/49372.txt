LU-16160 llite: make page state consistent

ll_fault()->vvp_io_fault_start()->vvp_io_kernel_fault(), and
ll_readpage() got called, and it take an extra cl_page reference, and
that makes the page won't be deleted from pagecache even
ll_releasepage() chooses this page. And when vvp_io_kernel_fault() got
returned, it tries release the extra reference, and following LBUG
could be met.

(cl_page.c:390:cl_pagevec_put())
	ASSERTION( page->cp_state == CPS_FREEING ) failed:
