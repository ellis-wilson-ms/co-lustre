--- Commit Message ---
LU-17916 llite: large folio allocation option

Enable large folio support via mount and get/set param:
   lctl get_param llite.*.large_folios
   lctl set_param llite.*.large_folios=[0|1]

Performance on a trivial VM cluster with patched 6.6.13 kernel:
mpirun -np 1 $IOR -e -l z -b 24g -t 4M -rw -o /mnt/lustre/ior/fio.bin
            large folio        4k folio             master
  write     840.41 MiB/s      689.75 MiB/s        243.37 MiB/s
  read      815.19 MiB/s      642.79 MiB/s        570.18 MiB/s

mpirun -np 4 $IOR -e -l z -b 4g -t 4M -rw -o /mnt/lustre/ior/fio.bin
            large folio         4k folio            master
  write    1120.80 MiB/s       861.44 MiB/s       569.78 MiB/s
  read     1065.38 MiB/s      1064.32 MiB/s       841.83 MiB/s
