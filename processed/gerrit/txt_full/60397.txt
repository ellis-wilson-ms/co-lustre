--- Commit Message ---
LU-19180 osc: ensure ext is aligned for rpc

When sending RPCs to older servers, without unaligned_dio support,
a large unaligned RPC can be construct from an extent. In such
cases the RPC is failed with -EINVAL before being sent.

However this is sub-optimal.

Instead avoid building an extent that could trigger interop and
flag extents with oe_no_merge if the next page would fail the
64k aligned check.

Fixes: 262957eaef ("LU-18691 quota: quota interop check for 64k page clients")
