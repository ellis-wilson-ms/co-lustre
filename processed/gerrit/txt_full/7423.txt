--- Commit Message ---
LU-136 build: add vmalloc option to allocate sbi->s_group_desc

Large kmalloc() for the group descriptor buffer pointers can
fail for large filesystems, which will cause the "not enough
memory" error while mounting. Fall back to vmalloc() if the
kmalloc() failed, as was done for s_flex_groups.

This patch is being revived to perform large-lun, 32TB,
testing on b1_8.
