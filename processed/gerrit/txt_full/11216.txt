LU-5408 fid: cleanup fid_is_*() names and uses

In fid_is_md_operative() return false for FIDs with seq
FID_SEQ_OST_MDT0 (because these are OST FIDs).

Rename fid_seq_is_mdt0() to fid_seq_is_ost_mdt0() and fid_is_mdt0() to
fid_is_ost_mdt0().  Rename fid_seq_is_default() to
fid_seq_is_lov_default(). Remove the confusing or redundant functions
fid_seq_is_mdt(), fid_seq_is_dot_lustre(), and fid_is_internal().  The
*is_mdt* names may have made sense before DNE but are just confusing
now.

Remove FID policy from some utilities. In lhsmtool_posix.c remove
fid_is_file(). In liblustreapi.c and lustre_rsync.c remove calls to
fid_is_sane(fid). Move fid_is_sane() out of lustre_idl.h and into
lustre_fid.h as a way of sending a message about who should and who
should not be using this function.
