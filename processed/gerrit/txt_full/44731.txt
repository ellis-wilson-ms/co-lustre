--- Commit Message ---
LU-14943 lnet: Update health of message originator on receive

If we're forwarded a message from a router then we do not currently
update the health value of the peer NI of the message originator.

Compare the NID of the msg_rxpeer peer NI against the src_nid in
the message header, and, if these do not match, lookup the peer NI
for the source NID for the message and update its health value.
