LU-19874 osd-wbcfs: rename statics to avoid conflicts

The OSDs in Lustre often use similar names for static
variables. Add 'wbcfs' to a number of static variables
in osd-wbcfs to avoid conflicts when compiling the OSD
into the kernel image.
