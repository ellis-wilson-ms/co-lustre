LU-14950 osc: Remove osc_consume_write_grant

Perf investigations with the patch series from LU-13799
showed that calling osc_consume_write_grant takes about 2%
of the time and it also holds a spin lock on the import, so
it can meaningfully impair multithreading.

Remove the function, replacing it with a += and move
the flag-set loop outside of the spin lock, since the spin
lock is protecting the import state, not the page lists.

Signed-off-by: Patrick Farrell <pfarrell@whamcloud.com>
