--- Commit Message ---
LU-4053 hsm: Don't acquire layout lock on dirty flush

It's not necessary to acquire layout lock on flush dirty.

This patch reverted the change of LU-3160:
(3141db609d95d379761e3b54899618b4037d38f6) where we disabled
'ignore_layout' for dirty flush to avoid the race of writeback
vs. layout changing. Actually, such race can be avoided by
fixing the lov_io_init() to always get conf lock regardless
of the 'ignore_layout' flag.
