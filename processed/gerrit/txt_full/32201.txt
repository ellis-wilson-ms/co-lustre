--- Commit Message ---
LU-10965 doc: add mirror options to "lfs find" in lfs.1

This patch adds the following mirror related search
options to "lfs find" command in lfs man page:

[[!] --mirror-count|-N [+-]n]
[[!] --mirror-state <[^]state>]

The usage of the options are explained in separate
"lfs find" man page.
