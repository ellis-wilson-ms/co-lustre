--- Commit Message ---
LU-7173 ldlm: do not grant intent-only lock

In ldlm_process_inodebits_lock(), do not grant intent-only lock
on lock server side, ldlm_lock_enqueue() could destroy it right
away, presuming that it's intent-only and expecting it no granted.

Add debug message for this case and if it is shown, it is confirmed
then.
