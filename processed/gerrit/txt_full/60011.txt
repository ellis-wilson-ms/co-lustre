LU-19143: tcu: store original UID/GID/ProjID into "unrm" XATTR

When files or directories are moved into Trash, the UID/GID/PROJID
will be changed from the existing IDs to the IDs defined by
- mdd.*.trash_can_uid
- mdd.*.trash_can_gid
- mdd.*.trash_can_projid

The original UID/GID/PROJID are saved in the "trusted.unrm" XATTR
for use during restore. And the space and inodes usage accounting
for the quota of the original UID/GID/PROJID are also updated with
the corresponding trash can UID/GID/PROJID.

Add sanityn/test_117t to verify it works as expected.
