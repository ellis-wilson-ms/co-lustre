--- Commit Message ---
LU-8854 llapi: do not define strlcpy & strlcat

LustreAPI should not define/export stlrcpy and strlcat,
because they may be defined by other libraries which
people want to link against.

Instead, use the preprocessor to let us keep calling
strl{cpy,cat}, while really defining ll_strl{cpy,cat}.

Signed-off-by: Patrick Farrell <paf@cray.com>
