> if (magic == LOV_MAGIC_COMP_V1 || magic == LOV_MAGIC_FOREIGN)
 
I really don't like the "if it isn't this specific magic, then it must be (something else known)" kind of check. As we add more and more different layouts, the chance of getting that wrong is very high.
