LU-20031 dkms: fix OFED symlink detection in dkms.conf

DOCA OFED 3.2.0+ uses DKMS by default, and installs headers
under /usr/src/ofa_kernel-dkms/ while creating symlinks from
/usr/src/ofa_kernel/$arch/$kernelver to the real directories.
The 'find' in the DKMS module selection cannot locate rdma_cm.h
through these symlinks.

Narrow $paths to the kernel-specific subdirectory before
searching, then use 'find -H' so the symlink at the starting
point is followed. This mirrors the approach taken in commit
db4927c53a ("LU-19820 build: fix ofa_kernel detection error").
