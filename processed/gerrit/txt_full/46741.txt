Patrick,

>So you are planning to just use one Jira...
Yes. You are correct. 

>Can you explain to me what this change is doing?  (I just don't understand what @ and * are doing here.)
shellcheck insist that '@' be changed with '*' as it expects all arguments to be string. 

my_array=(1 2 3 4 5)
${my_array[@]} is '1' '2' '3' '4' '5' (five saperate elements)

however...

${my_array[*]} is '1 2 3 4 5' (just one string)

In our case we are just mostly printing and not processing it further so either way both are correct. 

This is he error for '@' vs '*' thrown by shellcheck. "sanity.sh:11965:30: error: Argument mixes string and array. Use * or separate argument. [SC2145]"
