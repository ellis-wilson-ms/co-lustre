LU-16463 llite: fix the opencache for llapi_open_by_fid()

Since bdf7788, all the opens from llapi_open_by_fid are cached
because open_by_handle_at() adds the lld_nfs_dentry flag on the
dentry.

This breaks llsom_sync because LSOM are updated on the MDT on close
requests.
Moreover, on a scanning node (like robinhood or llsom_sync node) a
*lot of opens* can be unnecessary cached.

This patch adds an optional flag in the lustre handle to inform lustre
not to consider the open call coming from NFS.

"llsom_sync" is modified to clear LRU cache to flush the opencache
when using the "--sync" option. "sanity test 807b" covers this use
case.

"sanity test 806/807" are updated to not clear LRU cache before using
"lfs getsom".
