--- Commit Message ---
LU-3919 lvfs: fid2dentry violates locking order

The proper locking order should be i_mutex -> journal_lock, however,
in most case we call mds/mgs_fid2dentry() (which acquires i_mutex
for iopen dir) after journal started, that violates the locking
order and will cause deadlock in certain situation.

Actually it's not necessary to hold the i_mutex of iopen dir when
performing fid2dentry. This patch skips the i_mutex locking for
that case to avoid the locking order violation.
