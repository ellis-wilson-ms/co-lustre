--- Commit Message ---
LU-4076 fld: add local fldb to each target

1. Add local FLDB to each MDT, so OSD/OUT can check whether
FID is remote by looking up local FLDB, i.e. no need send RPC
to MDT0.

2. OSD will only do local lookup when checking remote FID.

3. During upgrade, MDTn(n != 0) needs to retrieve its fldb
entries from controller(MDT0) and insert into the local
FLDB.

4. MDT should also use LWP(instead of OSP) to communicate
with sequence controller (MDT0).

Yes, there is FLDB cache right now, but no local on-disk FLDB. And this patch will maintain a local on-disk FLDB, and it is not about the cache. Or I miss your point, Alex?

1. during initialization, each MDT will load these local FLDB entries from to cache, i.e. it does not need do FLD lookup RPC during setup and replay process to avoid unnecessary troubles.

2. If it is upgrade process, each non-MDT0 needs to read FLDB entries from MDT0 to write them into its local FLDB.

3. Other modules like OUT and OSP might need this local FLDB as well, so I do not put them inside OSD. besides, I do not any negative things to put them under /fld, unless I miss sth.
