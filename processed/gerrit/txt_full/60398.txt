--- Commit Message ---
LU-19180 ptlrpc: virtual 4k bulk alignment

A 64k side cannot transfer a 64k page if the first page has
an offset, so the total size exceeds LNet_MTU. However, a 4k
side can transfer some pages in MD for the same transfer.
This causes a situation where MD has a different amount of
data for transfer on both sides, and the transfer is hung.
Use 4k virtual alignment for the transfer to synchronize
both sides.
