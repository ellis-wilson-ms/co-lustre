--- Commit Message ---
LU-16035 kfilnd: Repost imm buffers on correct EP

Prior to this change, immediate buffers could be reposted on the EP
associated with the transaction instead of the EP associated with the
immediate buffer. This can cause EPs to have no immediate receive
buffers and prevent LNet from bring down the kfi interface.

This is fixed by always used the immediate buffer EP to repost when
the immediate buffer when references are returned.
