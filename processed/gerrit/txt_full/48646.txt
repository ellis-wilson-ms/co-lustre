LU-16017 osd-zfs: OSD_BASE_EA_IN_BONUS should include names

When calculating OSD_BASE_EA_IN_BONUS, include sizes of both xattr
names and their contents, including:
	trusted.link
	trusted.lma
	trusted.lov
	trusted.version

This allows ZFS to choose a dnode size large enough to store the
xattrs in the dnode rather than a bonus block.  Storing the xattrs in
a bonus block results in unnecessary additional I/O when creating,
writing, and reading objects, hurting performance.

The above xattr list reflects objects on MDTs which store inodes.
Although OST objects containing bulk data have fewer xattrs, this
patch result in an appropriate minimum dnode size (1k) for those
objects as well.
