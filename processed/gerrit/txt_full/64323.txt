--- Commit Message ---
LU-19842 tests: fix awk quoting in wait_all_targets_blocked()

In wait_all_targets_blocked(), the error handler uses awk with
single quotes to extract the MDT count from lfsck_query output.
Single quotes prevent shell variable expansion of ${com} and
${status}, so the awk pattern never matches and the error
message shows an empty count: "only of 4 MDTs are in completed".

Fixes: 955e38051765 ("LU-17385 tests: always_except sanity-lfsck/24")
