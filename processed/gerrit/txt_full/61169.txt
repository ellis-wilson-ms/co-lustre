LU-18222 quota: add LQA rb-tree and management commands

Add general commands to operate with LQA at QMT level: create,
destroy, add or remove ranges, list LQAs and ranges inside LQAs.
Ranges in an LQA are stored in a red-black tree.

Created LQA has 6 qmt_lqa entries for each resource(data and metadata)
and quota type(user, group and project). These entries are stored in
6 separate lists. Each LQA entry besides service objects(references,
locking, ...) has an LQE and a red-black tree. LQA LQE is not stored
in lquota_site hash table as a regular LQE - it is not required as LQA
LQE represents an aggregation of quota ids, not just a single one.
Red-black tree is used instead to check whether quota ID relates to
recent LQA.
