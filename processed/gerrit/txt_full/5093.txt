LU-2641 ldiskfs: handle missing ext4_ext_cache->ec_type

Commit b05e6ae5 (v2.6.37) dropped the ec_type member from struct
ext4_ext_cache because it can be generated using a combination of
the ec_len and ec_start values.

We introduce a helper to do the same when the member isn't present.
