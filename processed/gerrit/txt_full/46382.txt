LU-15248 tests: use killall -g to kill racer child processes too

It looks like when you just killall a shell script,
the child pocesses it happened to execute don't get a signal,
so depending on how long they run it might prevent the
subsequent cleanup.

Remove traps from the racer scripts as it's no longer
necessary with killall -g and in majority of the scripts
it was a noop anyway since no backgrounding was taking place.
