LU-19506 nodemap: appended filesets must consider alt filesets

When mounting a subdirectory, several mounting rules are in place that
handle matching a subdirectory to the correct fileset. Here, the man
page for "lctl nodemap_fileset_add" states case 4: "If fileset matches
in 3, the presented mounting subdirectory is appended to the primary
fileset."

However, this results in the read-only flag not being considered if
the appended fileset now matches a read-only alternate fileset. For
instance, for the fileset configuration
1. /dir
2. /dir/subdir (ro)
it is possible to mount "...:/FSNAME/subdir", which appends "subdir"
to the primary fileset, resulting in the subdirectory "/dir/subdir".
Currently, no other fileset is considered, but, in such a case, the
alt fileset imposes read-only restrictions. Therefore, alt filesets
should be considered in case 4 of the mounting rules for filesets.

This patch addresses this issue and modifies sanity-sec test_27e to
test this case.
