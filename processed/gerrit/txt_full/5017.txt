--- Message (Andreas Dilger, 2013-01-22 21:30:19.000000000) ---
Patch Set 1: I would prefer that you didn't submit this

(7 inline comments)

I don't like the idea of restricting the grant request to a single RPC, when each of the other RPCs is consuming grants.

It might be that some thread is doing a lot of high-priority RPCs under some cancelled lock, but the RPC that contains the grant request is waiting in the normal RPC queue, or has been dropped and needs to be resent.  That would mean that the client can quickly run out of grant and all RPCs are blocked, instead of just the one RPC that is low priority.

If you have 100k clients, then 200G of reserved space is about 1% of the filesystem space for 2T 8+2 RAID-6.
