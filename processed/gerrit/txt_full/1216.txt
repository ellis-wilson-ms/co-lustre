another thing we might want to fix is:

seq_client_alloc_fid()
......

rc = seq_client_alloc_seq(seq, &seqnr);

if (rc) {
    CERROR("%s: Can't allocate new sequence, "
           "rc %d\n", seq->lcs_name, rc);

    cfs_up(&seq->lcs_sem);

    RETURN(rc);
}

I think the cfs_up() at here will release the mutex we don't even have.

actually, seq_fid_alloc_prep() and seq_fid_alloc_fini() will change state of mutex (lcs_sem) on return, which is really confusing, I would suggest to cleanup this too.
