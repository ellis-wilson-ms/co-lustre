LU-15248 tests: kill all fs users at the end of racer

Wehn killall in racer cleanup script kills the shell scripts
it does not kill their child processes, so a number of such
processes might remain and be using the filesystem
preventing it from unmounting.

Add fuser -k -m at the end to ensure all of fs users are gone,
or at least the ones that are killable.
