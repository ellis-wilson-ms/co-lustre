--- Commit Message ---
LU-15367 llite: Add iotrace support for flock

Add D_IOTRACE logging to ll_file_flock to enable
tracking of flock operations in iotrace logs.

The iotrace_to_multiop.py script is updated to parse
ll_file_flock iotrace messages and convert them to
multiop 'j' commands (flock exclusive lock).

The script filters out unlock operations (type 2) and
only generates 'j' commands for actual lock operations
(type 0 or 1).

A new test (test_441c) is added to verify that flock
operations are correctly captured and converted.
