LU-19286 tests: Add lnet cleanup to test_280 in sanity-lnet.sh

test_280 in the sanity-lnet.sh test suite was missing proper lnet
cleanup before executing its test logic. This could lead to test
failures or inconsistent results when lnet modules are already
loaded from previous tests.

Added cleanup_lnet() call at the beginning of test_280() to ensure
a clean state before the test execution.
