--- Commit Message ---
LU-20167 build: Add _FAMILY suffix to HAVE_GENL_DUMPIT_INFO

Both lustre's config.h and extra ofed's config.h defined
the same HAVE_GENL_DUMPIT_INFO with different meanings:
lustre: Check whether struct genl_dumpit_info has the 'family' field
extra ofed: Check whether struct genl_dumpit_info is defined

The collision caused a compilation error so rename lustre's
HAVE_GENL_DUMPIT_INFO to HAVE_GENL_DUMPIT_INFO_FAMILY.

Test-Parameters: trivial
