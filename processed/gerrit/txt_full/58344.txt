LU-16518 lst: fix switch-case unannotated fall-through

Convert the service #define statements to an enum
srpc_service_type. Explicitly handle each case in
srpc_service2request() to avoid the unannotated fall
through error reported by Clang. Increase SRPC_SERVICE_MAX_ID
by 1 to avoid a conflict with SRPC_SERVICE_PING - and
adjust callers to account for this.
