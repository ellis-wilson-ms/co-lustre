LU-12288 lnet: preferred flag policy does not work

If there are 2 peers and the both peers are preffered,
there are cases where the Preffered peer(the first one)
is not selected(Preffered flag is ignored).

We fixed that route selection is in the following order.
    1.Preferred
    2.Health Value
    3.Credits
    4.Seq Number
