--- Commit Message ---
LU-17431 nodemap: require NID range first on dynamic nodemap

Because a dynamic nodemap inherits properties from a parent which gets
associated when the first NID range is added, prevent modifications of
a dynamic nodemap's properties as long as it does not have any NID
range. Otherwise these modifications get lost.
