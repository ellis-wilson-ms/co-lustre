--- Commit Message ---
LU-5896 mdt: allow clients with old mdc_swap_layouts

The size of struct mdc_swap_layouts was increased to improve HSM
layout swap handling, but this broke 'lfs migrate' interoperability
with old clients and new servers due to request buffer size checks:

    lustre_msg_buf_v2() buffer[2] size 8 too small required 24, opc=61
    __req_capsule_get() Wrong buffer for field 'swap_layouts' (2 of 6)
        in format 'MDS_SWAP_LAYOUTS', 8 vs. 24 (client)

Add a new RMF_F_MINIMUM_SIZE RPC buffer check, to ensure the buffer is
at least the requested size, but can be larger.

Add compatibility for smaller mdc_swap_layouts_217 until 3.4.53.

Fixes: 63caacb042 ("LU-5896 mdt: allow release after blocking migrations")
