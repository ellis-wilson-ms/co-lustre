Yeah... ss stands for 'socket stats'. It exists for a very long time, and have been advertised as the 'netstat' replacement for a while. It comes along with 'ip', which has the same story as an 'ifconfig' replacement. 'iproute2' is available and installed everywhere for so long, that's not really a big deal.

Than, are those tools better? That's a different story, but the history is moving away from ifconfig and netstat for sure...
