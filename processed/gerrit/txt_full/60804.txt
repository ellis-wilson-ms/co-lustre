LU-8066 obdclass: Use spinlock when accessing export nodemap

Printing the assigned nodemap on the export through debugfs was not
protected through its spinlock. This patch adds the spinlock and
improves the output to include the client UUID. Otherwise, the nodemap
name may get printed multiple times without further information.
