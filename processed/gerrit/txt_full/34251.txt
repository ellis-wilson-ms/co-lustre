--- Commit Message ---
LU-10279 tests: Reduce chance of writeback in 101

Despite disabling the time based dirty writeback in the
sanityn 101 tests, analysis of test failures shows that
occasionally some non-Lustre process is flushing our dirty
data.

A possible culprit is memory pressure.  Drop caches and
locks before each of these tests to reduce the chance of
writeback.

Test-Parameters: trivial testlist=sanityn
Test-Parameters: fstype=ldiskfs mdscount=2 mdtcount=4 testlist=sanityn,sanityn
Test-Parameters: fstype=zfs mdscount=2 mdtcount=4 testlist=sanityn,sanityn
