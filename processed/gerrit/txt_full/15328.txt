--- Commit Message ---
LU-6738 llite: Sanity check fid in ll_fid2path

We currently do not sanity check fids provided from
lfs fid2path before passing them to lower layers.

This can lead to an LBUG.

Instead, we should sanity check the FID as soon as possible
and return an error to userspace.
