LU-874 ptlrpc: handle in-flight hqreq correctly

--- debug use only ---
If there are in-flight requests pending, we shouldn't timeout the
corresponding ldlm locks. We shouldn't remove the requests from export
exp_queued_rpc list until the requests are really finished.
