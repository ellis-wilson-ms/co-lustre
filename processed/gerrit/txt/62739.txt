LU-19641: liblustreapi: fix lhm_pattern in lov_dump_hsm_lmm

$ lfs getstripe /lustre/PCC
...
    lcme_id:             131074
    lcme_mirror_id:      2
    lcme_flags:          init
    lcme_extent.e_start: 0
    lcme_extent.e_end:   EOF
      lhm_magic:         0x0BD70BD0
      lhm_pattern:       hsm
      lhm_length:        56
      lhm_type:          0x00000003 (pccro)
      lhm_flags:         exists,archived,pccro,0x109
...

Foreign component are used for S3 PCC and POSIX but not HSM.

This patch fixes the lhm_pattern to "foreign":

$ lfs getstripe /lustre/PCC
...
    lcme_id:             131074
    lcme_mirror_id:      2
    lcme_flags:          init
    lcme_extent.e_start: 0
    lcme_extent.e_end:   EOF
      lhm_magic:         0x0BD70BD0
      lhm_pattern:       foreign
      lhm_length:        56
      lhm_type:          0x00000003 (pccro)
      lhm_flags:         exists,archived,pccro,0x109
...
