--- Commit Message ---
LU-0000 ptlrpc: fix NUMA binding policy to use CPT

The ptlrpcd binding policy PDB_POLICY_NEIGHBOR does not correctly
manage offline cpus when kernel supports NUMA.

This patch implements a separate policy PDB_POLICY_CPT that relies
on the libcfs compute partition table. With that policy, each ptlrpcd
thread is bound to a cpu of the cptable. Its partners are all the
threads bound to a cpu of the same cpt. Additionnaly this policy take
care of NUMA binding.
