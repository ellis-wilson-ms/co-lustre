--- Commit Message ---
LU-19173 llite: remove inode_attach_wb() in ->write_end()

__inode_attach_wb() symbol is not exported any longer since Linux
commit v6.12-rc1-25-g8182a8b39aa2:
writeback: wbc_attach_fdatawrite_inode out of line

Remove it in ->ll_write_end(), use __mark_inode_dirty() instead to
ensure that the inode is associated and attached with a @wb.
