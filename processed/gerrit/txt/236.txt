--- Message (Fan Yong, 2011-10-10 07:56:48.000000000) ---
Patch Set 2:

> Patch Set 2:
> why would we need to transfer this information among MDS stack? AFAIU, all we need is to scan local objects and rebuild OI. all this can be done internally within OSD.

We plan to support online OI Scrub, which allows client to perform lookup-by-name as normal during the OI file rebuilding, which requires the lookup-by-name sponsor can verify whether the OI file is valid or not by itself. Currently, the 'lookup-by-name' on MDS is split into as two steps: the first step is 'name-to-FID', the second one is 'FID-to-inode'. What we want to verify is in the second step, but we need the information (ino/gen) from the first step. That is why we want to transfer these information among MDS stack. But on the other hand, I am also thinking whether we can verify the OI consistency at the first step, if yes, then need transfer nothing among MDS stack, that is why I say "(if needed)" in last comment, not make decision yet.
