LU-3767 ldiskfs: "force_over_24tb" mount option to "64tb"

Change the "force_over_24tb" mount option to
"force_over_64tb" and rename the ext4-force_over_24tb-*.patch
to ext4-force_over_64tb-*.patch after testing and validating
the 64TB LUN.

The maximum LUN size for b1_8 was increased from 16TB to
24TB in http://review.whamcloud.com/#/c/589/. This patch tries
to increase the max LUN size to 64TB.

--- Message (Andreas Dilger, 2013-08-27 23:57:05.000000000) ---
Patch Set 2: I would prefer that you didn't submit this

To be honest, I'd prefer that this be capped at 32TB, which is a reasonable upper bound for 4TB drives in a RAID-6 8+2 system.  There are just too many potential problems with RHEL5 kernels that I don't think it is wise to allow new large systems to be installed with larger LUNs in the future.

If users have storage systems over 32TB at some point in the future they should really be using Lustre 2.1 or 2.4.
