LU-12756 lnet: Take peer NI ref in lnet_discover_peer_locked

lnet_discover_peer_locked() requires that callers have a reference
on the lnet_peer_ni object that is being discovered. Since
lnet_discover_peer_locked() is responsible for releasing and
re-taking the net lock, it should also be responsible for managing
this reference.
