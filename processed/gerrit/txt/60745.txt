LU-19261 lnet: Fix ptl_yaml_route_display reply parsing

The netlink reply for dumping the route config consists of a sequence
of mappings. Each mapping correlates with a single route config, so
ptl_yaml_route_display() needs to print the route config when it
sees the YAML_MAPPING_END_EVENT rather than the
YAML_SEQUENCE_END_EVENT. This fixes a bug that resulted in only a
single route being displayed by the lctl show_route command even when
multiple routes were configured.

Have sanity-lnet test_111 check the output of lctl show_route in
addition to lnetctl route show.
