LU-12669 tests: add EC recovery tests

Add EC recovery tests covering different configurations,
I/O patterns, and file sizes.

Tests added:
- test_40b-d: Recovery with 2+1, 2+2, 4+1 EC configs
- test_41a-e: Partial reads, spanning raid sets,
  different offsets, mmap, Direct I/O with recovery
- test_42a-d: Recovery with files smaller than stripe
  size, partial stripes, incomplete raid sets, and
  PFL component boundaries

Known failures (added to ALWAYS_EXCEPT):
- test_41c: EC recovery fails for reads at certain
  offsets after OST failures (checksums change)
- test_41e: EC recovery fails for Direct I/O reads
  after OST failures (returns zeros)
