LU-19449 tests: survive sysfs leftovers in test cleanup

After a client is unmounted, it can take a little bit of time to clean
all sysfs entries up. So allow extra time before carrying on in
remount_client_normally(). And just ignore -ENOENT when accessing
files under sysfs in test cleanup phase.
