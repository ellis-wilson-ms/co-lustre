--- Commit Message ---
LU-10994 clio: remove cpo_own and cpo_disown

Remove the cpo_own and cpo_disown methods from struct
cl_page_operations. These methods were only implemented by the vvp
layer so they can be inlined into cl_page_own0() and
cl_page_disown(). Move most of vvp_page_discard() and all of
vvp_transient_page_discard() into cl_page_discard().

--- Message (Patrick Farrell, 2022-05-18 20:02:27.000000000) ---

Hmm.  Well, the readahead stats have to be top level (per-OSC stats would have to be aggregated to be useful), so I can't think of an obvious one.

Discards are an awkward stat, since they're saying "the page was never used", so we have to collect them when the page is removed.

So, I guess I can't think of another way.  It has to be (or at least should be) a top level stat, and it has to be generated when a page is removed.
