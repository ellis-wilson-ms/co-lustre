LU-16073 tests: double snapshot mount reproducer

In some cases lsnapshot_mount on already mounted snapshot
results in umount of snapshot and the following error
is seen:

-> lctl snapshot_mount -F testfs -n snap_test_fo
Can't mount the snapshot snap_test_fo: No such process

Add additional test to the existing sanity-lsnapshot.sh
(test_1b) to reproduce the above issue.
