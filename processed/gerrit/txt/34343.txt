LU-12021 lsom: Add an OBD_CONNECT2_LSOM connect flag

Add an OBD_CONNECT2_LSOM connect flag so that clients do not send
MDS_ATTR_LSIZE and MDS_ATTR_LBLOCKS flags to the old servers that
do not support them.
