(2 comments)

This is a good start, but as we discussed the client and MDS should skip unknown component layouts, and balance client load between replicas by using something like:

      sew = ((client_nid >> 8) + (offset >> 30) % replica_count

So that small numbers of clients are grouped together, but large numbers of clients are distributed between OSTs, and large files are also spread between OSTs. 

Ideally, clients would also prefer to read from a local OST (with the same NID).
