LU-2642 ldiskfs: handle ext4_ext_cache without ec_type member

Commit b05e6ae5 (v2.6.37) removed the ec_type member from struct
ext4_ext_cache since it can be calculated using ->ec_len or
->ec_start.

We introduce a shim to do the calculation if the member is missing.
