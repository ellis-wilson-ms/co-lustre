--- Commit Message ---
LU-0000 llite: fix IT_WRITE_LEASE intent handling crashes

Fix kernel crashes when processing IT_WRITE_LEASE intents by
adding proper support in the LMV and MDC layers.

The crashes occurred because the LMV layer didn't handle
IT_WRITE_LEASE in lmv_intent_lock() and the MDC layer didn't
set proper lock policy bits for IT_WRITE_LEASE operations.

This patch adds IT_WRITE_LEASE to the lmv_intent_lookup() path
in the LMV layer, adds an IT_WRITE_LEASE case in the
mdc_finish_intent_lock() switch statement, and sets
MDS_INODELOCK_UPDATE policy bits for IT_WRITE_LEASE operations.

These changes ensure IT_WRITE_LEASE intents are properly routed
through the intent lock infrastructure without causing kernel
panics due to unhandled intent types.
