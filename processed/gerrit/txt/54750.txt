LU-17726 hsm: hsm_control to purge specific types of actions

MDS, implement new <lctl set_param> hsm_control to purge just specific
types of actions

This adds support for the following options to hsm_control:

cancel_archives
cancel_restores
cancel_removes

It also adds sanity-hsm tests 115-117 to make sure each of the above
exclusively cancel just the type in question and the remainder are
able to successfully complete.
