--- Commit Message ---
LU-17000 lnet: Call ns_status when holding lock

lnet_ni_large_status->ns_status was accessed without
holding lock. This patch adds lnet_peer_ni_find_locked()
call to access ns_status which ensures proper locking as
done in other places of the code.
