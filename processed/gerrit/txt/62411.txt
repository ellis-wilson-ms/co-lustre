--- Commit Message ---
LU-19577 o2iblnd: don't destroy fmr pools under spinlock

Avoid use of kiblnd_destroy_fmr_pool_list under spinlock.
This destroys FMR/FRMR pools under spinlock which leads
into ib_dereg_mr() call which sleeps and causes
'BUG: scheduling while atomic' when executing rmmod.
