(1 comment)

I haven't looked at all of these in detail, but some of the tests should include i/o from two clients.  This is possible with $DIR2, which is a second client mount.

It's not already mounted in sanity-sec, looks like, so:
You can either add it to the whole suite with MOUNT_2=${MOUNT_2:-"yes"}
 at the top of the file (this is my preferred way)

Or if you look at recovery-small.sh, you can do mount_client $MOUNT2 and then unmount_client at cleanup...  But if you add it to the suite, then you don't have to worry about cleaning it up on exit, etc.  Definitely nicer, I think.
