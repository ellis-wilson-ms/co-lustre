LU-18687 compat: fix shrinker_debug_init/fini #ifdef

The #ifdef around shrinker_debug_init()/fini() in the
shrinker_debug.h header do not match the #ifdef around
those same functions in linux-prim.c. Since these conditions
can mismatch, we can see compilation errors.
