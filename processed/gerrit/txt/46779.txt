The question is who do we need to keep these old ldiskfs patch series around for?  I'd think that either users are upgrading to latest Ubuntu+Lustre versions and don't care about about old Ubuntu kernels with new Lustre versions, or they are using the old versions of Ubuntu+Lustre and don't care that new Lustre releases have old Ubuntu support.

I'm all for keeping around support for combinations that people are using, but it is just deadweight if nobody in the community is using those old versions.
