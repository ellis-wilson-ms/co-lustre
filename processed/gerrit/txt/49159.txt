LU-16314 osc: Migrate %p to %pK

Migrating to %pK which works in all the supported kernels.

A couple of passes of:

grep -rl '[^a-zA-Z%]%p[^a-zA-Z]' lustre/osc |
  xargs -i{} sed -i -E 's/([^a-zA-Z%])%p([^a-zA-Z])/\1%pK\2/g' {}

Also fixes white space around the affect code change
