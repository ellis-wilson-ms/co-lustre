> I'm working on a similar issue https://review.whamcloud.com/#/c/43589/
 > IMO, we should re-think overall llog locking, it's way too many
 > locks.

Alex, is the locking here incompatible with the locking in your patch?  I think they are both using the OSD locking, which should be consistent, as long as the two patches are not overlapping their locking. 

I agree that having fewer locks may be useful, but I don't think that you should spend a lot of time on that before we know it is a real performance bottleneck. Deleting whole llog files instead of individual records, as this patch does, will speed up llog cancellation 60000x, and that is worth a *lot* of extra locking... so getting this patch landed should take priority over lock optimizations.
