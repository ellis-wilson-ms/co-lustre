--- Commit Message ---
LU-13035 lnet: fix remote peer ni selection

When selecting the NI of a remote peer we can only round robin
over the NIs. Remote peer NIs have no credits and no health.
However, we'd like to still spread the traffic over the remote
peer NIs as much as we can.

Remote peer NIs sequence number was not being incremented and
therefore the round robin algorithm wasn't working.

Test-parameters: trivial

I think the desire of this patch, and the one from LU-12955, is that the originator of the send will choose the ultimate lpni of the destination. But the msg->msg_hdr.dest_nid is always set to final_dst_lpni->lpni_nid when we're sending the message to a router. This is the initial lpni that is looked up from the dst_nid supplied to lnet_select_pathway(), so we won't actually enforce a round-robin just by incrementing the sequence number, no?
