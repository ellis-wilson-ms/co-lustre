--- Message (Andreas Dilger, 2023-05-15 15:47:56.000000000) ---
Patch Set 3:

In terms of the fault-injection macros, they were originally developed as part of the Lustre code, then moved into LNet when it needed the same functionality, and the compat macros left in place to avoid the need for atomically changing all callers, but nobody ever got around to removing them. 

I always thought of libcfs being a genetic "libclusterfilesystem" and not "libClusterFileSystems", but I think the point is moot.  We have libcfs today that has the "source" macros, and having OBD versions is unnecessary code duplication.
