LU-17729 lnet: LNET goes down often in AKS deployments

ksocknal_handle_link_state_change and ksocknal_handle_inetaddr_change
both failed to pay attention to the index of the namespace associated
with a device.  If ksocklnd had a record of a device with a matching
index and name, it handled the link state or inet address change even
though such may not be appropriate if the real effect is occuring in a
different namespace.

This change corrects this to make sure all three match: device
namespace, device index, and device name.
