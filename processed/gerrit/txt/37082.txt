--- Commit Message ---
LU-13035 lnet: Use send_data fields to record next-hop

Utilize the existing fields in the send_data struct to record the
lnet_peer_ni and lnet_peer objects for the next-hop. That way we
don't need to pass pointers to lnet_handle_find_routed_path to
record this information.

Since we're already passing the entire send_data object, we can
also get rid of the dst_nid argument to lnet_handle_find_routed
path, since this is already recorded in the send_data object.
