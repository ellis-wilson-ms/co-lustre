--- Commit Message ---
LU-2041 osd: inconsistent OI cache

osd_it_ea_rec() can put inconsistent information in OI cache
if osd_ea_fid_get() failed, then we might get wrong ino and load
wrong inode in later call. This may cause data corruption or
other unpredictable failure.

This patch also fixed a bug in osd_iget_verify().
