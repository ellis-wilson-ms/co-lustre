LU-2177 ptlrpc: flock/posix lock cause LBUGs because of a race

The below races can happen when some threads are handling flock or
posix-lock

1) LBUG in ldlm_lock_decref_internal_nolock()
When a thread has granted ldlm_lock as PW mode and has finished ldlm
_lock_enqueue() but hasn't called ldlm_flock_completion_ast() yet, a
n evict process can set specific flags to the ldlm_lock of flock to
call ldlm_lock_decref_internal() in ldlm_flock_completion_ast().
This race causes calling ldlm_lock_decref_internal() in ldlm_flock_co
mpletion_ast() twice or multiple times but unnecessarily.

2) LBUG in ldlm_lock_destroy()
When ldlm_flock_completion_ast() has already passed the if-statement
to check LDLM_FL_FAILED but hasn't gotten lock_res_and_lock yet, an
evict process can set LDLM_FL_FAILED flag to the ldlm_lock of flock
which is being handled by this ldlm_flock_completion_ast().
Right after this, if ldlm_flock_destroy() is called from the inside
of ldlm_process_flock_lock(), it caused LBUG because no one can cal
l ldlm_lock_decref_internal() to decrease l_writers due to lock_res
_and_lock().
