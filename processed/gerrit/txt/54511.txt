--- Commit Message ---
LU-17431 nodemap: allow deleting a range from a dynamic nm

Deleting a nid range from a dynamic nodemap requires specifying the
'-d' option to 'lctl nodemap_del_range'. This in turn sends the
appropriate ioctl to the MDS or OSS device.
On kernel side, we need to allow deleting a nid range from a nodemap
even if the nodemap config file is not accessible (i.e. we are not on
the MGS). This dynamic nodemap is not stored on disk.
