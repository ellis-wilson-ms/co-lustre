--- Commit Message ---
LU-0000 osc: do not pin DIO pages

DIO is always immediately committed and so does not need to
be pinned.  The cost of pinning and unpinning every page is
non-trivial, though it only shows up in the ptlrpc threads
rather than in the user thread.
