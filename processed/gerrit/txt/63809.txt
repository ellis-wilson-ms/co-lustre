--- Commit Message ---
LU-19852 lod: raidset aware stripe allocator

Make the qos and rr stripe allocators raidset aware.

For data allocations:
For -c, require we have unique OSTs for all
data stripes.
For -C , require we have unique OSTs for
each raidset.

If there are not enough unique OST we allow to tweak the request
down to a smaller allocation if at least 75% of the requested amount
is available.

For parity allocations:
Parities can reuse OSTs selected for data stripes as long
as they are reused in a different raidset.

fixes I65ec32466 which assumed flags contain LCME_FL_PARITY
