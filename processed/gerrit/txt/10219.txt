--- Commit Message ---
LU-4416 osd-ldiskfs: hold i_mutex for truncate in osd_punch

Starting with 3.10, ext4 started enforcing the expectation that
ext4_truncate be called with i_mutex held. This patch adds the
mutex to the osd_punch so it's properly held.
