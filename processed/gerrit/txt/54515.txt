--- Commit Message ---
LU-17431 nodemap: allow setting sepol on a dynamic nodemap

Setting sepol on a dynamic nodemap requires specifying the '-d'
option to 'nodemap_set_sepol'. This in turn sends the appropriate
ioctl to the MDS or OSS device.
On kernel side, we need to allow modifying a nodemap even if the
nodemap config file is not accessible (i.e. we are not on the MGS).
This dynamic nodemap is not stored on disk.
