--- Commit Message ---
LU-19469 osc: add hole detect policy tunable

Add hole detect policy tunable osc.*.hole_detect_policy to control
the policy to detect holes and populate the client-side hole
cache.
There are four places that holes can be detected and populated:
- Read DLM extent lock request
- Read I/O RPC
- Extended truncate
- Hole punch

Test-Parameters: trivial
