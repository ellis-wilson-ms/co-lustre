My bad, I did not pay attention enough. I think it is ok to skip those 2 tests for now, as sepol is not widely used.

It is weird that patch https://review.whamcloud.com/37224 managed to pass sanity-selinux test_21a and test_21b multiple times, and now those tests are failing.

One possibility is that another patch has been landed since then, that disturbs the way we set and update sepol on nodemap ('lctl set_param -P').

Another possibility, which is more likely IMHO, is that patch https://review.whamcloud.com/37224 only went through sanity-selinux, whereas review-dne-selinux launches sanity and recovery-small *before* sanity-selinux. So we could perfectly imagine a side effect of one of the tests in sanity or recovery-small.
I will carry out investigations in that way.
