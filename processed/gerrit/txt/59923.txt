--- Commit Message ---
LU-16807 ptlrpc: fix req_history UBSAN error

"UBSAN: shift-out-of-bounds" in ptlrpc_lprocfs_svc_req_history_start()
was caused by shifting a 32-bit value by up to 64 bits, depending on
the order of the CPT table (though it would always shift > 56 today).
This was hit in sanity test_417g only on ubuntu2404.
