LU-10277 utils: 'lfs mkdir -i -1' pick the less full MDTs

If 'lfs mkdir -i -1 -c count' is specified, it will 'df' first,
and then randomly pick 'count' less full MDTs as specific MDTs.

Add sanity test 413.
