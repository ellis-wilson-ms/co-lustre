LU-14950 osc: osc_queue_sync_pages improvements

obd_dirty_pages is an atomic that is only ever accessed
under the cl_loi_list_lock, so make it non-atomic.

osc_update_pending essentially adds to an atomic and then
asserts the atomic is non-zero.  This is unlikely to catch
much of interest (though it's not totally useless).  But
this function is called under a spin lock, so we should
make it as efficient as possible.  (The relevant atomics
need to be updated together and are also read outside of
the spin lock, so we can't make them non-atomic.)

This nets us a few % improvement in aio and dio with a
small number of OSTs or stripes, reducing contention in
spin locks in osc_queue_sync_pages.
