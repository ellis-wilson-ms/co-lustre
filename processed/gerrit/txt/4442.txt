LU-2258 mount: solve races between evict and umount

There are two race conditions between evict and umount.

1) The one race is that obd_export_evict_by_xxx() can touch a
   lustre hash which has already been finalized but not unregistered yet.

2) The other race is that the class_decref() in
   lprocfs_wr_evict_client() can call lprocfs_remove() via osc_cleanup()
   while having _lprocfs_lock already. That's why this case ends up in a
   deadlock.

This patch solve these problems with the below solutions.
For 1)
- When evict processes is going on, obd_evict_inprogress will be
  incremented.
- class_cleanup() waits until obd_evict_inprogress reaches zero in order
  for evict processes not to touch the already-finalized-hash.
- obd_export_evict_by_xxx() see if obd_stopping is set  in order not to
  touch the already-finalized-hash

For 2)
- move class_incref() to just after LPROCFS_EXIT()
- move class_decref() to just before LPROCFS_ENTRY()
