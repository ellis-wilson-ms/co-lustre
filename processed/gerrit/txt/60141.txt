LU-11520 tests: add run_test_should_fail to test-framework

Adds a wrapper for run_test, and expects the test to fail.
This will allow splitting the test case and the fix into two
separate patches, and then expecting the test case to fail
(which will be considered a "PASS") and having the fix change
the run_test_should_fail to run_test to validate the fix.
