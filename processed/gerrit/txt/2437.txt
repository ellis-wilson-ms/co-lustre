--- Commit Message ---
LU-1030 osc: support to change max_pages_per_rpc

Changing max_pages_per_rpc is not easy as before any more after
osc_extent is used because each extent relies on that value to make
the boundary correctly.

In this patch, when changing max_pages_per_rpc, it's allowed for
`old' extents and `new' extents to co-exist for a short while, and
the greater one will be used to compose RPCs.
