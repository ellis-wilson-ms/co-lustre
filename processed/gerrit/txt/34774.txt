Patrcik,

I think this patch is not enough for AIO patch, we really need lockless part, I looked at original JinShan patch, and the main objection comes from preventing DIO vs non-DIO extent merge.

As my understanding we don't need queue Client lock for DIO, However, we still hold lock currently with DIO, it won't cause problem since DIO need wait IO finished and after that we release lock thus LRU lock cancel works without any problem.

However, with AIO introduced, We don't wait IO finished, and osc_lock_discard_pages()
won't work for DIO, see osc_page_init() we don't have such lock cancel logic for transient page.
