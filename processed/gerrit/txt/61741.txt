LU-19079 gss: retrieve nodemap name from downcall

In case the nodemap inferred from the client NID was not suitable, for
instance a nodemap with the gssonly_identification property set and no
explicit NID ranges, retrieve the nodemap name fetched from the sha256
in the authentication request, and embedded in the authentication
downcall data.
