LU-9602 test: wait for client processes in recovery tests

Several of the recovery test suites, such as
recovery-random-scale and recovery-mds-scale, run a workload
on clients to produce load on the file system.

All all client load processes must be killed at the end of the
test suite to ensure that the temporary directories on the
Lustre file system can be cleaned up.

Test-Parameters: fortestonly
Test-Parameters: envdefinitions="SLOW=yes" clientcount=4 osscount=2 ostcount=7 mdscount=2 mdtcount=1 austeroptions=-R failover=true iscsi=1 testlist=recovery-mds-scale
Test-Parameters: envdefinitions="SLOW=yes" clientcount=4 osscount=2 ostcount=7 mdscount=2 mdtcount=1 austeroptions=-R failover=true iscsi=1 testlist=recovery-random-scale
