LU-18771 flr: allow merge mirrored layout

This patch prepares a merge victim llapi_layout to contain all
mirrors, and mark them as stale mirrors. On MDS, LOD accepts it for
merge. After the stale mirrors have been merged, a resync will
update the content into the newly merged mirrors.
