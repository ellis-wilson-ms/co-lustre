--- Commit Message ---
LU-7915 llite: add a tunable to enable opencache

Lustre has the ability to cache opens on the client with a DLM
openlock, but this isn't done for regular opens by default
because it has extra overhead compared to uncached opens. It would
be worthwhile to implement a tunable to enable opencache on a
per-client basis and then measure the performance impact of this
tunable for normal usage.

sanity.sh test_425 is added to verify this patch.
