incorrect as pointed out by the ZFS developers. OpenZFS ticket

> Brian what am I doing wrong?

For the best performance Lustre needs to be using the dmu_*_by_dnode() interfaces.  These interfaces all take a dnode_t which already has a hold taken on it dnode_hold() until dnode_rele().  This lets us avoid a the usual lookups to find the dnode_t from the object number.  You will need to be careful never call dmu_*_by_dnode() without the dnode_t held or you'll probably crash the system.  You can build ZFS with ./configure --enable-debug to enable the ASSERTs in the code.  They should detect any misuse of these functions and assert.
