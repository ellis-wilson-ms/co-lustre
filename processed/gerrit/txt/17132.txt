--- Message (Oleg Drokin, 2015-11-14 12:58:48.000000000) ---
Patch Set 1:

Alex,it appears there is a real race, even though I am not sure the patch will help it.

See how llog_cat_new_log frees lgh_hdr and then opens up a transaction if there was not one and then later tries to do llog_init_handle.

The race is such taht if another thread somewhere finds this log handle in between those two actions and does llog_init_handle - we'll meet exactly the asertion we are seeing in this ticket.

I don't see any synchronisation for llog_init_handle calls anywhere to protect against this.
