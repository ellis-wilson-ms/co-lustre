LU-16685 osp: get statfs on max_create_count update

While setting max_create_count from 0 back to any other
new value it is important to get fresh statfs from related
OST. That drops OS_STATFS_NOPRECREATE flag in local statfs
copy which would prevent new object creations otherwise
