LU-9398 mgc: Prevent connection invalidation on mgc

This patch prevents errors when mounting more than one file system
simultaneously from a single MGS.  One mount thread creates the
initial MGC while the other waits on a mutex but after the mutex
is released another mount thread can acquire the mutex and will
invalidate the MGC import connections even though they are still
in the connecting state.  This causes an error to be returned for
the mount.  Instead make sure MGC imports in the connecting state
are not invalidated until at least one attempt has been made.
