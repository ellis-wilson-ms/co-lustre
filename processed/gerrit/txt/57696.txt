I see what the problem is. idx in the loop is not the same as idx for every NI existing. We use idx to keep track of where we are in the list of NIs are interested in. The easier solution is replace lnet_get_ni_stats() with a direct call to lnet_usr_translate_stats(). The only reason we need the idx is so lnet_get_ni_stats() looks up the NI but we already have the NI. Also lnet_usr_trasnlate_stats() doesn't need msg_stats->im_idx set.
