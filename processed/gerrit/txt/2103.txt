--- Commit Message ---
LU-1072 locking: bug in grouplock glimpse callback

When a file has more than 1 stripe, the grouplock glimpse
callback on mdt frees a grouplock temporary structure for each
stripe.
The patch replace this structure by a global one associated to
the grouplock.
