--- Commit Message ---
LU-8633 osc: check if vmpage is in use in osc_lru_shrink

lru_page_busy should always check if the vmpage is in use,
otherwise osc_lru_shrink could evict a page which is read in
by another thread handling a page fault and waiting on it
to become up to date.
Evicting such a page would cause a SIGBUS delivered to the
thread handling the page fault.
