--- Commit Message ---
LU-17431 nodemap: allow deleting a dynamic nodemap

Deleting a dynamic nodemap requires specifying the '-d' option to
'lctl nodemap_del'. This in turn sends the appropriate ioctl to
the MDS or OSS device.
On kernel side, we need to allow deleting a nodemap even if the
nodemap config file is not accessible (i.e. we are not on the MGS).
This dynamic nodemap is not stored on disk.
