LU-11544 tests: skip sanity-quota 10 for older servers

Default quota limits were added to Lustre with version
2.11.54.  sanity-quota test 10 started failing when
default quotas were implemented and the test was not
modified.

sanity-quota test 10 should be skipped when running with
Lustre server 2.11.53 or later and earlier clients.

Test-Parameters: trivial
Test-Parameters: clientversion=2.10.8 clientdistro=el7.6 testlist=sanity-quota env=ONLY=10
