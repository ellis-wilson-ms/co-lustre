I agree with Neil on this one. The code structure is pretty clearly "initialize; condition; next", avoiding the need for the "next" condition at the end of the while() loop. That isn't a huge deal here, but there are a couple of other places where there is logic like "goto end" to get the next element that can get messy. This also saves a few lines of code, which is never a bad thing.
