LU-6735 lnet: select appropriate optimization options for ko2iblnd

In order to support different IB cards in the best way we need to have
ability to select appropriate optimization options for ko2iblnd
according IB cards installed on the system.

The different defaults for different IB cards are selected by aliases
from /etc/modprobe.d/ko2iblnd.conf and customer can change them manually.

In this patch the general mechanism of selecting tunables is proposed.
If TrueScale IB cards are inserted a new optimal tunables are selected.

With proposed tunables the performance with TrueScale IB cards gets up
from 900 MB/sec to 2.2 GB/sec.

First problem is this doesn't look optional. Secondly IMNSHO you can't make a one shoe size fits all. For example our Cray systems are running OFED 3.12 stacking using the mlx5 driver which supports FMR. The MLNX 2.04 mlx5 drivers we use on our Power8 nodes don't support FMR plus most likely due to pages being 64K we have to set the map_on_demand to not over flow the pair queues.
This is not the case with our Cray OFED 3.12 stack. We have not had the opportunity to try the default mlx5 driver that comes with RHEL7 so I can't say if you need special tweaks.

In any case I added our admins since their opinions carry more weight than mine.
