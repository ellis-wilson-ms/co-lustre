--- Commit Message ---
LU-17987 ofd: always trust OST last_id

In normal precreate, the last_id on OST is always trustable because
it's maintained in memory, and it should be MDT that messes up the
last created FID in reconnect. Even if the gap is more than
OST_MAX_PRECREATE, reply the last_id to MDT to avoid leaving OST in
unavailable status.

MDT uses the last_created_fid to precreate, and for normal create, this value is maintained in memory, and it's from precreate reply, not the value in lov_objid. And the one in lov_objid is used in the first create which is used to delete orphans, and now if it's too much behind the LAST_ID on OST, the LAST_ID on OST is trusted.
