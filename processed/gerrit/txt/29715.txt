LU-10149 tests: avoid live-lock with concurrent memsets

This patch splits and delays concurrents memsets to reduce the
execution time of auto-tests using mmap_sanity, due to some
possible live-lock situation likely to occur in mmap_tst[3,4]
sub-tests.
