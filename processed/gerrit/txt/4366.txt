Should test_33a and test_33b be moved over to sanityn.sh, where they are much more likely to be run by developers?

Also, for the create_remote_dir() function, please do not use postional parameters, especially not positional parameters that depend on how many arguments are passed. Instead it would be easy to check:

        if [ "$1" = "-i" ]; then

        else

        fi
