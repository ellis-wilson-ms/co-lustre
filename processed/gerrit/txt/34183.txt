openssl/evp.h header and EVP_MD_CTX_create function, and disable
building l_getsepol in case they are missing.

I think it useful IMHO that the configure check and BuildRequires serve two different purposes.  BuildRequires enforces what we want for our RPM packages to include for end-users, while the configure check is for developer builds, or if the end-user is building and does not have all of the packages installed.
