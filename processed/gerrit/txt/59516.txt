LU-17000 utils: Fix error reported by coverity in lcfg_setparam_client

This patch fixes errors reported by coverity under
lcfg_setparam_client().

- Call globfree() to free memory allocated by glob()
- free 'bak_path' after its use
- free 'tmp_path' after its use
- free 'line' after its use
CoverityID: 457066 ("Resource leak")

- Do not call close() in case open() or mkstemp() have falied.
CoverityID: 457074 ("Argument cannot be negative")

- Check for NULL before accessing variable 'buf'
CoverityID: 457081 ("Dereference before null check")

Also add check for NULL after malloc
