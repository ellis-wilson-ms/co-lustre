LU-19161 lnet: Handle timeout correctly in lnd_nl_set

The lnd_timeout in the lnd tunable structs is meant to report to
user-space the value of the LND timeout. However, this field is
not read by the LNDs to get their timeout, so writing to this via
lnd_nl_set currently has no affect. Modify the lnd_nl_set methods to
update their actual LND timeout.
