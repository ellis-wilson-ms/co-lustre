LU-18756 mdt: check resource IDs before init_ucred in getxattr

When getting xattr on the MDT, "mdt_check_resource_ids()" should be
called before "mdt_init_ucred()" to avoid acquiring identity cache
entries for requests that will be rejected, and it ensures the
identity cache entries are correctly cleaned-up.
