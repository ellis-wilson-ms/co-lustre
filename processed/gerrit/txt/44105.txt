--- Message (Neil Brown, 2021-06-29 10:51:24.000000000) ---
Patch Set 1: Code-Review+1

Certainly an improvement, though for the two in klnds there is room for further improvement.
The callers often use sprintf to prepare the name. It would be nice to pass the format and args directly to kthread_run().
They are both very simple and could be a VARARGS macro.
In fact kthread_run is already a VARARGS macro which passes format and args through to kthread_create(), so a similar pattern could be used for these functions.

(alternately, they could be "real" functions and call __kthread_create_on_node () like kthread_create_on_node() does).
