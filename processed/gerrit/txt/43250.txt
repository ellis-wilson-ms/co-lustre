--- Commit Message ---
LU-14599 osp: limit allocation at osp_sync_process_committed

Sometimes osp cancels very large cookie list with 64K elements.
In this case osp_sync_process_committed() tries to allocate 64 pages
and uses vmalloc.
The fix limits memory allocation size to 4 page with kmalloc, and
reuse it in a loop.

>Why it is handling this with added complexity at the OSP layer instead of just limiting the maximum number of records that can be cancelled at one time in the caller (eg. 16KB/cookie size)?
osp_sync_process_commited() splice osp list with cookies, and callers rely on a full list processing. So logic at callers would be changed instead of small modification and size limiting at osp_sync_process_commited()
