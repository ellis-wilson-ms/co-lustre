--- Commit Message ---
LU-14616 readahead: fix race in ll_ra_count_get()

There are few problems in ll_ra_count_get():

1) @ret might be negative, in that case, we should
add @pages_min, rather than @pages_min - @ret.

2) use atomic_add(pages_min - ret) could be racy,
as @ra_cur_pages might have changed, we need atomic_cmpxchg()
helper to do this job.
