LU-15878 contrib: add lustre_opcode

Add a Lustre RPC opcode encoder/decoder to contrib/scripts/.

$ lustre_opcode 101
 101 LDLM_ENQUEUE
$ lustre_opcode LDLM_ENQUEUE
 101 LDLM_ENQUEUE
$ lustre_opcode enq
 101 LDLM_ENQUEUE
$ lustre_opcode GETA
  1 OST_GETATTR
 33 MDS_GETATTR
 34 MDS_GETATTR_NAME
$ lustre_opcode FLD
 900 FLD_QUERY
 901 FLD_READ
$ lustre_opcode OUT
  61 MDS_SWAP_LAYOUTS
1000 OUT_UPDATE
