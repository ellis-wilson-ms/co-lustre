--- Commit Message ---
LU-15405 tests: Imporove dom-performance IOR Test

This patch addresses two issue with present
test_IOR test under dom-performance.

1. IOR randomize access(-z) requires blocksize >
transferSize. This patch adjusts tsize accordingly

-----------IOR ERROR------------
ERROR: IOR will randomize access within a block
and repeats the same pattern for all segments,
therefore choose blocksize > transferSize, (ior.c:1485)
--------------------------------

2. Adjust iteration count of IOR to 1/4 as
higher count was making MDS run out of space

-----------IOR ERROR------------
ior ERROR: write() failed, errno 28,
No space left on device (aiori-POSIX.c:451)
--------------------------------

Test-Parameters: trivial testlist=dom-performance
