--- Commit Message ---
LU-4584 ptlrpc: force to replay open upon truncate

If a truncate occurs during Server reply for an open request, it
is likely to occur due to embedded (large!) layout. And thus, the
request must be replayed otherwise layout will be locked twice on
Server side, finally causing Client eviction since it will only
cancel once upon Blocking-AST.
