LU-6932 utils: bound check the index in llog_reader

A corrupted file can crash the llog_reader utility, if the index read
is out of bound. Add an extra check to catch that case.

Also fixed a typo, and replaced a printf with llapi_error, since
errors should go to stderr, and updated its error message with better
information.
