LU-3536 osp: cancel logs after all updates committed

Cancel all update logs after all updates has been committed

During cross-MDT operation, master MDT will execute update
first, and get the transno and logcookie of these updates and
send them to the slave MDT, so updates recorded on slave MDT
will include transno both on Master MDT and slave MDT, by which,
the slave MDT will know whether these updates have been committed on
both MDTs. If all of them have been committed, the slave MDT will

1. cancel local update log, which has been committed to disk.
2. send logcookie to the master MDT, which will cancel the update
log on the master MDT.
