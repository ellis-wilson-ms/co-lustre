LU-19501 mdd: check target in mdd_changelog_ns_store

when lfs rm_entry is being processed, target object (and it's fid)
is not defined, we can't use it. use 0-fid instead.

Otherwise mdd_object_fid() will dereference the invalid FID and crash:
