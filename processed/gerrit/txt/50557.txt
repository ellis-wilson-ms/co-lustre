LU-16721 lfs: Fix path2fid segfault

This patch fixes segfault when calling path2fid
under interactive mode.

Before Patch
============
lfs > path2fid /mnt/lustre/a
[0x200000401:0x1:0x0]
Segmentation fault

After Patch
============
$ lfs
lfs > path2fid /mnt/lustre/a
[0x200000401:0x1:0x0]

lfs > path2fid --parent /mnt/lustre/a
[0x200000007:0x1:0x0]/a
lfs >
