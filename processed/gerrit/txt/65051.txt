--- Commit Message ---
LU-19813 build: detect arch with $(uname -m) in O2IBDIR_PATH

On a ppc64le system, ${target_cpu} outputs powerpc64le
instead of ppc64le, which causes an incorrect O2IBDIR_PATH.
This patch fixes the above issue by using $(uname -m)
instead of ${target_cpu} to detect the system architecture.
