Patch Set 7:

I guess the question is whether a guaranteed LASSERT() tripping during production is better than a small chance of a lock inversion, but a customer system that keeps running?  I think printing a CERROR() in this case (of maybe CDEBUG(D_ERROR) so it isn't rate limited) referencing LU-14980 would be enough, and if the thread actually hangs then we get a bug report, but otherwise the cluster keeps running...

Optionally, these LASSERTs could be enabled during testing (e.g. if track_declares_assert=1 is set), but not during production?
