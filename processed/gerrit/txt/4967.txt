LU-2576 osc: Add timeout for debugging purposes

This change adds a 10 minute timeout to the wait event. If the process
is not woken up in 10 minutes a message is printed to the console and it
goes back to waiting indefinitely.

This is solely for debugging and not intended to be landed.
