LU-0000 mdt: fix workqueue environment initialization

Fix crash in mdt_flr_rdonly_work_fn caused by double context
initialization. lu_env_init() already calls lu_context_init()
and lu_context_enter() internally, so we shouldn't call them
again manually.

Also fix LDLM_LAST_OPC assertion in wiretest.c to account for
the new LDLM_CANCEL_LVB opcode.
