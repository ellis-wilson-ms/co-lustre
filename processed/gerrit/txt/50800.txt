In lov_layout_change() we call old_ops->llo_delete() which in
all cases call lov_layout_wait() to ensure we drain the wait
queue. This should reduce the lo_active_ios to zero before we
encounter the assert. Loking at lov_layout_wait() there is no
need for the while loop. This ensure we always call
wait_event_idle() and wait_event_idle() always waits untils the
condition is meet. Update the assert to report the lo_active_ios
value to ensure we haven't reached saturation.
