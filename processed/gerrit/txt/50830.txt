--- Commit Message ---
LU-6142 utils: use list_first/list_entry() on list heads

This patch changes
   list_entry(foo.next, ...)
to
   list_first_entry(&foo, ...)
and
   list_entry(foo.prev, ...)
to
   list_last_entry(&foo, ...)

in cases where 'foo' is a list head - not a list member.
