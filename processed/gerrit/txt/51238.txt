LU-16878 tests: use RUNAS_UID / RUNAS_GID for NRS TBF

Some of the sanityn NRS TBF test hardcode the use of uid 500 and gid
500. They are not guaranteed to exist so use RUNAS_UID and RUNAS_GID
instead.
