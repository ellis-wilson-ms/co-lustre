--- Commit Message ---
LU-19919 utils: add missing IB LND tunable handling

The work to add support of ip2nets changed how LND tunables were
handled. In the process of adding this new work infiniband support
was not included. Runnning lnetctl import for IB with LND tunables
are currently rejected. This patch restores that handling.
