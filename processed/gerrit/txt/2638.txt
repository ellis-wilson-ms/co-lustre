LU-56 lnet: SMP improvements for LNet

This patch contains:
- fine-grained locking for LNet, LNET_LOCK is replaced by two percpt
  locks: lnet_net_lock(i) and lnet_res_lock(i)
- LNet has multiple processing partitions, EQ callbacks can happen
  concurrently now.
- LNetMEAttach can new @pos = LNET_INS_LOCAL, which means ME and MD
  will be attached on local partition of current CPU.
- many code changes to support no-global-lock LNet.
- there are a few issues in this patch, they will be fixed very soon.
