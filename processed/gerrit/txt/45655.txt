--- Commit Message ---
LU-15275 lnet: Prioritize routes that are already up to date

Selecting a router that is not up to date w.r.t. discovery can
result in send delays while the router undergoes discovery. We can
potentially avoid these delays by prioritizing routers that are
already up to date at the time of route selection.

Modify lnet_compare_routes() to consider whether the associated
gateway peers are up to date.
