LU-16634 misc: standardize iocontrol param handling

Validate uarg and karg early in iocontrol processing where needed.
This needs kernel interop for 4.20+ kernels for access_ok(), but
this can be checked by #ifdef and does not need an autoconf test.

Fix incorrect definition of OBD_IOC_BARRIER to match reality.

Well, my reaction on opening it was "oh God".  It looks pretty long and disparate, but the problem with this sort of cleanup is unless you want to make 50 different patches like the kernel does (not practical with how we test), how to split it up doesn't jump out at me.

It would be ... interesting ... if we, as a project, could work out acceptable ways to enable more smaller patches, something like a way to say "don't test these intermediate ones, just test this final one".  Because fundamentally the reason we can't do lots of small patches is the testing burden - both on the hardware and on the people handling and restarting the failures.

So if you have good ideas for big splits here, that would make sense.  Otherwise ... this kind of mass cleanup can be hard to review.  So be it.
