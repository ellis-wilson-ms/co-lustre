(2 comments)

I was trying to figure out why this fix was only needed on the rhel7.6 version of the patch, and how this problem was introduced.

It looks like v7 of patch "LU-12477 ldiskfs: remove obsolete ext4 patches", based on parent v2_13_52-29-g2c0b2b7540) copied the old/broken version of the mballoc-prefetch patch.  That mballoc-prefetch was reverted in v2_13_52-30-g2c5700fcb4 and later replaced by patch v2_13_52-114-gb7cd65a3d1, that did not have these defects in the rhel7 version of the patch.  However, when "LU-12477 ldiskfs: remove obsolete ext4 patches" was rebased after b7cd65a3d1 was landed (due to the new conflict), it must have reverted the fixes.

Since this version of the patch has a few test failures already, I'm just going to update the commit message to add "trivial" and let it retest.
