--- Message (Andreas Dilger, 2025-05-17 12:39:27.000000000) ---
Patch Set 1:

No, the LNet numbers must be permanently assigned to a specific LND type. 

I think we would continue to allocate them as we have done in the past, which is to request a reservation and they are granted as needed. IIIRC we have room for 65536 different LND types, and 65536 LNet network numbers for each one, so there shouldn't be any shortage of expandability in the future.
