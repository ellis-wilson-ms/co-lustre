LU-3367 lfsck: remove objects from OST

In lfsck test, remove_objects() should remove objects from OST,
just like remove_files() removes files from MDT. And add a version
check to skip lfsck test on the servers for interop testing of
2.1.6 clients on {2.1.6-, 2.2.0+, 2.3.65-} servers.
