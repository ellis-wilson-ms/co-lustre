--- Commit Message ---
LU-4753 endianness: Fix cpu_to_leXX size mismatch

In lod_prep_md_striped_create() and lod_xattr_set_lmv we use
cpu_to_le32() to transfer __u16 values. This patch replaces
cpu_to_le32() with cpu_to_le16().
