--- Message (Andriy Skulysh, 2013-01-08 16:40:10.000000000) ---
Patch Set 3:

The error (-ERESTARTSYS) happens on a signal.
posix_lock_file_wait() can sleep due to unlock race. Previous lock was already unlocked by lustre but not by the kernel.

I prefer to change flock locking oder.
kernel lock should be taken after lustre lock (current behavior),
kernel lock should be released prior to lustre lock during unlock.

--- Message (Oleg Drokin, 2013-01-08 16:47:16.000000000) ---

Patch Set 3:
