LU-19239 tcu: timestamp repeated deletion policy for same fname

To disambiguate the files in Trash, the conflicting filenames
are disambiguated by appending a timestamp to the filename, like
$filename.2025-04-03T00:11:24, possibly adding .nsec if there is
still conflict.
The timestamp is the time point from when the file was deleted.
