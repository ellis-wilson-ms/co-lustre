LU-19262 osc: reduce grant_shrink_interval

Set the grant shrink interval proportionally to the OST size. The
minimum interval is 30 seconds for OSTs below 30GB and max is 1200 for
OSTs larger then 1200GB. This value is set only once based on the size
of OST from the 1st OST_STATFS RPC. This ensures clients are releasing
grant space more quickly for OSTs that have less free space.

Reduce the next grant shrink automatically when the FS is starting
to get full and the total available free space is low. When the OST
has less than 25 percent free space, reduce grant next shrink interval
proportional to free space so clients will release grant more quickly
and avoid out-of-space errors.

Add osc.*.grant_shrink_next to osc tunable parameters. It shows in
seconds when the next grant shrink will happen
