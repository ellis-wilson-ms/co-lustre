LU-16618 lfs: rmfid unable to handle fsname

01. Fix rmfid unable to handle fsname. For a case when
    the pwd will have the same file/folder as the fsname
    passed.

    For such case the open call succeeds instead of failing
    and therefore the conversion of the fsname to mount point
    does not happen. Failing the rmfid.

    <snip strace output>
    openat(AT_FDCWD, "lustre", O_RDONLY|O_NONBLOCK|O_NOFOLLOW) = 3
    <snip strace output>

    $ ls -1 | grep lustre | head -1
    lustre

    After Patch:
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~
    $ lfs rmfid lustre [0x200000402:0x2:0x0]
    $

    Before Patch:
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~
    $ lfs rmfid lustre [0x200000402:0x2:0x0]
    lfs rmfid: cannot remove FIDs: Inappropriate ioctl for device

    Testcase sanity/421aa Added.

Test-Parameters: trivial testlist=sanity
