> I'm okay with this, is there a reason not to require the latest?

I didn't think for 2.10 that it would be good to *require* the very latest, as that could cause problems for someone upgrading.  However, they definitely shouldn't be running something that was obsolete before 2.10 was even released.  We recommended 1.42.13.wc4 back for 2.8.0, so I don't think this will affect (m)any sites, but if they are running 1.42.7 then they really need to upgrade or risk losing data.
