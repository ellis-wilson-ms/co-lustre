LU-17242 libcfs: implement LUSTRE_TRACE

Implement the LUSTRE_TRACE macro which automatically
logs the entry and exit of a function to the Lustre
debug log. To enable this, backport the cleanup.h
header from the Linux kernel.

The legacy ENTRY/RETURN macros are inconsistently used
and prone to error; most often, developers will forget
to annotate all entry and exit points for the function.

Replace two incorrect uses of ENTRY with LUSTRE_TRACE.
