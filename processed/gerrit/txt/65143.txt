LU-20087 nodemap: fix wrong offset_start for GID mapping

In nodemap_map_id(), the NODEMAP_GID branch incorrectly uses
nm_offset_start_uid instead of nm_offset_start_gid. This causes
GID offset mapping to use the UID offset start value, resulting
in wrong GID mappings when UID and GID offsets differ.

Fixes: e3051ad0f1c8 ("LU-18109 utils: adding nodemap offset capability")
