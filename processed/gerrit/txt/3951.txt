LU-1842 mdt: add lvbo support to MDT namespace

While inodebits does not require a lvbo, quota locks managed by the
quota master will need one to manage quota allocation and index
version via a LVB. A LVB will also be needed for the layout lock
since the LOV EA will be packed in there.
