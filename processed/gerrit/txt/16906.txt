--- Commit Message ---
LU-6612 obdfilter: do not overwrite OBD_MD_FLCOOKIE flag

A (long standing!) bug has been introduced (by commit d2d56f38)
causing OBD_MD_FLCOOKIE flag to be unset in both callbacks
handling Unlink and Setattr LLOG records during MDS/OSTs
replay/recovery process.
This is causing these records to never be canceled and removed
from LLOGs (and likely to be unnecessarily be re-processed each
time), leading to concerned plain LLOGs to be kept on MDS.
