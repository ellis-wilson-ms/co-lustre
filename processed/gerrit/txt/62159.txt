LU-18449 llite: fix wrong VM_HUGEPAGE readahead check

The client should check whether the reading page is covered by a
DLM extent lock only when index of the page is not current mmap
reading one.

Otherwise, it will cause sanityn/test_16a fsx testing failure.
This patch fixes it accordingly.
