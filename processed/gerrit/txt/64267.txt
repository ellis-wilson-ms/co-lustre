LU-19487 osc: return SHRINK_STOP when scan frees nothing

Commits 109e32dc23 ("LU-19223 osc: stop after scanning")
and 9519f453f6 ("LU-19487 osc: fix shrinker loop in
osc_lru_list_shrink") fixed the inner shrinker loop but
left the outer do_shrink_slab() loop vulnerable.

do_shrink_slab() calls count_objects once, then loops
calling scan_objects with total_scan = 2 * freeable.
When drop_caches runs, invalidate_mapping_pages() may
empty our LRUs between the count and the scan.  With
empty LRUs we report nr_scanned=0, so the kernel's
total_scan -= nr_scanned makes no progress and the
loop runs indefinitely.

Return SHRINK_STOP after a full round of all OSCs
when nothing was freed.

Add obj_switches counter to osc_lru_list_shrink to
track cl_object switch frequency, which is the
expensive path with fragmented LRUs.
