--- Commit Message ---
LU-10391 mgc: gracefully ignore unrecognised recovery logs

Incoming recovery logs with unrecognised entries should not be a fatal
error.  Providing the size information looks sensible we skip the
entry and move on to the next, rather than aborting at that point.

The "datalen < 0" is unnecessary as we already check mne_length <
entry_len and datalen > entry_len.

This patch is suitable for backporting to stable releases to allow
some interoperability with newer releases that support IPv6 and other
large-nid protocols.
