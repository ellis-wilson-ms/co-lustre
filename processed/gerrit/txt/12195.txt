LU-5621 lod: Check ldo_striping_cached

Clear the ldo_striping_cached and ldo_dir_def_striping_set
for directory.

ldo_striping_cached = 1 means its stripe count and offset
in lod_object are valid. During creation, it will create
the sub-stripes according to ldo_stripenr. For other operation,
LOD will check this flag to know whether it needs to reload
the stripe information.

ldo_dir_def_striping_set =1 means the default dir stripeEA, it
can either inherit from its parent or get from the user (with
lfs setdirstripe -D). LOD will check this flag to know if it
needs to reload the default stripe information.
