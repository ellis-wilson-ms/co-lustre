> John, does is it possible to just explicitly deny renaming of the .lustre directory?

Yes. But that's not the issue.

The deadlock is very early in the sys_rename() path, before any lustre methods are called. If rename() is given two directories, it will check to see if the dentries are the same and if not it will lock d_inode->i_mutex on each dentry. OBF allows us to violate the assumption in linux that the mapping from dir-dentries to dir-inodes is 1-1. Niu and I both concluded that it cannot be fixed without a kernel patch.

I will disable the test.
