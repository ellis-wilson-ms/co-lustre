LU-12669 vvp: clear IOCB_DIRECT for EC recovery buffered read

vvp_io_ec_rd_end calls generic_file_read_iter to deliver the
reconstructed page-cache contents to userspace.  When the
outer IO was O_DIRECT, the iocb still carries IOCB_DIRECT and
generic_file_read_iter dispatches through
mapping->a_ops->direct_IO -- ll_direct_IO -- which LASSERTs
io->ci_dio_aio.  ll_file_io_generic frees the DIO aio before
restarting as CIT_EC_RD, so the LASSERT fires and the kernel
panics on every Direct I/O EC recovery (sanity-ec test_41e
crashes the VM, even before the AIO change).

Build a scratch kiocb with IOCB_DIRECT cleared, hand that to
generic_file_read_iter so the buffered read path runs, then
copy ki_pos back to the user's iocb so the caller sees the
correct post-read position.
