LU-15947 ptlrpc: Sort waiters on close_req completion

During scale testing multiple nodes were down due to spinlock
contention in wake_up_all().
Ex:
...
    __wake_up_common_lock at ffffffff810c5dc3
    __wake_up at ffffffff810c5e33
    obd_put_mod_rpc_slot at ffffffffa04f5964 [obdclass]
    ptlrpc_put_mod_rpc_slot at ffffffffa067fd34 [ptlrpc]
    mdc_close at ffffffffa08783ac [mdc]
...

This change places close_req waiters on the front of the wait
queue for LIFO processing. All other waiters are added to the
end of the wait queue for FIFO processing.

In obd_put_mod_rpc_slot() the wake_up wakes all the LIFO waiters
plus one (1) additional FIFO waiter.
