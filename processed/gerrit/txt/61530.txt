LU-14590 obdclass: remove duplicate health_check param

Remove the duplicate /sys/kernel/debug/lustre/health_check file,
since "lctl get_param health_check" is now printing duplicate
entries for this parameter.

The debugfs "health_check" file was printing which device was
unhealthy, but only 'healthy' or 'LBUG' or 'NOT HEALTHY' is used.
The unhealthy device is printed to the console/syslog for reference,
which is probably more useful than printing it to stdout.
