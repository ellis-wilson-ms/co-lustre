LU-19194 lnet: Hide read-only params for export --backup

Some LND parameters may not be tunable. In this case they should be
omitted from from lnetctl export --backup, though they may be shown
via lnetctl net show.

Modify lnd_nl_get() to take the "export_backup" flag from
lnet_net_show_dump(). This flag tells us whether we are gathering data
for lnetctl export --backup.

Some related fixes are included:

 - kfilnd's lnd_traffic_class_num parameter was missing from the key
   list. It has been added and kfilnd_nl_get() updated to retrive it.

 - The lnd tunable "timeout" is one of the read only parameters.
   Modify the lnd_nl_set() functions to perform noop if they are sent
   this key.

 - ksocklnd and kiblnd were initializing lnd_timeout to their
   respective timeout kernel parameters. This has been changed to
   their respective LND timeout functions (ksocknal_timeout() and
   kiblnd_timeout()) as these functions provide the true timeout
   value used by the LND based on the LND kernel parameter or the
   lnet_transaction_timeout and lnet_retry_count parameters.
