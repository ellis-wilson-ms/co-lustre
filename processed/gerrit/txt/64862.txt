--- Commit Message ---
LU-19768 obd: add job_stats/exports netlink

Add netlink handlers for per-client export statistics and
per-job counters. The export handler iterates connected clients
and emits NID, nodemap name, and lprocfs counter datasets. The
job_stats handler walks the job_stat RB-tree under RCU and
streams each job's counters.

A function-pointer indirection (nodemap_test_nid_fn) is used to
resolve NID-to-nodemap without creating a circular dependency
between obdclass.ko and ptlrpc.ko.
