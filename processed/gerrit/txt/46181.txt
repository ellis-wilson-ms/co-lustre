LU-13171 utils: add PIN flag for file components

Add a LCME_FL_PIN flag and "pin" option for "lfs setstripe",
"lfs getstripe", "lfs mirror split/delete", and "lfs find".  This
indicates to "lfs migrate", "lfs mirror split/delete", and space
management tools these component(s) shouldn't be removed from a file.

Access to set/clear the "pin" flag is limited to root, processes
with CAP_SYS_RESOURCE, or GID matching mdt.*.enable_pin_gid=N
(N=-1 allows all groups to set/clear this flag).
