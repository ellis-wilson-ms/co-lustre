--- Commit Message ---
LU-19180 osc: split i/o for servers without unaligned_dio

When sending RPCs to older older servers without unaligned_dio
in cases where an RPC would be large and unaligned the request
can be failed -EINVAL.

However this is sub-optimal.

Instead the error should be unique to the failure and the caller
can check for the error, -EDEADLK, and split the RPC request
performing a short i/o and re-queuing the remaining pages.
