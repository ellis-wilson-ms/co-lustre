LU-3967 lprocfs: Add history support for lprocfs_stats

This patch helps to collect the stats during some time intervals.
When enabled, it collects stats every 10 seconds and keeps the
counts during last 10 seconds, 100 seconds and 10000 seconds by
default. The time intervals can be modified when inserting module.
