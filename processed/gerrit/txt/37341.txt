--- Commit Message ---
LU-00000 ptlrpc: Avoid mutex in sec gc list

Call chains originating from lnet_finalize must not sleep.
sptlrpc_gc_del_sec is using a mutex for synchronization that
can be rewritten by including including the sec_cop_destroy_sec
call and re-working the sec_do_gc workqueue logic to avoid the
use of a mutex.

lnet_finalize
 -> spin_lock()
    lnet_eq_enqueue_event()
     -> ptlrpc_master_callback()
       -> request_out_callback()
         -> ptlrpc_req_finished()
           -> __ptlrpc_req_finished()
             -> sptlrpc_req_put_ctx()
               -> gss_sec_release_ctx_kr()
                 -> sptlrpc_sec_put
                   -> sptlrpc_gc_del_sec <- avoid mutex here
 <- spin_unlock()
