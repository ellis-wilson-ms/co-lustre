LU-4423 llite: remove 'ptlrpc_thread usage' for sai_agl_thread

Lustre has a 'struct ptlrpc_thread' which provides control functionality
wrapped around kthreads. None of the functionality used in statahead.c
requires ptlrcp_thread - it can all be done directly with kthreads.

So discard the ptlrpc_thread and just use a task_struct directly.
