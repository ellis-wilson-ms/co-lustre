LU-19361 tests: fix replay-single/61c with active hosts

While running replay-single/61c in hard failure failover mode,
the active OST host was changed after the OSS failed over to
its partner. So, the test needs to call $(osts_nodes) again
to get the active hosts afer running "fail ost1".

Test-Parameters: trivial testlist=replay-single
Test-Parameters: trivial env=FAILURE_MODE=HARD \
  clientcount=4 mdtcount=1 mdscount=2 osscount=2 \
  austeroptions=-R failover=true iscsi=1 \
  testlist=replay-single
