Patch Set 1: Code-Review+1

It's a bit sad that is several of these cases the "name" string is the result of an earlier sprintf() call.  I think it would be cleaner to call kthread_run() at the point where that first sprintf happens.
The wrapper this just increments a counter could be open-coded and discarded.

Or maybe var-args could be passed to the wrapper, but I'm not sure that is worth it.

So: I wouldn't "fix" it like this, but that patch doesn't make anything worse...
