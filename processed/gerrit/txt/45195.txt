LU-0000 llite: Remove cra_end_idx

cra_end_idx is another example of considering readahead a
trivial optimization, rather than the main way pages get in
to Lustre.

If the readahead prediction algorithm wants to ask for a
page, the lower levels of Lustre should try to provide it.
They should only say no if they are actually unable to do
so, which is already handled by verifying the existence of
an LDLM lock.

We should not stop at the end of the stripe, or the end of
the component (etc, etc) - readahead is a file level
concept, and there is absolutely no good reason to stop at
these boundaries.  The only effect is to introduce
unecessary misses and behavioral complexity.
