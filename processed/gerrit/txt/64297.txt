--- Commit Message ---
LU-19940 lfs: don't use lsa_pattern for hash

lsa_pattern is used for the pattern and for mdt hash, but overstripe
flag broke the check against the default and LOV_PATTERN_OVERSTRIPE
is treated as an lmv hash type.

Let's use separate fields for for lmv hash to avoid this issue.
