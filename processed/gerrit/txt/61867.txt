LU-19468 tests: Fix bad module load

When IPv6 is used, we need to configure LNet with large nid feature. A
simple "modprobe lustre" won't do it. For that, we use "load_modules"
that will do a proper configuration.

Test-Parameters: trivial testlist=conf-sanity \
  env=FORCE_LARGE_NID=true,ONLY="5 31"
Test-Parameters: trivial fstype=zfs testlist=conf-sanity
Test-Parameters: trivial fstype=ldiskfs testlist=conf-sanity
