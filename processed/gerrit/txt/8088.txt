--- Commit Message ---
LU-3601 Don't enqueue two locks on the same resource

Due to mechanics of ldlm internals, enqueueing two different ibits
lock on the same resource is deadlock prone.
As such change mdt_object_open_lock to not obtain openlocks at all if
the file has no objects (and as such would likely need them created).
