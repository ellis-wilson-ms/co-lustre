LU-14950 osc: No page transfer get/put for DIO

Page transfer get/put is not required for DIO pages, which
are guaranteed to have other references.

The get/put for this takes a little over 4% of the CPU time
in DIO, so it's worthwhile to remove it for DIO pages.
