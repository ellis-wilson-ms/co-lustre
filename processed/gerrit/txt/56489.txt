LU-18256 gss: support SHA2 enctypes

Introduce support for ENCTYPE_AES128_CTS_HMAC_SHA256_128 and
ENCTYPE_AES256_CTS_HMAC_SHA384_192 encryption types that are used by
GSS code for authentication context.
