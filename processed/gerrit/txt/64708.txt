--- Commit Message ---
LU-20023 lnet: set locked primary peer NI DOWN if notified

If discovery removes a locked primary peer NI,
the NI remains present, appears healthy, and so
may still be selected for TX.

Treat this as a lpni DOWN event instead.
This preserves the NI for identity while preventing
its use for transmission.
