--- Commit Message ---
LU-18687 compat: use unexported vfree_atomic in kernel

With the removal of RHEL7 support we can drop the special
vfree_atomic() implementation we have. Instead just use
cfs_kallsym_lookup to use vfree_atomic() that is available
in modern kernels.
