In fact I don't care so much about the exact details, so long as we are not too surprising. Let's support the same style as /proc/sys/lnet/debug:

 1) 'echo foo bar > file' means that only foo and bar are set.
 2) 'echo +foo bar > file' means that foo and bar are set in addition to whatever was set before.
 3) 'echo -foo bar > file' means that foo and bar are now unset but all other bits are unchanged.
