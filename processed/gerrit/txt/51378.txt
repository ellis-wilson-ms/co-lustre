LU-16918 build: Restore --disable-modules support

Support for --disable-modules was lost a while back. Other less
frequently used build combinations were also impacted.

Prefer the installed kernel headers if they suggest lustre
support is available, otherwise use the kernel headers provided
from the local libcfs tree.
