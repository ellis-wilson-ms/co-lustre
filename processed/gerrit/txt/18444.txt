LU-7579 mdd: do not mark object as an orphan early

do not set LUSTRE_ORPHAN_FL before calling __mdd_orphan_add()
as racing mdd_la_get() may set ORPHAN_OBJ causing an false
assertion in __mdd_orphan_add().
