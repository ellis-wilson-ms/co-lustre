LU-19202 llite: revert truncate_inode_pages() under inode lock

commit 7bb1e211d2 ("LU-16637 llite: tolerate fresh page cache pages
after truncate") has made it tolerate fresh pages after truncate so
we can avoid expensive locking for race cases just to make sure there
is no leftover in the cache after truncate.
