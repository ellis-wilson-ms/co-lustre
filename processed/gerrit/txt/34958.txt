LU-10674 ldlm: only check granted plain locks

In ldlm_process_plain_lock, it checks the compatibility of plain
locks on granted and waiting list, if one conflicting plain lock
(such as the EX plain lock enqueued by mgs_revoke_lock) can't be
granted because there is existing CR plain lock from some MGC and
can't be cancelled in time, it will have two conflicting plain locks
if a new CR plain lock is enqueued by another MGC, then neither of
the two conflicting plain locks will be granted.

For the lock compatibility check when enqueuing the lock firstly
(the intention is LDLM_PROCESS_ENQUEUE), it should still check the
compatibility of both the granted and the waiting locks, because
there could be another CR lock enqueue from MGC immediately after
the previous CR lock is cancelled, which causes the EX lock can't
be granted in short time (even could never be granted if there are
lots of MGC).

But for the reenqueue (the intention is LDLM_PROCESS_RESCAN), it
should only check the granted locks and the waiting locks before
itself. In ldlm_handle_conflict_lock, the lock is added to the tail
of the waiting list and ldlm_reprocess_queue is reprocessing from
the head, then it should only check the locks in granted list in
ldlm_process_plain_lock if the intention is LDLM_PROCESS_RESCAN.
