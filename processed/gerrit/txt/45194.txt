LU-0000 llite: Remove cra_contention

The concept of reducing readahead when we have *apparent*
lock contention is silly.  We already stop doing readahead
when there's no matching lock, so why should we reduce
readahead because we *think* there might be lock
contention?

This is part of the general idea that readahead is an
optional 'optimization', rather than *the main way pages
get read in to Lustre*.  If readahead wants a page, we
should let it try to get it.
