LU-5604 obd: Porting missing FAIL_IDs

"Unified request handlers" related commits (i.e. LU-3467,
LU-2145) removed assignment/conditional "if" statements
related to following macros:

- OBD_FAIL_OST_LDLM_REPLY_NET
- OBD_FAIL_LDLM_REPLY
- OBD_FAIL_OST_CREATE_NET
- OBD_FAIL_LDLM_ENQUEUE

These marcos were used by some tests and as those marcors
are removed it broke the tests. So in this patch porting
above mention macros appropriately to fix those tests.
