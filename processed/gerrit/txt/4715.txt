LU-2145 server: move mgs ptlrpc service to the unified server

Move ptlrpc service creation from mgs to the unified server.
MGS register just slice of opcode handlers upon initialization. This
is first step to moving request handling there too and unify it.

Note: mgs handlers are set to NULL in this patch and mgs_handle is
still called instead, this is temporary just to split patches into
small parts
