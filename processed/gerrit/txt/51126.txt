DIO, so we need a compat flag and a test to recognize those
servers.

Patch Set 40: Code-Review-1

(8 comments)

I recall we discussed this, but I'm not totally onboard with the implementation. I think it could be more isolated and not depend on exposing and checking the FSTYPE for every write.
