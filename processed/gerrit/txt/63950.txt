LU-12669 tests: add EC recovery stress tests

Add EC recovery tests for stale parity, concurrent
operations, and multi-mount scenarios.

- test_48a-d: Recovery with stale parity (normal
  reads, recovery, partial resync, overwrite)
- test_49a-d: Concurrent reads, writes to other

  during recovery
