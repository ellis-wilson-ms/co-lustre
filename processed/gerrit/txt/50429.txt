I now realize this patch is still not enough to solve all problems related to encrypted DoM files. Indeed, in case of attempt to read a DoM file without the encryption key, it does not prevent the associated pages from being kept in the page cache. And after inserting the encryption key, ll_dom_readpage() is not called (because the page is already available), and the page does not get decrypted.

So I am working on a fix for this patch.
