LU-15895 o2iblnd: check if noop is needed on immediate msg rx

If receiving a lot of immediate messages without having to
send anything back, peer credits may be returned too late.

To prevent the peer from running out of credits in this case,
add the check whether noop is needed to immediate rx handling.
