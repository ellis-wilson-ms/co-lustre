because of stale OI, otherwise LFSCK has no chance to
fix direntry. lookup should initiate OI scrubber and
give it a hint about FID->dnode mapping.

This should fix sanity-lfsck/23b failing with ZFS often.

> not sure I got your question.. do you ask about a similar change in osd-ldiskfs?

I mean whether you hit similar test failures against ldiskfs based system or not.


> actually what I'd very appreciate is your thought on the case in general - how LFSCK can fix dangling direntry if OSD doesn't return it?

The lfsck engine calls dt_index_operations::rec() to return the name entry and the FID, then check whether the object corresponding to the FID exists or not: if not, then it is dangling case. For such case, the LFSCK will create such object with the given FID.
