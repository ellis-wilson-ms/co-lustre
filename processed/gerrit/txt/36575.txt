> __set_page_dirty_nobuffers

In this case __set_page_dirty_nobuffers is being open coded to reduce lock contention, unfortunately account_page_dirtied going away breaks this open coding.

Possibly this type of optimization could be moved upstream with the introduction of a set_pagevec_dirty_nobuffers()?
fs/btrfs/extent_io.c has extent_range_redirty_for_io which could use such an optimization.
