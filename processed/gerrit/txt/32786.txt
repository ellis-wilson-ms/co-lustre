LU-9667 lnet: Userspace control to show lnet ni stats

Adding lustre_lnet_stats_ni() function to show
lnet NI's statistics.

Adding helper functions in liblnetconfig.c to traverse
the lnet ni sysfs structure and show their statistics.
With this change, lnetctl utility can be used to run
below example commands to show the NI stats.

lnetctl net stats
lnetctl net stats --nid <nid1>,<nid2>,<nid3>
lnetctl net stats --nid 192.168.56.[101-103]@tcp
lnetctl net stats --net tcp
