--- Commit Message ---
LU-17431 nodemap: do not revoke global lock for dyn nodemap

In case of an MGS/MDS combo, or when the MGS and the MDS run on the
same node, we need to detect if the 'lctl nodemap_xxx' operation is
carried out on a regular or dynamic nodemap. In case of a dynamic
nodemap, we should not revoke the global lock on the nodemap
configuration, otherwise it would lead to the loss of any existing
dynamic nodemaps on all servers.
