--- Commit Message ---
LU-2484 mdt: Remove unused md_stats

Do not allocate md_stats for mdt devices since they do not implement
md_ops.  Use obd and custom MDT_LPROC_XXX stats rather than ptlrpc
stats to back /proc/.../mdt/.../stats as the other target classes do.
And add a compatibility symlink from md_stats to stats.
