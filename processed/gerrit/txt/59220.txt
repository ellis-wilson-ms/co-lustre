--- Commit Message ---
LU-18175 lustre: fix shrinker debugfs support

If you have a kernel such that !HAVE_SHRINKER_ALLOC
and CONFIG_SHRINKER_DEBUG=y, the shrinker code will
not compile since "struct ll_shrinker *s" will not
be defined. Rework the allocation in ll_shrinker_create()
to fix this.
