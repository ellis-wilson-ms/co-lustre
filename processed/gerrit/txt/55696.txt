LU-18021 update: fix update_is_commited() FID lookup

Update log can have records with OST object, so update_is_commited()
should lookup for OST object FID. Otherwise this will fail the replay:

LustreError: 14032:0:(fld_handler.c:264:fld_server_lookup())
srv-lustre-MDT0000: Cannot find sequence 0x2c0000404: rc = -2
