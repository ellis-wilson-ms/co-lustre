--- Commit Message ---
LU-18763 osd: fix JBD2 credit exhaustion on sparse files

The (est <= allocated) heuristic in osd_declare_write() is flawed as
it includes metadata blocks, leading to underestimated journal credits
for sparse files. Replace it with ldiskfs_map_blocks() to accurately
detect overwrites and avoid JBD2 credit exhaustion.
