LU-14842 osd: remove small vars from osd_thread_info

Those small variables (8 or 16 bytes) defined in osd_thread_info
can't reduce the stack usage sometimes.
Furthermore, the 64-bit kernels have 16KB stack and those small
variables are not needed anymore.
