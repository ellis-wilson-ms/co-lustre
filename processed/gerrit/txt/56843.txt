--- Commit Message ---
LU-18072 ptlrpc: do not search for duplicate cancel requests

Cancel requests don't have any max inflight limitations, so
really could arrive in huge numbers and if they are also have a resent
flag, that leads to a lot of very expensive duplicates search that is
totally unneeded at the same time, so let's skip the check for cancels.

this is not a regular list, cancels are arriving to a separate portal where no other RPCs come to.

The whole situation arises only if there is a bunch of resends, this is what makes processing impossibly long, and since cancels are not rate limited like other RPCs, they are special and are disrupting the whole check - hencye why we do not want to chec kfor duplicates for cancels specifically.

And a resent cancel most ertainly is not going to update any attributes, it'd be dropped on the floor one way or the other because the previous RPC already came and killed the lock or because the generation is wrong and so on.
