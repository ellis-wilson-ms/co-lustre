--- Commit Message ---
LU-8367 osp: wait for precreate on reformatted OST

We should wait for precreate rpc to finish when we see a just
reformatted/replaced OST, otherwise the client could try
to access the object on OST before it's created.

Do not use sync_trans when recreating the objects on the
reformatted/replaced OST.

Fix detecting reformatted OST for FID_SEQ_NORMAL, for such
seqs the oid will be initialized as LUSTRE_FID_INIT_OID,
which is 1.

Definitely it is not a good idea to depend on the sleep time when running in VMs.  Your proposal to have the create block and wait on the OST to be ready is good from the point of view that this solves the problem for "real" workloads and not just the test case.  However, it is also a bit problematic, because that could also block the MDS threads during normal operations if the OST is offline.

Probably the middle ground is that if none of the OSTs have objects available, then the client could get a reply with EINPROGRESS, which would cause the client to resend the RPC 1s or more later, but without blocking an MDS thread.
