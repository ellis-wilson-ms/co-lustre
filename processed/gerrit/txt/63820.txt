LU-19841 mdt: reset md_packed after buffer shrink

due to race with a parallel mirror removal it's possible that open
can fetch a smaller LOV EA into a big (separately allocated) buffer.
do not assert in this case, rather re-allocate appropriate buffer
and copy LOVEA into a new one.
