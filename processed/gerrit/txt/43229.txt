LU-14583 llapi: handle symlinks in llapi_file_get_stripe()

In llapi_file_get_stripe(), if the IOC_MDC_GETFILESTRIPE ioctl handler
returns -ENOTTY or -ENODATA then try to resolve any symlinks in the
path and try again.
