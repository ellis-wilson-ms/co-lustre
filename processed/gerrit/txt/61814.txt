--- Commit Message ---
LU-14554 lfs: Add --force-resync option for mirror resync

It should be possible to force the resync of a mirror with:
    "lfs mirror resync --only N --force-resync <file>"
in order to resync an inconsistent component.

This patch introduces the "--force-resync" option to force
resynchronization of the specified mirror component, even
when it is not marked stale.

Test cases are updated to verify the correct behavior of
the new option.
