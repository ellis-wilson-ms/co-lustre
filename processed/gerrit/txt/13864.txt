(1 comment)

I don't think that the way this is implemented is very useful.

Firstly, while "dump_loc" seems generic, in fact it isn't a FAIL_LOC at all like "fail_loc" is, so the name is wrong.  

Secondly, if every LDEBUG() message results in a new debug log dumped in /tmp it will be almost useless since there may be hundreds of such logfiles.
