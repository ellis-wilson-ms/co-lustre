LU-14185 osp: alloc fails for OSP obj xattr cache

osp_oac_xattr_find_or_add() may get ENOMEM as it tries to allocate
over len=buf->lb_len=XATTR_SIZE_MAX, thus order:5. Use large alloc
instead.
