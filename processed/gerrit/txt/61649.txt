--- Commit Message ---
LU-18135 scripts: call ksocklnd-config with IPv6

lustre_lnet_setup_mrrouting uses ioctl to get the interfaces. But,
ioctl does not work with IPv6, so ksocklnd-config call is skipped. To
tackle that, we move the part where we find out the interfaces into
ksocklnd-config itself.
