LU-12187 lod: Add EC stripe count infrastructure

Erasure coding (EC) layouts require tracking two stripe counts:
data stripes (dstripe_count) and coding stripes (cstripe_count).
Without infrastructure to preserve these parameters through the
layout stack, EC layouts cannot be correctly configured or
maintained across layout operations.
