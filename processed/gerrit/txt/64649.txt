LU-18763 osd: fix credits calculation for overwrites

we can't rely on size=blocks*bs calculation to recognize sparse
files, but we can use brm structures to check whether specific
blocks have been allocated already.

overall stats shows that upto ~30% declarations find blocks already
allocated, thus we can save on credits. llogs is the most frequent
user of this code: osp logs, changelogs.
