--- Message (Niu Yawei, 2011-02-22 05:58:17.000000000) ---
Patch Set 2:

Again, what my concern for this patch is that the OBD_ALLOC_LARGE() is called on so many critical paths, the performance might drop silently when the allocate size exceeding the vmalloc threashold. On the other hand, without this patch, the possibility of allocation failure will be higher.

My personal opinion is that we should make sure exactly the vmalloc is only be called on non-critical path. If we have to allocate large trunk of memory on critical path, then we must face to the risk of ENOMEM failure on the system which is short of memory.

Of course, it doesn't mean that I object the OBD_ALLOC_LARGE(), It's fine to me either. :)
