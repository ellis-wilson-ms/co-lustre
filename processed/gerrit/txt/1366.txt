ORNL-29: Use bulk transfer to read IR logs

We used to read IR logs via GET_INFO RPC, this may have scalability problem
on large lustre deployment. In this patch:
  - use bulk RPC to read IR logs;
  - change the RPC name from GET_INFO to CONFIG_READ
