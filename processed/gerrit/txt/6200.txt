LU-2797 osc: GPF in osc_send_oap_rpc

Due to cl_loi_list_lock release in osc_send_oap_rpc(), this
leaves a window where lov_oinfo may be freed/poisoned when
back from both write/read steps/calls of osc_send_oap_rpc()
in osc_check_rpcs(). To avoid this, loi is unlinked from
all client_obd cl_loi_[hp_ready,ready,write_read]_list's
and all intermediate loi_list_maint() calls to relink it
have been removed.
