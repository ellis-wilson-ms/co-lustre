--- Commit Message ---
LU-18925 libcfs: prefer kmap_local_page

Prefer kmap_local* in favor of kmap and kmap_atomic in preparation
of conversion to struct folio and kmap_local_folio

Provide kmap_local_page alias to maintain mapping to
kmap_atomic use in older kernels.

Currently md_read_page [mdc_read_page] returns the acquired page
and kmap()'d. The page is to be kunmap()'d in ll_release_page.

Remove the kmap() in mdc_read_page and kunmap() in ll_release_page
and convert the intervening page_address() usage into kmap_local_page
