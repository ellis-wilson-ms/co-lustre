LU-16590 llite: Initialize ra_cur_pages

The 'ra_cur_pages' atomic, used to track currently used
pages in readahead, is not initialized.  This means it
will occasionally contain some unexpected value rather
than 0, which can break readahead.

Initialize it to 0.
