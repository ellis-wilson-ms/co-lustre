--- Commit Message ---
LU-9899 tests: rename combined_mgs_mds to combined_mgt_mdt

The test-framework script contains the routine combined_mgs_mds()
which tests if the hostname of the MGS and MDS are the same and
if the MGS and MDS device names are the same. We can have Lustre
configurations where the MGS and MDS hostnames are the same but
the devices are different which should elicit different behavior
in some of our tests. Thus, rename combined_mgs_mds() to
combined_mgt_mdt() and create a new combined_mgs_mds() that only
checks if the host names of the MGS and MDS are the same.
