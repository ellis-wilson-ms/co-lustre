LU-2675 build: Fix build issue with lustre-core.m4

With the removal of lustre/include/linux the Makefile*
were removed but reference to these files remained in
lustre-core.m4. This makes autogen.sh fail. We remove
the Makefile reference in lustre-core.m4 so autogen.sh
works again.
