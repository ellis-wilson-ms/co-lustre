--- Commit Message ---
LU-18675 llite: drop writepage() implementation

Newer kernels seems to have less reliance on requesting
freeing of specific pages. Many upstream filesystems (XFS,
NFS, and more) have dropped it by the 6.12 release.
Lustre is already flushing cached pages as fast as it can. If the mm subsystem requests that we free a specific page (or set of pages), there is very little we can do. Newer kernels have stopped requesting filesystems to do this. As a result, all upstream filesystems have dropped their writepage() implementations. Lustre should follow suit. Since the logic holds for previous kernels, we apply the change for all supported platforms.
