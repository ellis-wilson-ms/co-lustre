LU-20117 mdc: fix RMF_SWAP_LAYOUTS packing

Calling req_capsule_set_size() after ptlrpc_request_pack() does not
update buffer offsets. So writing "struct mdc_swap_layouts" in
mdc_ioc_swap_layouts() can overwrite data set by mdc_prep_elc_req().
