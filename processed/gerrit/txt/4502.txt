LU-2306 obdclass: solve an unexpected bahaviors of class_decref

- marge the two if-statemenets in class_decref() in order for
  class_decref() not to unlink the same self export multiple times
- unlink a self export and wait for releasing it in class_cleanup()
  to let class_detach() to put the last obd_refcount and to detach
  obd_device from the obd_devs list
