LU-9400 test: conf-sanity 103 remove brackets from routine

The test_renamefs routine in conf-sanity checks if
the Lustre file system being tested has a combined MGS and
MDS by calling the combined_mgs_mds routine. This routine
is not executed because it is in the "test" brackets [].

The combined_mgs_mds routine should not be called in the
brackets.

Test-Parameters: trivial combinedmdsmgs=false testlist=conf-sanity
