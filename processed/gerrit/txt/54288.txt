LU-17463 osc: put read page into unevictable list earlier

During a mlock() call, the kernel will first populate pages within
a range of address space. It will fault-in the page and then mark
the page with PG_mlocked.
However, there is a gap between fault()ing in a page and marking
it with PG_mlocked. The page may be evicted by Lustre page cache
shrinker wrongly.
To avoid this case, we put read-in page into the unevictable list
in Lustre earlier when detect that the corresponding @vma is
marked with VM_LOCKED (which means this @vma was once mlocked).

Add sanity/test_600f to verify it works as expect.
