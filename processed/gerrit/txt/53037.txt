I'm not very familiar with this code, but I guess it is important to understand what is the actual problem being fixed here?

If the client does not have enough "dirty_max_pages" to complete the write, doesn't it make sense to *throttle* the write on the client instead of continuing to send more and more RPCs to the server?

If the writes are hitting the dirty limit, it makes sense to block the writer and flush out the older pages, rather than sending many more sync write RPCs that are poorly formed and may further slow down the server performance...
