LU-12468 llite: Add writeahead tracking

It is potentially useful to track & predict writes in a
manner similar to readahead.  This can give users greater
visibility of application i/o behavior, but primarily, this
info can be used to optimize behavior in certain scenarios.

This patch is only tracking - Future patches will make use
of this information to do optimizations in appropriate
scenarios.

The particular scenarios we capture here are:
Sequential
Strided
None
