LU-15367 tests: Add seek, truncate, stat to iotrace script

Add support for parsing seek (ll_file_seek), truncate
(ll_setattr_raw), and stat (ll_getattr_dentry)
operations from iotrace logs.
Add comprehensive tests for these operations to
test_441a.
Remove automatic stat generation before mmap
operations, as explicit stats will appear in the
iotrace log if needed.
