--- Commit Message ---
LU-10938 wbc: Only request update bit on lookup with trybits

In the face of an EX lock on the child, use a trylock to avoid
preemptive cache flushing unless absolutely necessary.
So that if there is an EX lock on a client, do not disturb it
unnecessarily.

Test-Parameters: trivial
