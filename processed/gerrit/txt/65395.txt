LU-20157 tests: recovery vs IO deadlock

The patch adds recovery-small test to reproduce hang between
recovery and IO threads. A recovery thread waits at
filemap_invalidate_lock(inode->i_mapping). A IO thread
hold it and waits a recovery end and import FULL state.
So it is kind of deadlock.
