LU-2641 osd-ldiskfs: set dentry->d_sb for xattr calls

Commit 431547b3 (v2.6.32) changed the generic xattr routines to
use dentry->d_sb instead of dentry->d_inode->i_sb.

We must set it before calling into the xattr routines that may be
backed by the generic ones, like ext4.
