--- Message (Fan Yong, 2012-12-31 14:12:09.000000000) ---
Patch Set 2: (2 inline comments)

Currently, the LFSCK for linkEA is processed in MDD layer, FID-in-dirent and OI mapping are processed in OSD layer. For MDT layer, it knows nothing about the LFSCK processing. But related LDLM lock are handled in MDT layer. That means, the client may hold related LDLM locks on the ".lustre", ".lustre/obf" object when LFSCK check/repair them, but the LFSCK cannot revoke related LDLM locks, so the client cannot detect the changes caused by LFSCK. So here, we should not change OBF fid as did in my patch. I will consider other solutions.

Thanks for pointing the issues out.
