LU-12668 tests: add EC write pattern tests

Add tests 67a-71d exercising EC recovery with various
write patterns:

- test_67a-d: degraded-write and overwrite -- verify
  that writes to healthy stripes succeed and data
  integrity is maintained after OST reactivation
- test_68a-d: varied geometries -- different stripe
  counts and sizes during degraded writes
- test_69a-d: O_DIRECT writes -- direct I/O patterns
  during degraded operation
- test_70a-d: partial overwrites -- overwriting
  portions of existing EC data while degraded
- test_71a-d: batch recovery -- multiple files
  recovering simultaneously
