--- Commit Message ---
LU-7931 tests: Allow per-subtest setup/cleanup

This brings back ability to have per-subtest setup/cleanup
logic.
Most frequently used in the past as:
SETUP=setup CLEANUP=cleanup sh sanity.sh
to do unmount/remount of the whole stack after every test
to better pinpoint memory/lock leacks and other
accumulated behavior only visible at unmount.

--- Message (Andreas Dilger, 2016-03-29 21:54:08.000000000) ---
Patch Set 1:

James, ":" is the no-op operator in shell, so those lines are fine (they have existed since time immemorial).  I think they were added to avoid cases of if/else blocks that were otherwise completely empty if $SETUP or $CLEANUP were empty.

The only improvement might be to move those default values into init_testenv() instead of in each test script.
