--- Commit Message ---
LU-12040 lod: fix defect in lod_verify_v1v3

In case of replay lum->lmm_stripe_offset does not contain stripe
offset but layout generation. Taking it as ost index causes
lod_check_index_in_pool to return EINVAL and the replay to fail.

Take ost indexes from lum3->lmm_objects and check for their
presence in pool.
