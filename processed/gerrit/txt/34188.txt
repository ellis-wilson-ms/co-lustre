I'd rather avoid current situation where tests are silently skipped for *current* releases to handle the hypothetical case of interop testing with some ancient or far future release.  The "skip" was fine when this feature was first added in 2013 (commit v2_3_63_0-6-gead6f5b2b5) because interop tests with 2.1/2.2 would have failed, but I think it is more of a danger today than a benefit.
