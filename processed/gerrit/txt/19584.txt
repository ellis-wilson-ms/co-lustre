LU-7988 hsm: update many cookie status at once

Instead of calling mdt_agent_record_update, which calls
cdt_llog_process, once for every HAL, build a list of the cookies to
update with their status and call mdt_agent_record_update just once
per seconds at most.

Update mdt_agent_record_update to take a status for every cookie.

(2 comments)

You are right, there can be overflows.
Practically, it is unlikely to happen: it would require having at least INT_MAX / 24 files (~9M) in the fs and send an ARCHIVE and a CANCEL request on all of them; then, all those requests would need to arrive at the same time on the MDS so the coordinator tries to process them all at the same time, assuming its max_requests is set high enough.

However, not having to worry about this would be great. This is not the only place in the coordinator that is "vulnerable" to overflows. At the very least, some of them can be eliminated by removing the struct hsm_action_list.

The struct, which is supposed to group hsm requests, is barely useful as it is: an hal can only group requests that were emitted in the same batch and the command line interface is limited to sending at most ~100 requests at a time. Removing it would be a great cleanup for HSM.

It can always be reintroduced later with a better/safer interface that would among other things, offer protection against overflows.
