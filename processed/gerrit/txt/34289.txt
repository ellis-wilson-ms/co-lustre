Patch Set 6:

I don't see how this is a win.

The field they are stored in is "os_state" so calling them "OS_STATE_FOO" seems to make sense.
Calling them "..STATFS.." might cause confusion with the Posix statfs() system call.

The "OS" at the front appears to be short for either "OBD STATFS" or "OBD STATE", in either case, sticking "STATFS" afterwards seems weird.

I don't think the new version is "bad" particularly, but I do so think it is weird in much the same way that the original was weird.

I would probably have gone for "OBD_STATE_", but I still don't really have clear in my mind what an OBD is, so maybe that is just as weird.

What's in a name?  Thousands of hours of bikeshed!
