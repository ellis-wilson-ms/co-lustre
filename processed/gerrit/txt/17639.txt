--- Commit Message ---
LU-7566 lnet: add “quiet” parameter to parse_nidrange()

While mounting Lustre servers, cfs_parse_nidlist() outputs
bogus “can't parse nidrange” errors to the console. This
patch fixes the issue by adding a “quiet” parameter to
parse_nidrange() and also making lmd_parse_nidlist()
call cfs_parse_nidlist_quiet().
