LU-20134 tests: Wait for background processes in lockorder.sh

lockorder.sh kills createmany and statmany but does not wait for
them to exit before rm -rf. It is possible that the createmany
is still creating new files when lockdir is being removed, causing
"cannot remove '/mnt/lustre/lockdir': Directory not empty".
