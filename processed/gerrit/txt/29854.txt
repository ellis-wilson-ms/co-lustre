--- Commit Message ---
LU-10170 osp: fix the problem of missing wakeups

osp_sync_add_rec() adds the osp_device into the committed wakeup
list before inserting the actual record, this could cause a problem
that the device may be taken out of the list by a previous sync change
and then this device will never send SETATTR/UNLINK RPC to OSTs
thereafter if there is no further sync changes coming in.

This problem can be fixed by adding the device into list again after
the record is inserted.
