--- Message (Andreas Dilger, 2012-03-15 09:15:25.000000000) ---
Patch Set 1:

I think the use of LINUX_VERSION_CODE for non-Linux architectures is ancient.  I see only a handful of uses in the existing tree, and they are all in Linux-specific code (llite, lvfs) so could not possibly be in use by the MacOS port (which has its own VFS layer due to the many differences from the Linux VFS layer, and only runs on the client).

It should be fine to remove any mention of LINUX_VERSION_CODE from the darwin and liblustre directories.
