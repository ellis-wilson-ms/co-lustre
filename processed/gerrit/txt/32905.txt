If you want to submit patches that are not yet ready for review, that is fine.  Please add the keyword:

which means that Maloo will not mark +1 or -1 on the patch (so it cannot land), and it is a clue to the reviewers that this patch is a work in progress rather than something that is intended to be landed.

That said, it is still useful to review patches before they are ready to land, so that the minor issues can be fixed along the way.
