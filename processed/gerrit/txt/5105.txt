LU-2641 ldiskfs: sync_filesystem requires sb->s_umount lock

The mainline kernel code has always called get_super() before calling
into quota code, which implies that it is holding a shared lock
on sb->s_umount.

I haven't investigated whether the lack of holding sb->s_umount could
have caused problems with Lustre, but in forward-porting for the 3.0
kernel I ran across the following situation:

Commit 5af7926f (v2.6.30) introduced a WARN_ON into sync_filesystem
that checks whether it is called with sb->s_umount lock held to
ensure the read/write check doesn't race.

That wasn't important until commit ca0e05e4 (v2.6.35) introduced
ext4_quota_off, which calls sync_filesystem().

So, in order to avoid the WARN_ON (and probably actually be more
correct in quota handling), We must also take sb->s_umount before
calling ->quota_off.
