--- Commit Message ---
LU-8802 obd: add uuid and name hash tables

Currently, adding an additional OBD device is an O(n^2)
operation due to the class_name2dev() calls in
class_register_device(). class_name2dev() and
friends are converted to use rhashtables, thus performing
lookups in constant time.

This should improve mount times and OBD registration times.
The number of devices loaded in sanity/55b has been increased
to reflect this.

The locking for class_register_device() and
class_unregister_device() has been widened to account for
the new hash tables.
