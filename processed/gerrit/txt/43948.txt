LU-14743 tests: skip sanity test 429 for older servers 

sanity test 429 was added to Lustre 2.14.51.142.  All
version interoperability testing with Lustre clients
2.14.51.142 and later with Lustre servers prior to this
version will fail.  Thus, skip sanity 429 for this
combination of Lustre servers and clients.
