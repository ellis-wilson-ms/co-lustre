--- Message (Andreas Dilger, 2013-05-14 05:45:05.000000000) ---
Patch Set 11: I would prefer that you didn't submit this

I'm supportive of splitting the patch into a generic and a distro-specific chunk (we previously did this with mballoc patches before they were merged).  However, these patches should be kept together instead of pitting the "ext4-options" patch at the end if the series. That will likely result in the ext4-options patch having to be refreshed for every distro whenever an earlier patch is changed, and will not result in reduced patch maintenance.

I'd rather see  "ext4-max-dir-size" and "ext4-max-dir-size-rhel6.4" and they are kept together in the patch series.  It will also make it much more clear to the next developer that these two patches are related, which is not at all clear in the current version.

If necessary to avoid a lot of other patch updates, the "ext4-max-dir-size" patch could be moved to the end of the series.
