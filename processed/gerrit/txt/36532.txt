--- Commit Message ---
LU-12756 lnet: Drop ref on peer_ni in lnet_nid2peerni_locked()

The lnet_nid2peerni_locked() function takes a ref on the lnet_peer_ni
object that it returns, however most callers of this function do not
need this reference as they hold a net lock when referencing the
lnet_peer_ni. Remove the addref in lnet_nid2peerni_locked() and
adjust callers appropriately.
