LU-19859 scripts: Use actual bash binary

Replace #!/bin/bash with #!/usr/bin/bash in scripts to avoid conflicts with
/bin/sh on RHEL 9. Also replace #!/bin/sh with #!/usr/bin/bash in scripts
shebangs because /bin/sh is not always bash.
