LU-19758 ldiskfs: ensure s_mb_prealloc_table is freed

During umount s_mb_prealloc_table may be left allocated.

Ensure ext4_mb_release() does free s_mb_prealloc_table

This affects servers rhel9.1 through rhel10, excluding rhel9.2 and .3
Ubuntu LTS 22 and 24 are affected.
