LU-4416 osd-ldiskfs: add support for ext4_map_blocks

Linux 2.6.35 added ext4_map_blocks but kept the extent walking
interfaces around for a while. Since Linux 3.8, those interfaces
have significantly changed and the right fix is to use
ext4_map_blocks.
