LU-2468 ldlm: shrinker path should not allocate memory

Found a glitch along the ldlm_shrink path,

ldlm_cancel_lru(ns, nr, LDLM_ASYNC, LDLM_CANCEL_SHRINK)->

ldlm_bl_to_thread(ns, NULL, cancels, nr, LDLM_ASYNC)->
