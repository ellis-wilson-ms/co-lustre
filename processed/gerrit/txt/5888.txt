If a patch is failing testing, then it always makes sense to investigate the cause of the failure.  If it is a single test hitting an intermittent failure, then a quick search in Maloo will show other similar failures and verifying the cause will be quick.  If a patch is consistently failing 7 different tests, it is time to look deeper.

As to _why_ this is failing, I can't say from looking at the patch, but I might guess it is in check_and_start_recovery_timer() causing the recovery service_timer not to be increased, and somehow a structure is being freed while it is in use.
