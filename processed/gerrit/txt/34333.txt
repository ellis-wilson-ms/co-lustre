--- Commit Message ---
LU-12034 ldlm: pass env in few cases

LU-11164 patch https://review.whamcloud.com/32832 added lu_env_init()
and lu_env_fini() call in ptlrpc_set_wait() that are not needed in
some cases.  This hurt createmany open+close performance after the
patch, and ftrace profiling isolated this to somewhere in mdc_close(),
as mdt_close() did not change in speed:

    Before LU-11164:
    total: 20000 open/close in 33.23 seconds: 601.83 ops/second
    ll_file_release:8576370.0:20000:428.0
    mdc_close:8149427.0:20000:407.0
    mdt_close:2930872.0:20000:146.0

    After LU-11164:
    total: 20000 open/close in 34.55 seconds: 578.94 ops/second
    ll_file_release:9271202.0:20000:463.0
    mdc_close:8846957.0:20000:442.0
    mdt_close:2967136.0:20000:148.0

Pass env to a few LDLM methods so they can pass it along to
ptlrpc_set_wait() in order to avoid env recreation on client side.

Fixes: e02cb40761ff ("LU-11164 ldlm: pass env to lvbo methods")
