LU-19237 llite: adopt i_version use to guard directory cache

NFSv4 (knfsd) uses i_version as a way to gauge if inode
have changed in addition to ctime, since we don't support
nanosecond timestamps, a lot of activity could go in that
othewise goes unnoticed.

So to combat stale readdir cache we are adding i_version use.

This is the simplest possible implementation where we let VFS
manage i_version on regular files for us and we only do it for
directories.
Furthermore we don't actually store i_version on disk as it's
not really important to our usecase I think, all we want to
ensure is that NFS exports are fine, zero use for our own benefit.
And besides transferring i_version from server is a protocol
change and othe hassles. See some more discussion in the ticket.
