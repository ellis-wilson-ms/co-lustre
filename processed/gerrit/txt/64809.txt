--- Commit Message ---
LU-19768 obd: add health/recovery/ldlm netlink

Add new netlink handlers for health status, recovery status, and
LDLM lock statistics. Health exposes OBD memory usage and LNet
traffic counters. Recovery exposes per-target recovery state,
client counts, and replay progress. LDLM exposes per-namespace
lock statistics, LRU sizes, and contention parameters.

Also updates api-ni.c to guard lnet_counters_get_common_locked()
against use before LNet is running, and adds UAPI enum entries
for all new netlink commands.
