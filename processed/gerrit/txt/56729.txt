LU-18348 tests: skip checking sha1sums for 2.15 image

conf-sanity t32_test() does not have the codes to unlock
the fscrypt encryption files, which are included in the
2.15 image. So, let's skip checking sha1sums for it.

  env=SLOW=yes,ENABLE_QUOTA=yes,ONLY=32 \
  serverjob=lustre-master serverbuildno=4584 \
  mdscount=2 mdtcount=4 ostcount=8 \
  testlist=conf-sanity
