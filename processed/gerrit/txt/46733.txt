--- Commit Message ---
LU-15526 mdt: enable remote PDO lock

Once parent directory is located on remote MDT, enqueue two locks like
local PDO lock if it's locked in LCK_PW mode. With this change,
creating directories (either local or remote) under one directory will
hardly trigger commit-on-sharing (unless their PDO hashes equal).

Updated sanityn 33c.

Patch Set 4: Code-Review+1

The patch looks good.
But I have a concern that:
In the original implementation, we just take PW lock on remote parent directory.
After this change, we need two DLM ibit locks:
CW on the remote parent and PW PDO lock.

As PDO lock is an ibit DLM lock based on hash, the hash value may be conflict the creation from another client. This may also trigger COS.

But as you said, it may be rare.
