--- Commit Message ---
LU-000 lprocfs: remove useless init functions

Remove useless lprocfs initialization wrappers from all modules,
and instead just call the initialization functions directly.
These wrappers have been around since the beginning of lprocfs,
but serve no purpose except to obfuscate the code.
