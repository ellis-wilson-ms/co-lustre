--- Commit Message ---
LU-19498 lnet: fail gracefully on too many NIs

When too many NIs get created, i.e. in excess of
lnet_interfaces_max, that triggers an LBUG in
lnet_ping_target_update().

Add the count of local NIs and fail with an error
instead of causing a crash when the limit is about
to be exceeded.
