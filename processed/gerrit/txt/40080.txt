--- Commit Message ---
LU-12564 ptlrpc: Do not free requests under spinlock in ptlrpc_free_committed

The buffers might be allocated with valloc and need to be freed with vfree
and vfree can sleep leading to all sorts of deadlocks

ptlrpc_free_committed is now renamed to ptlrpc_prune_committed
and returns a list of requests to free from the caller once the spinlock
could be safely released.
ptlrpc_free_pruned_req_list is added to free such a chained list of requests.
