--- Commit Message ---
LU-11394 osd-zfs: rename ZFS_MAJOR et al

ZFS_MINOR, used for the ZFS minor version, conflicts with a macro
defined within zfs.

ZFS_MINOR was added to zfsonlinux by commit a6cc9756, and is used
there for the device minor number.  Rename the Lustre macro
ZFS_VER_MAJOR, and make the similar change to ZFS_MAJOR, ZFS_PATCH,
and ZFS_FIX for naming consistency.
