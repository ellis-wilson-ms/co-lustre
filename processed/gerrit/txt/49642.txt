--- Commit Message ---
LU-16478 ldlm: don't reconnect disconnected exports

eviction can race with a reconnect and this in turn can lead
to a leaked export reference prevent further umount -
mdt_obd_reconnect() grabs a reference via nodemap_add_member(),
but complimentary mdt_disconnect() is not called if the export
has been disconnected aleady.
move the check just before the call to obd_reconnect().
