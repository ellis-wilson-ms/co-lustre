LU-19768 lnet: add LNET_CMD_STATS for global traffic counters

Add a new LNET_CMD_STATS generic netlink command that exports
global LNet traffic counters (message counts, byte counts, errors,
and allocation high-water marks) via the lnet_family.

Also move the memset of lnet_counters_common out of the locked
helper into the caller, and guard against reading counters when
LNet is not running.
