LU-14055 obdclass: fill hole in struct obd_device

Performance testing showed a significant performance reduction from
adding the obd_debugfs_vars pointer field near the end of struct
obd_device.  This reduced client IOR performance by 45%, though the
specific cause is unknown.  Adding and removing only the one field
showed that it was directly the cause of the performance regression.

Rearrange a few fields in struct obd_device to fill two 4-byte holes
and avoid the increase in structure size caused by the new pointer.
