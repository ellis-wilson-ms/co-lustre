--- Commit Message ---
LU-14428 libcfs: discard cfs_trace_copyin_string()

Instead of cfs_trace_copyin_string(), use memdup_user_nul().
This combines the allocation with the copyin, and nul-terminates.

The resulting code is a lot simpler.
