LU-17335 mgs: new option "lctl barrier_freeze --nomgs"

This patch is to add an option "--nomgs" to "lctl barrier_freeze"
to freeze all of the storage targets, except the MGT device.
Also, conf-sanity.sh test_46c is added to verify if this option
can work for online MDT and OST addition correctly.

Test-Parameters: testlist=conf-sanity env=ONLY="46c" mdtcount=4 mdscount=2 combinedmdsmgs=false standalonemgs=true
