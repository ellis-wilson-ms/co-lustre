LU-13814 osc: remove cl_page from transfer_page_init

Since DIO won't have cl_page, transfer_page_init can't use
cl_page.  Removing it is easy enough.  This patch leaves in
cp_type in cl_page, because removing that will come once
we've actually eliminated usage of cl_page for DIO.

So this results in some duplication, but only as an
intermediate step.
