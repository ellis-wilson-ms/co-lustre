LU-9576 osp: skip llog_add error

Before osp_sync thread start, we add llog record with generation
mark. It is used for proceesing unlink records. We always have a
records to process until it. If this record is not added, for
example no free slot at catalog, it`s not a critical error.

The patch introduces the defferd LLOG_GEN_REC if ENOSPC happen
at osp_sync_init. In this case, osp_sync thread started and
trys to send/cancel unlinks. And when llog has space we add
generation mark.

The patch adds sanity 263 test "check mds mount with full catalog"
Simulate ENOSPC during llog_add for osp_sync_init.
