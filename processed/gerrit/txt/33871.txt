--- Commit Message ---
LU-9846 lod: Raise stripe count limit to 10K

The overstriping feature makes it reasonable to use much
larger stripe counts than with a 1-stripe-per-OST limit.

So, this patch raises the stripe count limit to 10K.  This
value was chosen because it's large enough for expected
next gen systems, but small enough that it still works on
current-gen clients without additional fixes.
