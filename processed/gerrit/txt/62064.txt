--- Commit Message ---
LU-19525 lod: prevent stranding parity mirrors on deletion

Erasure coding parity mirrors require a corresponding data mirror
with matching extents. Prevent deletion of a data mirror if it
would leave a parity mirror without any matching data mirror.
(A "parity mirror" is any mirror containing parity components.)

Parity mirrors can always be deleted as they can never be un-stale
unless their matching data component is ALSO un-stale.
