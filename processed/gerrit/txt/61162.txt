--- Commit Message ---
LU-19068 quota: truncated time should be kept during race

During waiting to refresh the quota usage in "qsd_upd_thread"
after some big files are deleted, the final time to wait
(lqe_truncated_time) should be kept when the lqe is added to
the queue for defer adjust.

The patch adds a fix in the test_96 to wait the deletion to be
completed to ensure the quota usage to be refreshed in time.

The patch also adds some debug output about the delayed refresh.

Test-Parameters: ostcount=2 ostsizegb=5 mdtfilesystemtype=ldiskfs ostfilesystemtype=ldiskfs testlist=sanity-quota env=ONLY=96,ONLY_REPEAT=30
