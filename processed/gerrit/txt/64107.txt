LU-18786 tests: fix quota test_90a/90b race

The check_quota_no_mount and check_quota_two_mounts
helpers compare exact output from two separate lfs
quota calls. When the -a flag lists all users, root's
kbytes-used and files-used counters can change between
calls, causing a spurious mismatch.

Add a normalize_quota helper that masks volatile usage
columns (kbytes used, files used) before comparing.
The exact match is still tried first; normalization is
only used as a fallback when the raw comparison fails.
