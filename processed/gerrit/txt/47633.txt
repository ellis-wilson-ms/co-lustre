--- Commit Message ---
LU-15946 llite: restrict DIO to a multiple of pages

During scale testing variations on the following:
   ASSERTION( ... (ergo(i == 0, poff + pg->count == PAGE_SIZE)
   ... failed: i: 0/2 pg: 00000000295a3943 off: 0, count: 3928
was observed.

This reverts a change to allow page aligned DIO that is
not page sized.

lfs migrate will temporarily drop O_DIRECT for I/O
that is not a multiple of pages.
