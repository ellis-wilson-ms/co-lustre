LUG 2012 was held at the Hyatt Regency Austin, Downtown on the Lake, Monday, April 23rd through Wednesday, April 25th, 2012.

This 2-1/2 day event was this year's primary venue for discussion and seminars on open source parallel file system technologies with a unique focus on the Lustre parallel file system.

LUG is a user-led event whose organizing committee is made up of representatives from Indiana University (IU), Naval Research Laboratory (NRL), Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL), Sandia National Laboratories (SNL), Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory (LLNL), and Texas Advanced Computing Center (TACC).
