Mailing Lists
General
-  lustre-announce (http://lists.lustre.org/listinfo.cgi/lustre-announce-lustre.org) A low traffic announcements-only mailing list.  Keep up to date with the latest Lustre news and events.
  -  lustre-announce archives (http://lists.lustre.org/pipermail/lustre-announce-lustre.org/)
-  lustre-discuss (http://lists.lustre.org/listinfo.cgi/lustre-discuss-lustre.org) The main mailing list for Lustre users and administrators. Discuss all aspects of designing, installing, configuring, using, and maintaining a Lustre filesystem.
  -  lustre-discuss archives (http://lists.lustre.org/pipermail/lustre-discuss-lustre.org/)
-  lustre-devel (http://lists.lustre.org/listinfo.cgi/lustre-devel-lustre.org) The primary mailing list for discussing Lustre software development.
  -  lustre-devel archives (http://lists.lustre.org/pipermail/lustre-devel-lustre.org/)

Working Groups
-  lwg (http://lists.opensfs.org/listinfo.cgi/lwg-opensfs.org) The OpenSFS Lustre Working Group (http://wiki.opensfs.org/Lustre_Working_Group) (LWG) is the place the where the participants of OpenSFS come together to coordinate their software development efforts for the Lustre high-performance, Open Source, parallel filesystem.
-  dwg (http://lists.opensfs.org/listinfo.cgi/dwg-opensfs.org) The OpenSFS Documentation Working Group (https://wiki.opensfs.org/Documentation_Working_Group) (DWG) allows collaboration to improve the Lustre Operations Manual (https://doc.lustre.org/lustre_manual.xhtml) and other Lustre-related documentation (this wiki, man pages, etc.) to help new and experienced Lustre users find the information they need.
-  lustre-org (http://lists.opensfs.org/listinfo.cgi/lustre.org-opensfs.org) The Lustre.org Working Group (http://wiki.lustre.org/Lustre.org_Working_Group) (LOWG) manages the lustre.org web domain and its associated content.
