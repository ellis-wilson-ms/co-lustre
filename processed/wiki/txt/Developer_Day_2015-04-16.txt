OpenSFS hosted a Lustre Developer Day in Denver, Colorado on April 16, 2015.  The meeting was held the day after LUG 2015 concluded.

The agenda was as follows:

-  Patchless ldiskfs servers - Andreas Dilger
-  Lock Ahead and Strided Locking – Patrick Farrell
-  Security – Stephen Simms
-  Kerberos – Sebastien Buisson
-  ZFS and Lustre – Brian Behlendorf
-  Low level MDT scans – Henri Doreau
-  Move lustre tests to external repository – Elena Grayznova
