LUG 2021 (https://www.rc.ufl.edu/lug-2021-agenda/) was to be held in Gainesville, FL on May 19th-20th at the University of Florida Research Computing (https://www.rc.ufl.edu/).

The event was instead held virtually due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic.
