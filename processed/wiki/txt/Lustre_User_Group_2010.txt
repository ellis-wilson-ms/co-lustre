Thanks to all of the attendees and presenters at the 2010 Lustre User Group (LUG). This year's event was hosted at the beautiful Seascape Resort in Monterey Bay, California, and featured a Lustre Advanced User seminar and two days of informative presentations on select Lustre features, upcoming enhancements, site-specific experiences using Lustre.
