LUG 2019 was held in Houston, Texas from May 14th-19th, 2019 at the University of Houston (https://conference.cacds.uh.edu/). For the first time this year, the agenda included two open tutorial sessions on the opening day for new and experienced users and administrators to learn about Lustre.  Additionally, the Lustre Developer Day 2019 was held on May 14th.
