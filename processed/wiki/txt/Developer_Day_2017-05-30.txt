OpenSFS hosted a Lustre Developer Day in Bloomington, Indiana on May 30th, 2017, along with the first ever Lustre Hackathon.  The meeting was held the day before Lustre User Group 2017 began.

The agenda was as follows:

-   Developer Day Welcome and Logistics - Peter Jones, Intel
-   Intel Development Update - Andreas Dilger, Intel
-   Tracepoints - James Simmons, ORNL
-   Lustre QOS - Li Xi, DDN
-   Lustre Security - Sebastien Buisson, DDN
-   MMP for Lustre on ZFS - Olaf Faaland, LLNL
-   OpenZFS Developments Impacting Lustre - Brian Behlendorf, LLNL
-   Upstream Lustre Client - James Simmons, ORNL
-   Fujitsu Development Update - Shinji Sumimoto, Fujitsu
-   Developer Day Wrap up - Peter Jones, Intel
-   Hackathon Project Ideas - Andreas Dilger, Intel
