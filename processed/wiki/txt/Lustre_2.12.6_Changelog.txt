- RHEL 7.9 (3.10.0-1160.2.1.el7)

- RHEL 7.9 (3.10.0-1160.2.1.el7)
- RHEL 8.3 (4.18.0-240.1.1.el8_3)
- SLES12 SP5 (4.12.14-122.20.1)
- Ubuntu 18.04 (4.15.0-72)

Recommended e2fsprogs version: 

- v1.45.6.wc2

Interoperability Support:
