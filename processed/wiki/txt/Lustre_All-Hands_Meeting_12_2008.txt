Once a year, the Lustre™ Engineering team gathers to discuss new features under development and testing efforts. This week-long event is known as the Lustre all-hands meeting. The Development presentations made at the December 2008 all-hands meeting are available here:

-  Simplified Interoperability Recovery - Huang Hua
-  Recovery Overview - Robert Read
-  Quotas-TOI - Yong Fan
-  Quality Initiative Talk - Robert Read
-  OST Pools - Nathan Rutman
-  OST Migration RAID1 SNS - Andreas Dilger
-  Lustre NRS Simulation - Yingjin Qian, Wang Di
-  Lustre Interoperability 1.8 - Huang Hua
-  HDFS - Wang Di
-  GIT Overview - Robert Read
-  COS-TOI - Alexander Zarochentsev
-  CLIO-TOI - Nikita Danilov
-  CLIO-TOI-notes - Nikita Danilov
-  CLIO - Nikita Danilov
