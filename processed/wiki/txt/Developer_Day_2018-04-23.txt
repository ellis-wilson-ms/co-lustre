OpenSFS hosted a Lustre Developer Day in Chicago, IL on April 23rd, 2018. The meeting was held the day before Lustre User Group 2018 began.

The agenda was as follows:

-  Developer Day Welcome and Logistics - Peter Jones, Intel
-  Developer Day - FLR Improvements/Erasure Coding - Andreas Dilger, Intel
-  Persistent Client Cache - Li Xi, DDN
-  Lazy Size on MDT - Dongyang Li, Li Xi, DDN
-  T10-PI - Li Xi, DDN
-  Client Writeback Cache - Oleg Drokin, Intel
 
Lunch 12:30-1:30
 
Afternoon (1:30 start, 30 mins break either in one block or two smaller blocks)
-  Upstream Kernel Client - James Simmons, ORNL
-  PFL Self-extending Layouts - Patrick Farrell, Cray
-  HSM Improvements - John Hammond, Intel
