--- Description ---
lfs df -h report this flag on the output of a lustre 2.10.5 client.

 

scratch-OST0008_UUID 356.7T 1.8M 353.1T 0% /scratch[OST:8] R

On the commit 95d7592a9f33f62accade96d81e0cc3ca0fb94e2 we can see it is related to OS_STATE for LU-8152.

D stands for OS_STATE_DEGRADED

 R stands for OS_STATE_READONLY

 S stands for OS_STATE_ENOSPC

 I stands for OS_STATE_ENOINO

It could be great that this info was available in the lustre manual. maybe in this section :

19.6.1. Checking File System Free Space
