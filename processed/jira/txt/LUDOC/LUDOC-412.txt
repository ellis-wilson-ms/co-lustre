--- Comment (adilger, Sun, 26 Aug 2018 03:50:48 +0000) ---
There is not really an upper size limit for ZFS, but this is the recommended maximum for management and performance reasons. The ZFS filesystems could be larger if there is not a need for high performance, just capacity, but we have never tested such large filesystems ourselves, nor do we know of any sites using larger ZFS filesystems.
