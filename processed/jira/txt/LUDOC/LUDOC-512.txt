The Lustre Operations Manual does not document the append_pool or append_stripe_count options.

adilger wrote in the Linux Lustre Client #general Slack:

note that there is a special "append" layout, see "mdd.*.append_pool" and "mdd.*.append_stripe_count", because often append files are logs and have different allocation needs, and also to avoid the "append instantiates all components" issue that is tracked under LU-12738.
