--- Description ---
Section 21.1 "Working with Quotas" of the Ops Manual begins, "Quotas allow a system administrator to limit the amount of disk space a user or group can use in a directory." [emphasis mine]

However, Lustre quotas are not directory based and this statement leads to confusion.  This minor issue can be resolved by deleting "in a directory".
