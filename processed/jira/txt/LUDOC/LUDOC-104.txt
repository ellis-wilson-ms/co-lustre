--- Description ---
The Lustre Manual is currently rendered through the vanilla style transforms for Docbook 5. This produces documents that are generic in design.

More powerful document design tools are only available using custom styling.

The current tool chain should be modified to include custom transforms and style sheets that enable access to advanced design features.
