--- Description ---
In sector  27.3.1. Add a PCC backend on a client, there is an example:

client# lctl pcc add /mnt/lustre /mnt/pcc  --param "projid={500,1000}&fname={*.h5},uid=1001 rwid=2"

It should be:

client# lctl pcc add /mnt/lustre /mnt/pcc  --param "projid={500,1000}&fname={*.h5},uid={1001} rwid=2"
