--- Description ---
Since Lustre 2.4.0, lfs quotaon and quotaoff are deprecated. Although the deprecation is well noted in the manual, this should be added to section 33.1.3 "lfs options" after quotaon and quotaoff are listed as options and, probably, the example in 33.1.4 should not use quotaon and quotaoff.
