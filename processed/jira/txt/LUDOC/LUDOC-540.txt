--- Description ---
The following pages are all over 3 years old.  These are being reviewed as part of the efforts of the Lustre Documentation Working Group.  The main intent of this bug is to evaluate if these pages require:

	Reorganization

	Removal due to staleness

	Extension due to missing material

	Correction to fix incorrect or stale material

The author is expected to annotate this work item with the changes expected.  They may or may not make those changes as part of this work item.  If they choose to not make those changes, they are expected to create a new work item where the identified, explicit improvements can be made by another individual within the LUDOC stream.  If they do choose to make those changes, please clarify such in the comment section.
