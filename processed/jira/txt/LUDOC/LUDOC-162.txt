In the June 2013 manual there is this heading:

31.2.6. Watching the OST Block I/O Stream

when referring to the brw_stats file.

Elsewhere I have seen "brw" referred to as "bulk read write" as opposed to "block read write" - I don't know the history, but presumably after going from blocks (ext3) to extents (ext4) this subtle change was introduced.  If, in fact, this file is now called "bulk read write", this and any other references to "block" should be updated.
