--- Description ---
In Section 13.11 of the Lutre manual the line is incorrect:

mds1# cat /proc/fs/lustre/mds/testfs-MDT0000/recovery_status

should be "mdt" instead of "mds"

In section 36.19.3

There is this line

    /proc/fs/lustre/mds|obdfilter/*/exports/

For the MDS this is /proc/fs/lustre/mdt/*/exports     (not "mds")

For obdfileer directory is only on the OSS.

—

also in this section:

• Improved MDT statistics

More detailed MDT operations statistics are collected for better profiling.

/proc/fs/lustre/mds/*/stats

should be "mdt" and not mds" for the directory.
