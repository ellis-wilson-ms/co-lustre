--- Description ---
Add an * after Lustre on the title page and as described below

From the Legal site page: Proper Use of Other Party Trademarks by Intel (link below):

•	Designate other party trademarks with a superscript asterisk . 

•	In the first appearance in a headline, and/or the first prominent use

•	In the first appearance in the body of text 

•	The asterisk  may go at the end of the other party's trademarked name or at the end of the product name.	Examples from the Legal site page: 

•	Microsoft Windows Vista OS*

•	Windows* Vista OS

•	HP Pavilion system*

•	Adobe Acrobat* software

These examples show various acceptable formats. The recommended format is an asterisk at the end of the product name, before the noun.

Use an appropriate noun after the trademark.	On Windows* OS; on Linux* systems
