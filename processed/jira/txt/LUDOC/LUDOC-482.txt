--- Description ---
https://review.whamcloud.com/#/c/33012/ from LU-11215 replaced "large_xattr" with "ea_inode" and the Ops Manual was updated.  However, it also made it impossible to remove ea_inode:

 Don't try to turn off "ea_inode" feature on the targets anymore,

it's not supported by upstream e2fsprogs.

And if you try you get:

> Clearing filesystem feature 'ea_inode' not supported.

There are various "Notes" in the Ops Manual that say how to turn it on.  They should be updated to indicate that it is a one-way street.
