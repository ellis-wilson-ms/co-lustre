--- Description ---
The current online HTML manual provides detailed information of the packages should be installed to servers and clients.  However, the current text seems to read "The required packages for Lustre 2.9 EL7 servers are listed in the table below...".  Rather than having the manual attempt to capture this dynamic information for one release, perhaps the details of a specific release could be removed, and in place there could instead be a hyperlink to the Wiki (perhaps to the "Lustre Releases" page at: https://wiki.whamcloud.com/display/PUB/Lustre+Releases ) where there is room to include the information for all the unique Lustre releases in a static context.  Just a thought...
