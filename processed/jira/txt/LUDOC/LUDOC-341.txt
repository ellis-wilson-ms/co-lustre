--- Description ---
'lfs poollist' no longer works in master (2.8.53) and was replaced by 'lfs pool_list'. Thus, the Lustre manual should be updated to reflect this change. There are four references to 'poolist' in the manual:

32.1.1. Synopsis

32.1.3. Options

32.1.4. Examples (twice)

Also see LUDOC-59 and LU-1364 for thoughts on which form of pool list to use.
