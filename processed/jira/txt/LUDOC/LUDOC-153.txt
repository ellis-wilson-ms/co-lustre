--- Description ---
If one or more OSTs are active yet unavailable, standard 'df' will hang unless the lazystatfs option is specified.  [Additionally, 'lfs df' can be used instead.]

The option should be documented in the Ops Manual.  It should be noted that the reported information will be inaccurate (and that there is no way to tell from 'df' that that is the case).
