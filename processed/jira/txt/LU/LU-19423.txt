--- Description ---
Currently changelog garbage collection could be optimized further, one significant change would be to begin regularly warning in system / console logging that changelogs have hit certain thresholds (e.g. changelog using 20% MDT free space usage, 22%, 24%, ...) to allow the administrators to easily know from existing logging that the garbage collection process may begin soon and or that there are consumers which are lagging or not consuming.
