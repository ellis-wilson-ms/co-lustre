--- Description ---
The issue was found during work around the problem described in LU-8367.

When exist several reserved objects(i.e. opd_pre_reserved > 0) osp_precreate_cleanup_orphans is blocked until opd_pre_reserved becomes 0.

Thus we need to wake up osp_precreate_cleanup_orphans  when opd_pre_reserved becomes 0.
