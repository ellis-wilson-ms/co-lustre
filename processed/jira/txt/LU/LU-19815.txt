--- Description ---
I am working on an application that reads changelogs and wants to correlate changelog records with filesystem objects by comparing the FID (present in changelog) to the client-side inode number.

It looks like fid_flatten64() is the function that does the transformation of FID to inode number, but I can't call it because it's defined in a private header.

Is there any willingness to expose these functions in the liblustre API? Maybe with new llapi_fid_flatten*() wrappers?
