--- Comment (heckes, Thu, 17 Mar 2016 15:02:06 +0000) ---
Soak has been continued to execute b2_8 RC5 build with reformatted Lustre FS.

Now there's only 1 MDT per MDS and 5 OSTs per OSS (unchanged). MDT had

been formatted with ldiskfs and OSTs using zfs.

	Recovery process always completes successful for  'mds_restarts' (no client is evicted)

	For mds_failover(+ interrupt recovery on secondary node by umounting immediately again)

    the recovery is only partially successful for a subset of the clients, i.e. clients will always be evicted

	For mds_failover(+ wait for recovery to complete on secondary before failback)

   the recovery process is always successful on the secondary and the primary node.

The attached file recovery-times-20160317 shows the events for the three 'categories' above

	All events between 2016-03-16 08:45  — 2016-03-17 04:38  were executed with 'mds_restart', 'mds_failover + immediate failback'

	All events between 2016-03-17 04:38  — 2016-03-17 07:25  were executed with 'mds_restart', 'mds_failover + wait for recovery'
