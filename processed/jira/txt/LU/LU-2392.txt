--- Environment ---
Linux distributions running Kernel >= 2.6.32

--- Description ---
Kerberos support for lustre employs the GSS framework and the keyring mechanism (gss_keyring.c) to maintain and cache cryptographic keys in the Kernel. Due to major structural changes between 2.6.x and 2.6.32 the support is broken.
