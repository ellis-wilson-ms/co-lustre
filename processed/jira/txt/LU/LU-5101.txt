The e2fsck will put the orphans under /lost+found that is invisible to the client. The namespace LFSCK should move the orphans to normal namespace according to the linkEA. If the orphan cannot be recover, such as because of its parent is lost, it should be moved to /ROOT/.lustre/lost+found/MDTXXXX/,  then the users can operate them via common system tools.
