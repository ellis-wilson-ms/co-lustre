--- Description ---
All *_seq_write functions in lustre/osd-ldiskfs/osd_lproc.c declare

buffesr as "const char *buffer" and should use "const char __user *buffer" instead.

This includes:

osd_brw_stats_seq_write

ldiskfs_osd_cache_seq_write

ldiskfs_osd_wcache_seq_write

lprocfs_osd_force_sync_seq_write

ldiskfs_osd_pdo_seq_write

ldiskfs_osd_auto_scrub_seq_write

ldiskfs_osd_full_scrub_ratio_seq_write

ldiskfs_osd_full_scrub_threshold_rate_seq_write

ldiskfs_osd_track_declares_assert_seq_write

ldiskfs_osd_readcache_seq_write

ldiskfs_osd_index_in_idif_seq_write
