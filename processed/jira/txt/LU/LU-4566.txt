--- Comment (cliffw, Wed, 5 Feb 2014 23:43:29 +0000) ---
There should be no issues during a downgrade, as you are moving only two minor releases. There are no changes to the Lustre configuration, only versions of the RPMs change. For downgrade, you would simply re-install the previous set of RPMS. Also, there are no issues in this case with a mixed environment, so you can do a rolling upgrade or a rolling downgrade. The cluster and clients should work just fine with any mix of 1.8.7-wc1 and 1.8.9-wc1.
