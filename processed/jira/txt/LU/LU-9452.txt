--- Comment (adilger, Fri, 5 May 2017 23:40:13 +0000) ---
Unlike the OBD_FAIL_TIMEOUT(OBD_FAIL_LDLM_AGL_DELAY, 5) call slightly earlier in ofd_intent_policy(), this call to OBD_FAIL_TIMEOUT(OBD_FAIL_LDLM_GLIMPSE, 2) is done after lock_res(res), so the thread is holding a spinlock.  I believe that Oleg's test environment is using a debug-enabled kernel, so it BUGs when a sleeping function is called while holding a spinlock.

It looks like this is just a holdover from liblustre days, and the test no longer makes sense.
