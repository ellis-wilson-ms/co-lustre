--- Description ---
We seriously need to take measures to fix the jira spam from Build Master.  It is quite disruptive to have a nice conversation going in a jira ticket only to have it interrupted by 20 entries from Build Master with most repeated information.  So I page-down like a maniac only to later find that I skipped over an important little comment smack dab in the middle of two big Build Master spam sessions.

A single message consisting of one line (a few lines at most) with a URL to a summary page would be acceptable.
