--- Description ---
Currently In striped directory creation, if MDT of stripes are not specified, the directory will created on the MDT where its parent is, and the first stripe will be located on the same MDT, and following stripes will be located on the following MDTs by MDT index. To rebalance MDTs usage, the following stripes should be located on the MDTs which has more spare space and inodes.
