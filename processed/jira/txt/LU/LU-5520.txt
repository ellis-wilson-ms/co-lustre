--- Description ---
While clients will resend client->server RPCs, servers would not resend server->client RPCs such as LDLM callbacks (blocking or completion callbacks/ASTs).  This could result in clients being evicted from the server if blocking callbacks were dropped by the network (a failed router or lossy network) and the client did not cancel the requested lock in time.
