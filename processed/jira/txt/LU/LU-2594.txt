--- Description ---
We would like to add additional nids to our OSS and MDS with out taking down the filesystem. Can we add nids and have them usable by new clients?

--- Comment (cliffw, Fri, 15 Feb 2013 17:30:36 +0000) ---
Please reopen if you have further questions

--- Comment (cliffw, Mon, 14 Jan 2013 12:10:52 +0000) ---
You need to do the --writeconf so the servers can use the new NIDS. And the clients have to be un-mounted to do the --writeconf. See the Lustre Manual.

--- Comment (mhanafi, Thu, 10 Jan 2013 16:48:40 +0000) ---
If we are adding an additional for new clients and not changing the one for existing clients do we need to do a umount?

--- Comment (cliffw, Thu, 10 Jan 2013 15:58:06 +0000) ---
No, you will have to run --writeconf on the servers to establish the new NIDS, and this will require the clients to umount. In addition, a new MGS NID may change the clients mount command.
