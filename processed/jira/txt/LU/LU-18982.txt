--- Comment (adilger, Wed, 7 May 2025 08:08:11 +0000) ---
The lov_mds_md struct existed long before DoM was created, and this field has "come along for the ride".  It is not possible for the DoM component to be on a different MDT from the inode itself, so it doesn't make sense to allow this field to be changed.

I could imagine that this field would not be printed with "lfs getstripe" for a DoM component?

--- Comment (adilger, Tue, 6 May 2025 01:21:54 +0000) ---
The "lmm_stripe_offset" is the OST starting index of a file in the file layout, and is printed with "lfs getstripe -i" (for the last initialized component).  This is unrelated to the MDT number returned by "lfs getstripe -m".
