--- Description ---
The lsvcgssd daemon is expected to spawn a few additional threads at startup to carry out extra work. For instance with SSK, it spawns a temporary additional thread to compute the number of rounds used for Miller-Rabin prime testing. In this case finding the PID of the 'main' thread can be complicated. So do not try to record this by ourselves, and let systemctl handle that.
