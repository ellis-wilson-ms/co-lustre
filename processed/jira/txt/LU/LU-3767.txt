RHEL5.9, distribution provided ib stack

--- Description ---
I understand that ldiskfs has not been extensively tested on LUNs over 24TB with lustre 1.8.x and el5.x and the force_over_24tb option is required to format a larger LUN. We would like to take advantage of 32TB LUNS from a RAID6 with 8x4TB drives if at all possible on a 1.8 branch. Has testing on larger OSTs been undertaken since LU-136, which appeared to only test 128TB on the 2.1 code?  Are there any other issues that might prevent 32TB OSTs from working?

--- Comment (jamesanunez, Sat, 14 Sep 2013 03:53:55 +0000) ---
ORNL has decided to not test/create 32TB LUNs for use with their 1.8 file systems. Thus we are closing this ticket since the testing is no longer needed. 

Please reopen this ticket if there is a desire to put 32 TB LUNs into production.

--- Comment (jamesanunez, Fri, 16 Aug 2013 13:46:31 +0000) ---
Prior to landing http://review.whamcloud.com/#/c/589/1, format and mount of 128TB LUNs with 1.8.6 were successful using the "force_over_16tb" flag. Only a partial test, llverdev and llverfs, was run on the 128TB LUNs. Full tests were not run.

Officially, we've only tested and support up to 24TB LUNs for 1.8.x, but it looks like it just may work.
