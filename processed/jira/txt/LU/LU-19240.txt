--- Comment (tappro, Wed, 15 Oct 2025 19:03:02 +0000) ---
It is not possible to use nodemap info to get client networks for filtering. The only source of information can be request itself, e.g. rq_source. The list of further research in this area:

	check if rq_source contains real client NID in router setup too

	get LNet peer by rq_source

	get all list of all peer NIDs

	consider all NIDs attached to peer as client NIDs and use them to build networks list for filtering

by this we might get possible networks on client though I am afraid with not 100% confidence (do we need it at all?)
