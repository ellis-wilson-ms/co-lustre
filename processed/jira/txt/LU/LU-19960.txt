--- Description ---
In multi-rail configurations using both physical and VLAN interfaces, only the rails

backed by physical devices may get selected due to missing CPT locality on the VLAN NIs.

If possible, resolve VLAN devices to their underlying physical device and derive CPT

from the parent.
