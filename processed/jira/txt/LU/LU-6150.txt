--- Description ---
Gerrit 2.8.5 (current version on review.whamcloud.com) supports a search clause, "file:" that enables one to find patches that affect a specified file.

This would be useful, among other things, for finding others working in a given area, and for finding a changeset when you know the area but don't recall who was working on it or exactly when.

https://gerrit-documentation.storage.googleapis.com/Documentation/2.8.5/user-search.html

When I used it on review.whamcloud.com, it responded that a secondary index needed to be enabled.

Can you look into enabling this feature?

thanks,
