--- Environment ---
1.8.2 servers run sles10sp2 and 1.8.5 servers run centos 5.5.

--- Description ---
This is the NASA Ames installation.

An ll_ost_io thread starts consuming 100% CPU on an OSS and eventually the OSS

becomes unresponsive (can't ssh or log in via serial console).

We had to disable quota to avoid the problems. See Bugzilla 23858.

Oracle pushed a fix to its prime b1_8. We would like Whamcloud to pick up the fix.
