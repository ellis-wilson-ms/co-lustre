--- Environment ---
ldiskfs OSD

--- Description ---
After LU-7660 LOD doesn't cache default striping, so each create will fetch it from bottom, since LU-7660 enables OSP xattr caching, and ZFS OSD caches xattr by default, ldiskfs OSD should cache xattr too.

Use mdtest to compare performance between before and after.
