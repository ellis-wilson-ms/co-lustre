--- Description ---
The output in "osd-*.*.oi_scrub" and "mdd.*.lfsck_*" and "obdfilter.*.lfsck_namespace" should print the total number of objects to scan in addition to the number currently being scanned, and print an estimated time for completion based on the average rate.

While the number of object may not be wholly accurate (used inodes may increase and decrease, and no easy estimate exists for directory count) this is better than having to correlate manually with "lfs df" and/or "osd-*.*.filestotal/filesfree" and then work out the expected completion time (or date!) on a napkin.
