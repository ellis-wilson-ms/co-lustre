--- Description ---
The 

lctl set_param -P

 functionality, which landed via [LU-3155| for 2.5.0, behaves different than the 

lctl conf_param

 functionality it deprecates. According to email exchanges, 

lctl set_param -P

 is part of a design change to move away from /proc as a method of storing configuration. This issue exist to discuss continuation, alternatives, or removal of 

lctl set_param -P

.
