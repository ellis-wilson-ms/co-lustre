I don't know why this (mostly) happens in interop testing.  There is code in b_es6_0 test-framework.sh::init_compression() that may be changing PERM_CMD="$LCTL set_param -P" from the default PERM_CMD="$LCTL conf_param" (on both master and b_es6_0), but this test is also failing with master-next:b2_15 and master-next:b2_16 interop testing.

I think the right solution here is to delete any default setting for llite.*.max_read_ahead_whole_mb at the start of the test (for both set_param -P and conf_param) and remount the client before assuming that the current value is the default.
