Tony

Probably the simplest option for outgrowing your MDT is to enlarge the size of the existing MDT rather than adding an extra MDT. As for upgrading, I would recommend that you upgrade first to a 2.5.x based release - that's the largest upgrade jump possible from 2.1.x, still supports RHEL 6.x servers and will provide you with options to move on to more current releases if/when you choose to do so.
