--- Description ---
In order to apply QoS on Lustre traffic , the service-id of the Lustre traffic needs to be added to qos-policy.conf and then mapped to the appropriate service level. There is no good way to identify what the service-id of the Lustre traffic is. This feature adds the ability to find the service-id from lnetctl by doing:

lnetctl service-id
