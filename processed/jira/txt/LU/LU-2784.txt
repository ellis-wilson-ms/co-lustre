--- Description ---
Enhance Lustre build to provide RPMs which can be installed on Intel(R) Xeon Phi(TM) card automatically. This means the binaries should be compiled by cross compiler and deployed into Intel(R) Xeon Phi(TM) infrastructure on Intel(R) Xeon Phi(TM) host to be automatically installed on Xeon Phi card after boot. So, customer should just do the following commands to install Lustre on Intel(R) Xeon Phi(TM) card:

rpm -ivh lustre-client-mic-modules-.x86_64.rpm
rpm -ivh lustre-client-mic-.x86_64.rpm
service mpss restart
