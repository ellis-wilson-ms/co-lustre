This break build might affect old kernel from v4.17 to v4.19. Because v4.17 adds  and make fs.h #include  , v4.20 introduces full xarray support.

The cause is that fs.h(from kernel v4.17 commit b93b016313b3) is already included , so #include  which contains the definition of 'struct xa_node'  don't take effect.

 

cat libcfs/libcfs/linux/linux-prim.c
...
#include 
...
#ifndef HAVE_XARRAY_SUPPORT
#include 
#endif
