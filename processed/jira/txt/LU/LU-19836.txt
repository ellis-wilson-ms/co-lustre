--- Comment (JIRAUSER19404, Wed, 11 Feb 2026 03:04:52 +0000) ---
Alex, Andreas,

Quick head up.  26b "evict dead exports" tries to evict dead exports through 'umount -f', with scripts like

zconf_mount $HOSTNAME $MOUNT2

# make sure all imports are connected and not IDLE
do_facet client $LFS df >/dev/null 

ost_nexp=$(do_facet ost1 lctl get_param -n obdfilter.${ost1_svc}.num_exports)

zconf_umount $HOSTNAME $MOUNT2 -f

new_ost_nexp = wait_client_evicted ...

((ost_nexp - 1 == new_ost_nexp)) || error "..."

 

right after zconf_mount , $LFS df may run before LOV has fully consumed the OST configurations from MGS. In that case, no obd_statfs goes to the OSTs, the expected new connection isn’t created yet, and ost_nexp won’t count the new MOUNT2 by then. The result is timing‑dependent: if initialization finishes quickly the ost_nexp is correct and the ** test passes, otherwise it doesn’t.
