--- Comment (eaujames, Fri, 13 May 2022 14:55:36 +0000) ---
It is not the same issue that LU-15759.

The owners are correctly defined for those debugfs files.

The problem here is that "stats"/obd structure is freed at umount time. If a user have an open handle on the debugfs inode after umount , debugfs fops could access to already freed memory .

The issue occurs only with debugfs file, procfs stats don't cause a crash.
