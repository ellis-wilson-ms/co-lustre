--- Comment (adilger, Sun, 2 Nov 2025 10:16:15 +0000) ---
If there isn't any serious drawback to having a large number of NIDs configured, it would be better to increase lnet_interfaces_max=4096 or some very large limit and make that the maximum. There still has to be some NID limit to avoid a broken/malicious client sending a connection request with 1M or 1B NIDs and causing the server to OOM.
