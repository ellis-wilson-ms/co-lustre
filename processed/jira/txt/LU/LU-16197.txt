--- Description ---
Lustre cray-2.15 supports LNet NID types larger than 32-bits. As a part of this change, some of the LNet NID fields are stored in big endian order. kfi_cxi expects things to be in host byte order. kfilnd needs to translate LNet NID nid_num to host byte order. Failing to do this is preventing multiple LNet NID indexes from being used.
