--- Description ---
Currently when making ACL changes or XATTR changes, changelog only represents them as 'XATTR' change types. This is workable, however requires probing of both ACL and XATTR data to determine what changed (assuming you knew what it was before).

I propose that ACL changes have their own changelog flag to better identify those changes.

--- Comment (cfaber, Thu, 17 Oct 2013 17:08:03 +0000) ---
After briefly speaking with shadow about this, he suggests that these events could be separated with a client flag indicating which event type is coming through, either ACL, XATTR or Both.
