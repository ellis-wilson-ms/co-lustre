--- Description ---
The LUTF is a tool that can functionally test LNet through exercising its DLC interface. It's a python based tool that currently has a repository of 95 scripts and growing that test the DLC feature and the MR feature.

This ticket is opened to determine the best approach to integrate this tool in AT.

For more details on the design and usage of the LUTF please refer to:

https://wiki.whamcloud.com/pages/viewpage.action?pageId=36973031
