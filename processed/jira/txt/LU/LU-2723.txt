--- Comment (vitaly_fertman, Thu, 31 Jan 2013 07:48:00 +0000) ---
If dio overlaps with mapped region, first overlapped pages are sent by generic_file_direct_write->filemap_write_and_wait_range->writepages. After that the dio pages are sent by write_begin/write_end, writepages.

However, write_end->osc_page_cache_add does not release extent because the IO is not considered as synchronous. as the result, the following writepages->osc_cache_writeback_range() does not send pages are the extent is still ACTIVE and osc_cache_wait_range() hangs forever.
