--- Description ---
With dkms-3.1.3, installing lustre-client-dkms package hit the following failures:

WARNING: /usr/lib/dkms/common.postinst does not exist.
ERROR: DKMS version is too old and lustre-client was not
built with legacy DKMS support.
You must either rebuild lustre-client with legacy postinst
support or upgrade DKMS to a more current version.

It turned out the /usr/lib/dkms/common.postinst was removed from dkms-3.1.3 via the following change:

  Adjust installation for Red Hat based distributions by @scaronni in #461
