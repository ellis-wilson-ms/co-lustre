--- Description ---
Avoid use of kiblnd_destroy_fmr_pool_list under spinlock. This destroys FMR/FRMR pools under spinlock which leads into ib_dereg_mr() call which sleeps and causes "BUG: scheduling while atomic" when executing rmmod.
