--- Comment (JIRAUSER19288, Tue, 23 Sep 2025 14:06:11 +0000) ---
This can be reproduced on Rocky 9.4 and Lustre 2.16.58 due to an uninitialized pointer in "__gen_ssk_prime()". Steps to reproduce:

$ lgss_sk -f fs1 -t server -d /dev/urandom -p 512 -w /tmp/fs1.key
Reading random data for shared key from '/dev/urandom'
$ cp /tmp/fs1.key /tmp/fs1_cli.key
$ lgss_sk -t client -m /tmp/fs1_cli.key
Generating DH parameters, this can take a while...
Segmentation fault (core dumped)

Sébastien mentioned patch https://review.whamcloud.com/c/fs/lustre-release/+/60624, which fixes this issue as part of some restructuring. The patch was recently landed on master (pending 2.16.59).
