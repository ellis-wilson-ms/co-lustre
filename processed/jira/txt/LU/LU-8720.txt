--- Description ---
Configuration logs are append only files of limited size. In course of time the logs may grow over the limit size. Usually, configuration logs keep needless records marked as SKIP. The new lctl command "clear_conf" is added to

allow administrators to clear configuration files by removing mentioned SKIP records.  lctl man page is updated.
