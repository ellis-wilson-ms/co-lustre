--- Comment (niu, Mon, 27 Oct 2014 03:17:21 +0000) ---
For example here we see that root has 2.9TB on OST0. We have check the file at the filesystem and the objects on the ost and this value is wrong.

How did you check to find this value is wrong?

Is your system upgraded from 2.1 or it's an fresh 2.4 system? If it's upgraded from 2.1, that's could be dup of LU-3784, and you need to upgrade your e2fsprogs and trigger quota check on the problematic OSTs. I always suggest you upgrade your e2fsprogs to the latest version (1.42.12.wc1-7)

There is no way to do quotacheck online. You need to umount the OST and tirgger quotacheck by "tune2fs -O ^quota $ostdev", then "tune2fs -O quota $ostdev" (disable, then re-enable quota feature on OST device).
