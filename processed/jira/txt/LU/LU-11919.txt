--- Description ---
cl_dirty_max_pages (the internal representation of max_dirty_mb) is not zeroed at initialization.  The function client_adjust_max_dirty is called, but it only sets it if the value is most of the time for random memory...  But not all of the time.

So sometimes cl_max_dirty_pages ends up at some random positive value.  Easy fix.
