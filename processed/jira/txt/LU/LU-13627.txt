--- Description ---
Due to commit 3b2ad5cdb4833f052f4ac3d058e5adb8d55ab1ec in master-luster of e2fsprogs the build fails on Ubuntu.

 

ext2_types-wrapper.h doesn't belong in HFILES_IN since it is not generated into the build dir through a .in file. And a corresponding piece of code to make ext2_types.h into ext2_types-%(arch).h etc is missing in debian/rules.
