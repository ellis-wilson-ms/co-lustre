Jane

No, IML only works up to 2.12.x - there is not a version of it that support 2.15.x. As scanning the agendas of past Lustre conferences will tell you, there are a variety of monitoring options used in the community. I cannot really recommend one over another, I think that it is a question of you just finding one that is a good match for your requirements
