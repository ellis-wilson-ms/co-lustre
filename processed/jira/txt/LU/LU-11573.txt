--- Description ---
osd-ldiskfs has hard-coded limit on number of pages in a single IO:

                       bio = bio_alloc(GFP_NOIO, min(BIO_MAX_PAGES,

where

#define BIO_MAX_PAGES		256

so it can't form IOs larger than 256 pages or 1MB.
