--- Description ---
I create this ticket to follow the issue seen by @apercher  (cf. LU-8346's comment).

Here are the commands/configs to reproduce the issue:

fstab:

::/fs1 /mnt/fs1 lustre defaults,_netdev,noauto,x-systemd.requires=lnet.service,flock,user_xattr,nosuid 0 0
::/fs1/home /mnt/home lustre defaults,_netdev,noauto,x-systemd.requires=lnet.service,flock,user_xattr,nosuid 0 0

commands:

while true; do
 mount /mnt/home & mount /mnt/fs1
 umount /mnt/home
 umount /mnt/fs1
 lustre_rmmod
done

After some iterations "rmmod lustre" will hang in "lu_context_key_degister"
