--- Description ---
symptom:

got this error msg "umount: /tmp/lustre-ost1: not mounted"

root cause:

for local device

   "do_facet ost$devnum umount -d $dev"  turns out to be "umount /tmp/lustre-ost1"

should be changed to

    local mntpt=$(facet_mntpt ost$devnum)

    do_facet ost$devnum umount -d $mntpt
