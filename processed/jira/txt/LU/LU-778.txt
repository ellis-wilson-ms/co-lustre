--- Environment ---
RHEL 6.2

--- Description ---
As of RHEL6.2 kernel 2.6.32-204.el6, rdma_create_id() requires a queue pair type as a fourth argument.  This was previously inferred from the rdma_port_space argument. Consequently, ko2iblnd fails to build on this kernel.  We have a patch to address this.
