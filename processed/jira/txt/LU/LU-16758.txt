--- Description ---
Currently Lustre clients rely on the lustre_root/@REALM principal to authenticate.

Consider supporting the more standard Kerberos machine principal (i.e. host/@REALM) as well to avoid the need for additional keytab entries.

This would bring Lustre in line with other services such as OpenSSH and NFS.
