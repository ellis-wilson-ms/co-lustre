--- Description ---
Now by default the size of DEBUG_SIZE is 2MB multiplied by number of cpu. This way lustre debug buffer could be just 4MB on a system with 2 CPUs, despite 3GB RAM. It is the reason why often time period in debug logs doesn't scope the reason of failure(sometimes depending on log level debug buffer stores just several seconds).
