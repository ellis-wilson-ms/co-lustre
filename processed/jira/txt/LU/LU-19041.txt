--- Description ---
with the kernel 5.14 from RHEL9.x/Rocky9.x, libcfs_call_trace prints:

2025-04-16 10:18:11 [60475.942360] Lustre: mdt00_010: service thread pid 591995 was inactive for 201.381 seconds. The thread might be hung, or it might only be slow and will resume later. Dumping the stack trace for debugging purposes:
2025-04-16 10:18:11 [60475.942363] Pid: 592018, comm: mdt_rdpg00_003 5.14.0-284.18.1.x7.2.000.43.x86_64 #1 SMP PREEMPT_DYNAMIC Tue Apr 1 11:50:35 EDT 2025
2025-04-16 10:18:11 [60475.942368] Call Trace TBD:
2025-04-16 10:18:11 [60475.942370] No stack, save_stack_trace_tsk() could not be found

instead of the stack trace.

Unfortunately when kallsyms_lookup_name was removed (backported for SUSE) we lost access to the not exported stack_trace_save_tsk().

As of kernel v6.6-rc1-3-g7d672f40941a stack_trace_save_tsk is now exported as a GPL symbol.
