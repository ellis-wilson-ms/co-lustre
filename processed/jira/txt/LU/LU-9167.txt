--- Description ---
hsm_cancel_all_actions() assumes that we are running in the CDT's context but we are really running in the context of some user process (usually lctl). Therefore hsm_cancel_all_actions() must not use the CDT environment (via mdt->mdt_coordinator.cdt_env).
