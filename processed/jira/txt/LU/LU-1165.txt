As far as I understand, the ll_ost_io threads have a value in their lu_context for the ccc_session_key lu_key because of the tag LCT_SESSION. Unfortunately nothing prevents deregistering ccc_session_key while ll_ost_io threads are processing requests. So the system asserts either because ccc_session_key is still in use (key->lct_used > 1) when deregistering, or because ll_ost_io thread's lu_context is calling key_fini() on the just'removed ccc_session_key.

It seems to me there is a synchronisation mechanism missing between ll_ost_io threads and ccc_session_key destruction. But there is no reason to correlate these two lustre component, isn't it ? Maybe having two distinct LCT_SESSION tags one for server side, one for client side would be better.
