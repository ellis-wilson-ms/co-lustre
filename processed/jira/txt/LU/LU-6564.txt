--- Description ---
In the results.yml file, the lustre test framework prints escape characters in the error strings. It may print them in other areas within the results.yml file but I haven't found any instances. 

error: "skipping\ SLOW\ test\ 24o"

Maloo and autotest are in the process of upgrading to newer ruby versions. The yaml implementation is changing from syck to psych which chokes on such a string. It would be nice to have this fixed at the source rather than running a search and replace on all results.yml files in autotest and maloo.
