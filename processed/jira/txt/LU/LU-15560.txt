--- Description ---
conf-sanity test_125 started failing with ‘test_125 failed with 1’ on March 6, 2021 for 2.14.50.130 servers and 2.12.6 clients at  https://testing.whamcloud.com/test_sets/84da2d25-f8e9-4cb9-866b-64a9eed672a0 . On September 25, 2021, the error message changed to ‘test_125 returned 1’ for 2.14.54.92 servers and 2.12.7 clients at  https://testing.whamcloud.com/test_sets/3bca7cdf-65d7-4562-8ff4-d5698d725761 . 

The error message we see in all of the suite_log  is

CMD: trevis-78vm3 libtool execute l_tunedisk /dev/mapper/ost1_flakey
trevis-78vm3: sh: libtool: command not found

Although the timelines don’t match up, the failure to find libtool is most likely related to changes in https://review.whamcloud.com/40253 landed to master on January 22 2021.

This test fails 100% of the time for interop testing. 

For recent failures see:

2.14.0.21 servers and 2.12.7 clients - https://testing.whamcloud.com/test_sets/3ee61e14-5cc9-487f-9c2c-bb1cdd1f7bfd

2.15.0.RC2 servers and 2.12.7 clients - https://testing.whamcloud.com/test_sets/22ac659a-68d8-45b2-86bf-af05113ec932
