--- Description ---
When lnd module starts, some parameters are set to some values.

For instance, in o2iblnd, lnd_peercredits_hiw is changed to be between lct_peer_tx_credits/2 and lct_peer_tx_credits-1.

When a NI is added using lnetctl, these modifications are not done.

Depending on the parameter, either an automatic set, or a warning could be triggered.
