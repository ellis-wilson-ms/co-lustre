--- Description ---
It would be nice if configure cache generated when initially invoking ./configure with C or cache-file options could be used by subsequent configure invocations made as part of make rpms. The idea would be to pass along the -C or -cache-file options from the initial ./configure to the one launched as part of the rpm build.
