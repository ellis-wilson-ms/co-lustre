Stephane, can we get MDS and client dmesg just in case there's something interesting in there?

The issue is very likely that the lease taken by migrate is being broken by another operation.  This error message would occur in that case.  So that would be a write or another modifying operation, including migrate.

It's possible there's a race condition in the code, but it's more likely an operation that's actually being requested on the client that's causing this.  If you can get client debug, we can try to see what's going on.

adilger , what do you say to moving the -116 here for lease checks away from CERROR?  It is expected if the lease is broken and printing to dmesg/console is not ideal.
