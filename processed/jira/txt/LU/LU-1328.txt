--- Comment (laisiyao, Sun, 27 May 2012 20:37:06 +0000) ---
Hmm, it looks to be a bug in ll_splice_alias():

Once ll_splice_alias() found a reusable dentry for current inode, it will tries to reuse it, and dput() the just allocated dentry, but before that d_rehash() is called to hash this newly allocated dentry into dcache, with dentry->d_inode set to NULL. This originates from a kernel bug (d_rehash() used to be called by d_splice_alias() too), and actually it's planned to be removed in another patch, but we just think this line is redundant, and don't see it will cause error.

I'll update FC15 patchless client patch later to reflect this change.
