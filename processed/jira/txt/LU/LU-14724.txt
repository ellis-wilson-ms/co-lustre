--- Comment (lixi_wc, Mon, 24 May 2021 12:12:11 +0000) ---
The process of trigger the problem is:

1. change the NRS policy to "tbf", and create two rules of 

"opcode={ost_write},nid={192.168.*.*@tcp}"

2. change the rank of a rule

3. change the rate of a rule

4. stop the rule

5. change the NRS policy to "tbf gid", create a rule of 

"gid={500}"

, repeat 2, 3, 4

6. change the NRS policy to "tbf uid", create a rule of 

"uid={500}"

, repeat 2, 3, 4

7. change the NRS policy to "tbf jobid", create a rule of 

"uid={dd.*}"

, repeat 2, 3, 4

8. change the NRS policy to "tbf nid", create a rule of 

"nid={192.168.*.*@tcp}"

, repeat 2, 3, 4

I am not sure whether the process can be reduce to simpler steps. But it seems quite easy to reproduce.
