--- Comment (hongchao.zhang, Thu, 26 May 2011 03:42:34 +0000) ---
the problems are as follows,

1, rundbench is a bash script, and "/bin/sh" is not identical to "/bin/bash" on all distros.

2, if "MPI_USER" isn't set, don't use "su $MPI_USER" in function "mpi_run" in functions.sh

3, in some kinds of Linux(say, Ubuntu),  truncate is a system command, then the directory containing our truncate tools (the test directory)

   should be appended to use our truncate explicitly

4, in obdfilter-survey, drops sh as the interpreter of OBDSURVEY, for the same reason of #1

5, add VERBOSE option in test-framework.sh
