--- Description ---
Hello,

We had two OSS on Fir (Sherlock's scratch filesystem) that became unresponsive one after the other, last weekend, with a high load. It's a full flash system running Lustre 2.15 on top of mdraid/ldiskfs.

First it started with OSS fir-io6-s1 and when it came back online (after a reboot + recovery), another one fir-io7-s1 did the same. I have traces and crash dumps for both.

It looks like in both cases, many threads were blocked on jbd2 transactions:

At some point to avoid this deadlock, @Alex Zhuravlev recommended to run with https://review.whamcloud.com/#/c/48033/ along with https://review.whamcloud.com/47925 . Please note that this new deadlock happened with a version of Lustre having the following patches on top of 2.15.5:
