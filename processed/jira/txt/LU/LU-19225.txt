--- Comment (JIRAUSER18433, Tue, 5 Aug 2025 21:36:35 +0000) ---
Lustre retries establish a new connection. There are LNet retries, but the timeout is short (180 seconds maybe?). So LNet would give up during a longer downtime.

We've seen cases where socklnd won't reconnect correctly after a service interruption. It'll get stuck in a loop of attempting to connect indefinitely. It's better to destroy all of this old connection state and start fresh. Try to establish a connect to send an LNet message - if that fails, let the upper layers decide what they want to do. If LNet or Lustre want to keep trying, let them. But don't loop in the LND forever.
