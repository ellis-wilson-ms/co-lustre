--- Comment (pjones, Fri, 29 Mar 2024 15:45:02 +0000) ---
Jane

No you cannot. You could build 2.15.4 against el8.7 if you wished to stay on a specific kernel version but older Lustre versions will not build against more current Linux distributions (unless you backport the patches that added in this support- again, not something we would encourage/support - we produce the maintenance releases to make this unnecessary)

Peter

--- Comment (pjones, Thu, 28 Mar 2024 21:13:48 +0000) ---
Jane

Mixing versions among OSSes is only expecting to be a very short-lived state of affairs while upgrading and is only tested as such, so I cannot really say what the outcome would be. Given that the difference between the versions is small, it might well work without problems, but it would certainly not be a recommended or supported configuration.
