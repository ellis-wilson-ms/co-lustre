--- Description ---
osd_oxc_get() has a quirk where ENTRY / EXIT may be unbalanced with respect to the out: label.

In addition when CDEBUG() is enabled EXIT the macro trace is included in the rcu critical section. Move EXIT outside of the critical section and re-balance the ENTRY/EXIT macros.
