--- Description ---
On the first client, create and change to a directory.

c1$ mkdir /mnt/lustre/dir1; cd /mnt/lustre/dir1

	Then, on the second, rename that directory:

c2$ mv /mnt/lustre/dir1 /mnt/lustre/dir2

	Back on the first client, open(".", O_DIRECTORY...) will fail.

c1$ ls

/bin/ls: .: No such file or directory

c$ pwd

/mnt/lustre/dir1

c1$ readlink /proc/$$/cwd
