--- Comment (niu, Fri, 11 Mar 2016 04:06:27 +0000) ---
I see, the requirement makes sense to me, but I don't think tuning the least_qunit is a proper way, because it affects the hard limit as well.

A better way is to add a proc interface to quota master (qmt), and user may turn off the qunit adjustment for soft limit (no qunit adjustment even if soft limit is exceeded) via this interface. Of course, people should be warned that grace time accuracy will be lost when he turns it off.

I can compose a patch if you don't against. Thanks.

--- Comment (mhanafi, Wed, 24 Feb 2016 18:16:56 +0000) ---
I think there are some IO patterns that are effected by the softquota limit then others. At least that is what our users are reporting. Since most of our quota are 100'sTB and we have large gap between soft and hard quota we are willing to give up limit/grace time accuracy for no lost of performance.
