--- Description ---
Pool quotas recalculation works only when all quota types are enabled(enabled=ugp). Otherwise, even if only one or two types are enabled qmt_pool_recalc() stops recalculation and returns -EINVAL. As a result Pool Quotas may have insane granted space resulting in a wrong -EDQUOT.
