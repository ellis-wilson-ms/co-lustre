--- Description ---
Replicate and/or allow services running on MDT0000 to other MDTs/MDSes:

	FID Location Database (FLDB) replication, controls FID meta-sequence mapping to MDTs and OSTs, changes very rarely

	Quota Master Target (QMT), that controls quota limits which change only infrequently.  Quota usage that is changing continuously can be reconstructed from the MDTs and OSTs directly in case of a failover.

	flock DLM locking, in-memory only, possibly just reconstructed like normal MDT0000 failover and lock recovery, but on another MDS

	group DLM locking, in-memory only, possibly just reconstructed like normal MDT0000 failover and lock recovery, but on another MDS
