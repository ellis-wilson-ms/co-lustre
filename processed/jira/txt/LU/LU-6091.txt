--- Description ---
How to reproduce:

With a fresh mounted client or purge the cache by echo 3 > /proc/sys/vm/drop_caches

For any regular file, do a open(2) with O_RDONLY, then FIEMAP ioctl without stat(2). The FIEMAP ioctl will return an empty result.
