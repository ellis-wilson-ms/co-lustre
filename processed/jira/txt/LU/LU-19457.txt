--- Description ---
Lustre clients that belong to a nodemap are identified based on their NIDs. In some use cases, this can be considered inflexible and difficult to implement.

Alternatively, when strong authentication is in place (SSK or Kerberos), some information conveyed by the authentication token could be used to identify clients and assign them to nodemaps. That would make the implementation easier and more flexible.
