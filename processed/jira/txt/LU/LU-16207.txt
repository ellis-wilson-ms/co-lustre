--- Description ---
SLES15 SP3 fails to build using rpm-build-4.14.1-29.46 from the main O/S repository with error message:

Dependency tokens must begin with alpha-numeric, '_' or '/': BuildRequires: %kernel_module_package_buildreqs

Updating rpm-build to 4.14.3-150300.46.1 or higher resolved the build issue.
