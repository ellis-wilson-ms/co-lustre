--- Comment (paf, Fri, 7 Jun 2024 15:36:29 +0000) ---
Yes, this is a known issue, it's not possible to do UDIO with GDS since UDIO requires accessing the pages on the CPU.

So this crash is expected

There's a patch to disable it here: https://review.whamcloud.com/c/fs/lustre-release/+/52391
