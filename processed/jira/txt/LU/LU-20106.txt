--- Comment (kwebb, Fri, 10 Apr 2026 19:05:30 +0000) ---
I would like to report that with a mostly default configuration, waiting for a single MTIME update takes about 10 minutes to report to the changelog.  As a workaround, stat()ing the file in question seems to force the update to the changelog much more quickly.  For backup using changelog 10 minutes seems like a long delay.  So now I am looking for guidance on how to keep changelog based incremental backups as accurate as possible.  For example, if a particular client is not very active, could flushing/syncing the local cache to lustre more frequently be a possibilty?
