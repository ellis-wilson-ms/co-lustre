--- Comment (adilger, Sun, 26 Mar 2023 23:30:52 +0000) ---
In all of the cases where fs_log_size() is checked (runtests included) the actual space compared was based on OST space usage.  However, it looks like runtests was failing because fs_log_size() was scaling the returned value based on the MDT count, and not on the OST count.  It looks like the main "leakage" of space on the OSTs was only due to blocks being allocated to the object directories O//dN, and this was very obvious when runtests was run in isolation and failed repeatedly, but passed if it was run after other tests.

Change fs_log_size() to scale the "slop" by OSTCOUNT rather than MDTCOUNT.
