--- Comment (yujian, Tue, 26 Jul 2011 08:49:50 +0000) ---
Note that some of the tests in conf-sanity.sh hard-code "lustre" as the fsname because they create a new test filesystem using that name, or they use a filesystem image with the fsname already formatted in the image.

Yes, they are conf-sanity test 32a and 32b. The $LUSTRE/tests/disk1_8.tar.bz2 contains the mds and ost images which were formatted with fsname "lustre". So, we should not change the "lustre" to "$FSNAME" in those two tests. I just checked that they passed with "FSNAME=testfs" on master branch: https://maloo.whamcloud.com/test_sets/360d9620-b785-11e0-8bdf-52540025f9af

After looking through the test suite, James' patch has covered all of the cases.
