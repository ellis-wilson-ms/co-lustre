It looks like ZFS* is somehow reporting that NAME_MAX IS 256 but it only accepts filenames of 255 bytes.  This confuses POSIX. Probably the easiest thing is to change osd-zfs to return a max filename size of 255 to match ldiskfs. 

[*] pathconf() results might be generated inside glibc instead of the filesystem, which is annoying and needs a patch to glibc to fix.
