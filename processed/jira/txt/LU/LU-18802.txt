[root@dev lustre]# lfs hsm_restore fifi                                         
Cannot send HSM request (use of fifi): Operation not permitted                  

Was an HSM restore already queued?

If so, nothing can be done, an EX lock is taken on the server: everything will wait this lock. This action needs to be canceled/timeout/failed to release the EX lock.
