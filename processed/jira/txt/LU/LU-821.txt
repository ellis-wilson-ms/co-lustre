--- Comment (johann, Thu, 3 Nov 2011 12:25:53 +0000) ---
> At CEA they have a weird situation where a user presumably has only 8 GBs of data, whereas the quota commands report a usage of several TBs.

Have you tried to run quotacheck?

> So we would like to know where the quota information is stored by Lustre:

Space accounting is handled by ldiskfs itself. Operational quota files on the slaves (i.e. MDT & OSTs) are available at the root under lquota_v2.user and lquota_v2.group. As for administrative quota files (i.e. stored on the MDT which is the quota master), there are under the OBJECTS directory and the file names are admin_quotafile_v2.usr & admin_quotafile_v2.grp.
