--- Comment (adilger, Wed, 23 Jul 2025 19:13:33 +0000) ---
Candidates in this list would include:

	better stats for recovery times so we can measure improvements (client reconnect time histogram, RPC recovery/sec, LDLM lock recovery, waiting for client eviction) - LU-18681

	reduce ldiskfs MMP recovery time - LU-13932

	COS (Commit-on-Share) to ensure that there are no dependencies between clients during recovery (improve recovery concurrency, reduce evictions), but may impact runtime performance (which may be preferable to some customers over a longer recovery window)

	VBR (Version Based Recovery) to allow clients & servers to recover filesystem modifying operations independently of each other, instead of depending on the RPC transno to sequence client recovery

	parallelize RPC recovery/resend with VBR and multiple server threads (AFAIK RPC recovery is currently single-threaded on the server)

	start RPC recovery and LDLM lock replay as soon as client connects (as much as possible, taking dependencies into account but maybe none with COS) while waiting for other clients to reconnect (related to COS, VBR and parallel replay above)

	active server->client reconnect once initial client reconnection is done and server is waiting for clients

	tune client reconnect backoff time so that they retry connections to servers more aggressively after MGS notification

	fault tolerant MGS would allow IR to always be used even when "primary MGS" would be offline - LU-17819
