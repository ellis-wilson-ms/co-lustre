--- Description ---
With --no-verify option, mirror extend just concatenate a victim file's mirror without stale its component, the result FLR file is inconsistent.

--- Comment (adilger, Fri, 30 Apr 2021 08:53:28 +0000) ---
I think this means "--no-verify" is "working as designed".  The whole point of "--no-verify" is that it does not verify the attached mirror is correct.  This option is intended for processes that do the data mirror create/resync outside of lfs and then attach the new mirror directly to the file.
