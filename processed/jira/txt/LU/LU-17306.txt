--- Description ---
During the cleanup orphan phase after MDT start, reconnection leads to unsynchronized last id between MDT and OST. This means that MDT could assign non existing objects to a client for a file create operation.

Oct 25 15:20:31 capstorn075 kernel: LustreError: 358076:0:(ofd_dev.c:1570:ofd_create_hdl()) capstor-OST0087: dropping old orphan cleanup request
Oct 25 15:22:20 capstorn075 kernel: Lustre: capstor-OST0087: MDS LAST_ID [0x2540000400:0xb6941:0x0] (747841) is 352 behind OST LAST_ID [0x2540000400:0xb6aa1:0x0] (748193), trust the OST
