[   45.564829] LustreError: 8282:0:(llog_osd.c:1243:llog_osd_next_block()) lustre-MDT0000-osd: invalid llog tail at log id [0x1:0x3:0x0] offset 8492 last_rec idx 65536 tail idx 5 lrt len 2 read_size 300

# ps ax|grep osp

   8284 ?        I      0:00 [osp-syn-1-0]
