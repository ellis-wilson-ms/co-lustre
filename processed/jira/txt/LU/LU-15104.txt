--- Description ---
Random ha_dir_stripe_count allows clients to create directories with different stripe counts in one ha.sh test session. With

  DSTRIPECOUNT=3 DSTRIPECOUNTRAND=true

ha.sh will create the directories with stripe counts 1,2 or 3.
