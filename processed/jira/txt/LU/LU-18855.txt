--- Description ---
The function liblustgreapi_hsm.c:ct_open_by_fid uses the old implementation of open by fid. This should be change to call llapi_open_by_fid_at directly.

The will make the open_by_fid_fd field in the struct hsm_copytool_private obsolete. Although I don't think we can't remove the field, we can stop initializing it.
