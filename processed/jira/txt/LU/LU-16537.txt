--- Description ---
4k ext4/ldiskfs files are 4294967295 blocks long. Last block never gets written due to a bug in osd_ldiskfs_map_inode_pages():

                if (fp->index + clen >= max_page_index)
                        GOTO(cleanup, rc = -EFBIG);
