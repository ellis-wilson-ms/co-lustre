--- Description ---
The output from "lfs_migrate -v" is not well formed when migrating links or special files.

/mnt/testfs/l0: /mnt/testfs/l0: done
\n\r[K/mnt/testfs/l1: already migrated via another link
\n\r[K/mnt/testfs/l2: already migrated via another link
:

This is due to incorrect use of "-e" and also passing the "-v" option through to "lfs migrate", which causes the filename to be printed twice.
