--- Description ---
In parallel OST mount scenario, the MGC could be disconnected manually in "lustre_start_mgc".

furthermore, if the first mount failed, the corresponding OSS device will be destroyed asynchronously,

which will cause the following mount fail for it can't setup the OSS again.
