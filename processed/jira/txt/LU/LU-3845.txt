--- Comment (doug, Wed, 28 Aug 2013 00:42:55 +0000) ---
The updated test plan for this feature indicates this change in behaviour.  Fujitsu decided that the "lower" the priority number the higher the priority.  Our implementation was updated to match theirs.  Priority 0 has the highest priority so what you are seeing is correct.
