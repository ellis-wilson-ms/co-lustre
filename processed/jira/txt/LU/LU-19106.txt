I compared lod_pool_qos_penalties_calc() with ltd_qos_penalties_calc(), and their calling in lod_ost_alloc_qos(), found the only difference is from the following lines:

        /* Detect -EAGAIN early, before expensive lock is taken. */
        if (!ltd_qos_is_usable(&lod->lod_ost_descs))
                GOTO(out_nolock, rc = -EAGAIN);
