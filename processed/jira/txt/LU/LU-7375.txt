--- Description ---
the el6.7 case is missing from one of the auto detect functions in lbuild.  this means that when building for el6.7 the --target option must be present on the command line.  This isn't true for any other distro.

Simple oversight that prevents automatic detection of the right target on el6.7 builds.  I will push a patch to fix this.
