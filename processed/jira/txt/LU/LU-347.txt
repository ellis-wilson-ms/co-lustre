--- Comment (bobijam, Fri, 27 May 2011 06:47:30 +0000) ---
LU-106's patch can fix this error, resolve it as dup of LU-106, and feel free to reopen it if the issue reoccurs after LU-106's patch got landed.

root cause:

mgs was in clean up phase, while conf-sanity.sh was checking /proc/fs/lustre/devices and find the mgs device, obd_devlist_next() class_incref() it, afterward class_decref() it which called obd_cleanup() again, causing invalid memory access violation.
