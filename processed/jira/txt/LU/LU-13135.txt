--- Comment (adilger, Fri, 9 Aug 2024 23:39:42 +0000) ---
@Andreas, so an ordinary user won't be able to remove own files and free space if root has consumed his quota?

I'm not sure exactly what scenario you are thinking about, so I will guess.

I don't think it would ever be the case that removing files would fail because of quota limits.  Possibly if there was a quota limit on projid=0 and ldiskfs didn't explicitly exclude that ID, but I don't think this will happen in practice.  In the deployments that I'm aware of, the projid quota limit is only applied to non-zero projid.

That said, I don't think it is a great idea to enforce quota limits on the root user, but some sites have strange configurations and we can't prevent them from doing such things if they really want to.
