--- Description ---
The pacemaker resource agent files are scripts that need executable permission but lustre/conf/resource/Makefile.am treats them as DATA, should be SCRIPTS.

And debian/lustre-resource-agents.install has a typo in the target directory, usr/lib.ocf/

Thus the debian packaged resource agents doesn't work.
