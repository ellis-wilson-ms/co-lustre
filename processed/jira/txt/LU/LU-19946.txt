--- Description ---
Watching an AI hallucinate the --mmp option for mkfs.lustre got me thinking that this might be a useful thing to have. Right now we automatically enable MMP if mkfsoptions="-O MMP" etc. is specified but this may not be desirable in all cases (such as testing, etc). 

As a result I'm proposing we simplify this with this --mmp or --MMP to enable / disable.
