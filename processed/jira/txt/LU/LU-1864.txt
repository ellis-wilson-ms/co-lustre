--- Comment (liwei, Thu, 13 Sep 2012 22:31:16 +0000) ---
arc_prune_func() uses osd_device::od_site.  It is correct on orion but not on master and b2_3, where osd_device::od_site is not initialized or used at all.  The simplest fix is to use osd_device::od_dt_dev::dd_lu_dev::ld_site, which points to ofd_device::ofd_site, instead.
