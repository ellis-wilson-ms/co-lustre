--- Comment (jay, Fri, 30 Mar 2012 14:23:42 +0000) ---
This issue is because it holds mutex of cl_lock when it's flushing pages. If the OST is really slow to finish that IO(pretty common in 2.x), glimpse ast will be blocked for a long while and finally caused this client to be evicted.

To fix this issue, we shouldn't hold mutex of cl_lock when writing the pages out. I'll work out a workaround soon.
