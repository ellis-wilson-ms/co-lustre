--- Description ---
After applying the patch for ll_fsync()" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="LU-7927">LU-7927 to our code, another deadlock was exposed.  It does not look like this was CAUSED by ll_fsync()" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="LU-7927">LU-7927, it just seems the timing change caused by ll_fsync()" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="LU-7927">LU-7927 allowed this bug to be observed.  (Or possibly this code was deadlocking there first - It's hard to say precisely)

The lli_trunc_sem is taken in 'read' mode in both ll_page_mkwrite and vvp_io_fault_start.  This can lead to a deadlock with another thread which asks for the semaphore in write mode before that time.

—
