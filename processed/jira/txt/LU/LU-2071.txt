--- Description ---
There seems to be a minor memory leak in all versions of lustre since at least Feb 2007.

The name##_seq_open does single_open, but corresponding lprocfs_seq_release does seq_release instead of single_release, and as such a bit of memory allocated in single_open remains not freed.

As such every call to lctl leaks 32 bytes.
