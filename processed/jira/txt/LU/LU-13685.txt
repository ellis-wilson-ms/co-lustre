--- Comment (ashehata, Wed, 17 Jun 2020 14:02:29 +0000) ---
Yes it should be able to. A config like this:

Client:
lnetctl route add --net o2ib3 --gateway hop1@o2ib1

HOP 1:
lnetctl route add --net o2ib3 --gateway hop2@o2ib2

HOP 2:
lnetctl route add --net o2ib1 --gateway hop1@o2ib2

Server:
lnetctl route add --net o2ib1 --gateway hop2@o2ib3
