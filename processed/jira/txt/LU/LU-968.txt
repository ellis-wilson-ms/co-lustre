--- Comment (aboyko, Fri, 6 Jan 2012 06:15:40 +0000) ---
root cause of the bug:

e2scan/filelist.c have next code at filelist_dblist_iterate_cb() function: 

 is_dirname = (dirent2->file_type == EXT2_FT_DIR) ? 1 : 0; 

for /mnt/lustre/test/ file_type is 18, but type of dir is only 3 bits.

patch is submitted to http://review.whamcloud.com/1925
