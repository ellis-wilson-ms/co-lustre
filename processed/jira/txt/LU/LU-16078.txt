--- Description ---
kiblnd_get_completion_vector() will always hash multiple connections to the same peer to the same comp_vector. One strategy being deployed in RXE to spread out work is to schedule work based on the comp_vector value. This approach requires o2iblnd to use a different comp_vector with muliple connections going to the same peer.
