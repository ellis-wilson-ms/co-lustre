--- Description ---
Back in 1.6/1.8 days we were able to run e.g. sanity as:

SETUP=setup CLEANUP=cleanup sh sanity.sh and that caused a remount after every subtest.

Other more involved proedures were possible to all from run_one() calls.

This all was lost somethime after run_one() migration to test-framework.sh

Now the functionality is useful one so let's restore it.
