--- Description ---
mdd_parent_fid() reads xattrs without taking a lock, so the xattr cache can be populated with stale data. So link_ea data forms a cycle :

        osp 
         b'trusted.link' 
          b'16' [0x2c0000400:0x21:0x0]

         osp 
         b'trusted.link' 
          b'[0x2c0000400:0x21:0x0]:0' [0x2c0000404:0x720:0x0]

        osp 
         b'trusted.link' 
          b'8' [0x280000403:0x7:0x0]

        osp 
         b'trusted.link' 
          b'[0x280000403:0x7:0x0]:3' [0x2c0000409:0x729:0x0]

It is reproduced by racer.
