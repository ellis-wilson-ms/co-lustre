--- Comment (adilger, Wed, 25 Jun 2014 17:39:01 +0000) ---
I think this is just a usage problem, though possibly something that should be fixed in a way that makes lctl easier to use. The lctl command does not save the "filter" and "show" masks across lctl invocations. They are only really useful for interactive lctl use:

# lctl
lctl> filter all_types
lctl> show ldiskfs
lctl> df /tmp/debug /tmp/debug.txt

That said, this is not very convenient to use, and adding a "--filter" and "--show" options to debug_kernel may be useful.
