--- Description ---
The cr_time field of a changelog record is (secs . So lfs_changelog() should use ".09d" when printing the fractional part. Instead it uses ".06d":

		secs = rec->cr_time >> 30;
                gmtime_r(&secs, &ts);
		printf(LPU64" %02d%-5s %02d:%02d:%02d.%06d %04d.%02d.%02d "
		       "0x%x t="DFID, rec->cr_index, rec->cr_type,
        	       changelog_type2str(rec->cr_type),
                       ts.tm_hour, ts.tm_min, ts.tm_sec,
		       (int)(rec->cr_time & ((1cr_flags & CLF_FLAGMASK, PFID(&rec->cr_tfid));
