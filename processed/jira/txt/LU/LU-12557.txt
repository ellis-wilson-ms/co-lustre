--- Description ---
This morning, we found a group on Oak that was able to get past their inode quota. The limit should be 1.5M inodes, but they were able to get past it at about 1.9M inodes. We have checked that this didn't involve Lustre 2.12 (tested on a 2.10.8 client also just to be sure it was not a 2.10/2.12 compat issue).
