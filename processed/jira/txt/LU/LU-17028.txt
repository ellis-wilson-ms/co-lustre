--- Description ---
Add ldiskfs support for openEuler 20.03 4.19 kernel.

The older LTS openEuler 20.03 is still under supportting to 2026.03 by openEuler community[1].

Lots of users still using it or its derived OSes in their production environments.  It is worth enabling Lustre for it.
