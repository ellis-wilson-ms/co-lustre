--- Description ---
In commit fb7a499bf47530b9eee162990c8f1815b643d30e there was a sed introduced that was broken across several lines with no line continuations:

		ver=$(echo $ver |

		  sed -e 's/^v(.*)/\1/'

		      -e 's/_RC[0-9].*$//'

		      -e 's/_/./g')

This is, of course, incorrect.
