--- Environment ---
RHEL10.1 with Lustre client installed

--- Description ---
l_getsepol calls security_policyvers(), which returns version 33. However, RHEL 10.1 uses policy version 35 (located at /etc/selinux/targeted/policy/policy.35). Because l_getsepol searches downward from the returned version (33, 32, 31...), it fails to find the actual policy file.
