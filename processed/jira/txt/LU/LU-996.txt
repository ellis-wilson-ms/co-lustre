--- Description ---
The parsing of the "user" mount option by mount_lustre.c is missing.  The opt_map[] entry for "nouser" exists and is correct, but the "user" mount option does not exist.  There may be technical or security reasons why non-root users cannot mount a Lustre filesystem, but I don't recall the details at this time.
