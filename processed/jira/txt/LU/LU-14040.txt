--- Description ---
Statistics are important when trying to understand the behaviour of a cluster's network. This epic will track:

	LNet message timing statistics:
	
		How long a message spends on the different queues

		How long a message takes to complete

	
	

	Load/locking statistics
	
		The CPT architecture allows for processing load to be spread across the different CPTs, but there is no way to understand how heavily each CPT is being used
		
			keep track of time taken for CPT specific operations; example MDUnlink/MDLookup
