--- Environment ---
AlmaLinux 8.9 , Lustre 2.15.4 on clients and servers

--- Description ---
Again we encountered a problem with the quota transactions that prohibits to remove selected files, similarly to the fixed issue LU-15193. Now, the problem reports more than 12 qids, instead of 8.

MDS logs show:

LustreError: 18169:0:(qsd_handler.c:900:qsd_op_begin()) pd01-MDT0000: more than 12 qids enforced for a transaction?

In contrast to LU-15193, now this effect seems to occur quite randomly, as all of our directories have enabled project quotas and most of them work without any problem. However, for some of the files the error with the 12 qid transaction occurs.

Moreover, in some cases, setting project ID 0 does not solve the problem and the file removal is still prohibited.

In the past, when we had the 2.15.3 version, we encountered the LU-15193 problem that we fix by updating it to 2.15.4.
