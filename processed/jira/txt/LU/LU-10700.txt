--- Description ---
lfs hsm_cancel file1 file2... sends all cancels to the first copytool found for archive of file1. This is because of the logic in hsm_find_compatible() and the fact that a hal has an archive_id but a hai doesn't. This is not critical for the posix CT since it doesn't implement cancel. (Cancel "works" but it's ignored by the CT until a progress RPC fails.)
