It is because the LFSCK slave on OST returns -ENODEV (-19) to the LFSCK master on MDT to indicate that related target device does not involve in LFSCK or have finished and left the LFSCK, but such errno has been explained as system recovery by ptlrpc by wrong. I have made a patch to fix it in http://review.whamcloud.com/#/c/9661/
