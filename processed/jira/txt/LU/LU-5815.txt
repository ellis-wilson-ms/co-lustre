--- Description ---
"d_count" is defined at

lustre 2.6.0

    lustre/include/linux/lustre_patchless_compat.h,  line 123

and

sles11sp3 3.0.101-0.35.0

    include/linux/dcache.h, line 153.

The '#define d_count' is new in sles11sp3 3.0.101-0.35.1, (Not in -0.31.1)

and '#define d_count' was named '#define  d_refcount' in 2.4.3.

Now we have redefined symbols.
