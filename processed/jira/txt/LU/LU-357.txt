--- Description ---
racer test currently has 2 defects :

1) a very short duration: 120 sec;

master, SLOW=yes, duration=120 sec :

2) racer.sh is called recursively :

Since LUSTRE/tests is the first in the PATH, then racer is :

racer=/rw/usr/lib/lustre/tests/racer.sh

lustre/tests/racer.sh ->

    bash $(dirname $0)/runracer ->

       racer=`which racer.sh` ->

          racer=/rw/usr/lib/lustre/tests/racer.sh ->

             bash $(dirname $0)/runracer -> ..., etc.
