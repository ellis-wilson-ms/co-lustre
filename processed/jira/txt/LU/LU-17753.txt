--- Description ---
The following race between stats may lead to wrong timestamps provided to stat:

Process 1
stat("file")
  ll_getattr_dentry
    ll_glimpse_size
      ll_merge_attr_nolock
        inode_set_mtime(inode, lli->lli_mtime, 0)
        ..
        inode_set_mtime(inode, mtime, 0)

Process 2
stat("file")
  ll_getattr_dentry
    ll_glimpse_size
      ll_merge_attr_nolock
        inode_set_mtime(inode, lli->lli_mtime, 0)

Process 1
    stat->mtime = inode_get_mtime(inode);
