--- Description ---
On the "failover pair" Lustre configuration, like

    mds1_HOST=host1

    mds2_HOST=host2

    mds1failover_HOST=host2

    mds2failover_HOST=host1

fs2mdsdev can not be mounted on mds1_HOST because of fs2mdsdev has failover.node=. Mount fails as:

    Denying initial registration attempt from nid @tcp, specified as failover.
