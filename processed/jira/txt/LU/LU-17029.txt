--- Description ---
Current lustre.spec.in only matchs vendor for openEuler Linux.

But the openEuler derived OSes have a different vendor name, e.g.  KylinOS's vendor name is Kylin.

We should match rpm macro 'openEuler' in the spec for openEuler and its derived OSes a.k.a openEuler family OSes.
