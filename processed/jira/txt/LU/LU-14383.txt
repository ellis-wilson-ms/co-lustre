--- Description ---
The following race between mkdir and migrate may end with failure of mkdir:

       mkdir $DIR1/$tdir

       mkdir $DIR2/$tdir/dir2 &
       $LFS migrate -m 1 $DIR1/$tdir

Please confirm whether it is expected behavior has to be fixed.
