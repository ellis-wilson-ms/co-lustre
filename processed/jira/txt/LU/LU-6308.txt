--- Comment (niu, Mon, 2 Mar 2015 02:46:52 +0000) ---
Did you enable quota feature on the OST0034? You can check it by "cat /proc/fs/lustre/osd-ldiskfs/$OSTNAME/quota_slave/info" on OST0034, the output will look like:

target name:    lustre-OST0001
pool ID:        0
type:           dt
quota enabled:  none
conn to master: setup
space acct:     ug
user uptodate:  glb[0],slv[0],reint[0]
group uptodate: glb[0],slv[0],reint[0]

"space acct: ug" means the quota feature (user & group quota accounting) are enabled.

If the quota feature isn't enabled, you can enable it by 'tune2fs -O quota $OSTDEV" (the OST must be offline).
