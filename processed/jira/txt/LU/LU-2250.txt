--- Comment (adilger, Tue, 30 Oct 2012 00:39:41 +0000) ---
Unfortunately we will not be fixing selinux support for the 1.8 client.

Current Lustre 1.8 users are required to disable selinux in their kernel, and fixing the client code would be too much change for the stable 1.8 release, which is only receiving critical bug fixes for customers at this point.
