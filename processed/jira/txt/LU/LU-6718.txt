--- Description ---
The lustre-osd-ldiskfs-mount package did not get updated when the lustre-osd-ldiskfs was updated with lustre rpms from a repo. This is because the dependency on lustre-osd-ldiskfs-mount in lustre-osd-ldiskfs does not have a version specification.

# rpm -q --requires lustre-osd-ldiskfs  |  grep lustre-osd-ldiskfs-mount
lustre-osd-ldiskfs-mount

Now, properly version controlled, there is really no reason to upgrade lustre-osd-ldiskfs-mount with every Lustre release, but I doubt we are being disciplined enough to have proper cross-version compatibility of all of our RPM sub-package components (yet).

Probably it is our intention that lustre-osd-ldiskfs-mount always be from the same general release as the rest of the Lustre packages, yes?
