--- Description ---
We currently have the option to dump bulk page contents to the debug log on checksum failures.

It's very rarely useful to have the option to dump the contents of all bulk pages to the debug log - regardless of checksum state.  This generates a remarkable amount of log traffic and shouldn't be enabled often.
