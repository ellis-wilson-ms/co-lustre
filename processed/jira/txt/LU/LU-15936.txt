--- Description ---
The DOSTID macro is not printing the FID of llog files properly in the logs.  It is printing logids like "0x2:2147506325:0" but the FID is actually "0x1280005895:0x2:0x0", which makes debugging llog problems difficult.

The DOSTID macro should be fixed to print the llog FIDs properly.

--- Comment (adilger, Sun, 12 Jun 2022 02:28:57 +0000) ---
This DOSTID formatting problem was likely introduced by LU-9153 trying to transition the FID printing between the old format and the new format.  At this point it is very unlikely that anything is using the old format and it should just always print the llog IDs as proper FIDs.
