--- Description ---
If HAVE_LIBREADLINE is not defined then liblnetconfig.so will export readline(). This is a bug since it breaks readline if liblnetconfig is linked before libreadline.

--- Comment (jhammond, Mon, 13 Jun 2022 15:28:38 +0000) ---
> just wonder how could libreadline be linked if HAVE_LIBREADLINE is not set?

By telling the linker to do so. HAVE_READLINE depends on the readline headers being installed.
