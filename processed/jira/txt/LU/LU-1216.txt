--- Comment (laisiyao, Thu, 15 Mar 2012 02:29:40 +0000) ---
The first ll_get_dir_page() failed with errno -1 (-EPERM), this looks to be returned from MDS, could you check MDS log?

This assert is caused by statahead code, which is buggy in 2.1 code, and it's fixed in 2.2, the patch is at http://review.whamcloud.com/#change,1208, but unfortunately it depends on some other changes, so it can't be backport to 2.1 directly. To fix this issue, you can disable statahead by `echo 0 > /proc/fs/lustre/llite//statahead_max` on client, or else I can help backport the patch to 2.1, which may take a little time.
