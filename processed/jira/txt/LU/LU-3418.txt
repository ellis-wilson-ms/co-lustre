--- Description ---
Correct inaccurate references to Sun Microsystems or Oracle.

The following information addresses ownership, usage and guidelines when using the Lustre name and logo. On February 19 2013, Xyratex Technology Ltd. (“Xyratex”) announced the acquisition of Lustre and all its assets, including the use of the Lustre name and logo. 

Xyratex counts the Lustre logo among its corporate assets and the goodwill it represents. To protect this asset, Xyratex has included in the logo usage document specific usage guidelines for the Lustre logo and name. These guidelines should be followed to ensure proper usage of the Lustre trademark and to prevent any damage that could result from improper usage.

Included is an updated Lustre logo in a few of the most common print and file formats. This new logo must be used in place of any existing Lustre logos in any of your technical, marketing and sales materials. The attribution line also must be updated to reflect Xyratex’s ownership of Lustre. More details about such usage and attribution can be found within the Lustre logo guidelines document. 

A complete Lustre logo package is also available for download at the www.xyratex.com/products/lustre/trademark

Please contact trademarks@xyratex.com if you have any questions about using the Lustre logo or name.

We appreciate your compliance in meeting the stated logo and name requirements
