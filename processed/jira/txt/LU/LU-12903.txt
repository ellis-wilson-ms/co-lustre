--- Description ---
The man page for lctl-pcc does not exist on the Lustre client. 

[root@trevis-62vm8 ~]# man lctl-pcc
No manual entry for lctl-pcc
[root@trevis-62vm8 ~]# man lctl_pcc
No manual entry for lctl_pcc

Yet, ‘man lfs-pcc’ says it should exist. From the lfs-pcc man page:

      SEE ALSO
              lfs(1), lfs-hsm(1), lfs-pcc-detach(1), lctl-pcc(8)

The issue is that ‘lctl-pcc’ does not exist in the lustre/doc/Makefile.am file.

It looks like the following are also missing:

lctl-pcc.8

lfs-pcc-detach.1

llapi_pcc_attach.3

llapi_pcc_attach_fid.3

llapi_pcc_attach_fid_str.3

llapi_pcc_detach_fid.3

llapi_pcc_detach_fid_fd.3

llapi_pcc_detach_fid_str.3

llapi_pcc_detach_file.3

llapi_pccdev_get.3

llapi_pccdev_set.3

llapi_pcc_state_get.3

llapi_pcc_state_get_fd.3
