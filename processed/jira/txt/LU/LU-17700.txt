--- Comment (simmonsja, Thu, 4 Apr 2024 21:59:14 +0000) ---
Oh, that is right. o2iblnd is not really IPv6 ready with its special internal protocols.

--- Comment (ssmirnov, Thu, 4 Apr 2024 21:28:52 +0000) ---
It looks like the cause of the problem is the increased size of the buffer: if projected using "large" NIDs, it becomes large enough so that o2iblnd can't use the "IMMEDIATE" message type and tries to do an RDMA transfer to itself, and this fails: there are no errors, but the data is wrong.

It is not clear to me whether "self RDMA" is expected to work. An easy workaround is to decrease the max number of NIs in the buffer - currently it is 200, decreasing it to 100 for example allows the o2iblnd to not use RDMA and ping works.
