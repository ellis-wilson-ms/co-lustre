--- Description ---
During an eviction, Lustre calls ldlm_namespace_cleanup, and it will sometimes end up dumping all of the locks on a particular resource to the console log (ldlm_resource_complain), which is very wasteful and only rarely helpful.

Move the debug level for this to D_DLMTRACE.
