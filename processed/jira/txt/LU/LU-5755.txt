tracker for el6.6 support

now that rhel 6.6 has been announced (see http://www.redhat.com/en/about/press-releases/red-hat-enterprise-linux-66-now-generally-available) we can start tracking progress on support for it in master in a public fashion.

POR is to replace el6.5 support with el6.6 following our standard practice of only supporting the latest version, not many versions, of a given distro.
