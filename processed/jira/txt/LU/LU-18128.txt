--- Description ---
fiemap returns EINVAL when applied past EOF for a non-zero length file, which is not consistent with the EXT4 behaviour. It's also inconsistent with the Lustre behaviour for a zero length file. It's also inconsistent with the fiemap specification in linux/Documentation/filesystems/fiemap.txt.
