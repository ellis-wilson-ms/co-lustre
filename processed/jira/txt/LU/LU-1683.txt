--- Comment (yong.fan, Sat, 28 Jul 2012 07:22:29 +0000) ---
Thanks James for caching the bug. The reason is found as following:

When AGL trigger osc_enqueue_base(), it may found the cached ldlm lock on client side without AGL RPC sending. Under such case, the AGL upcall() will change the cl_lock::cll_state from CLS_QUEUING to CLS_HELD directly by cl_wait_try(). But in cl_wait_try(), such case was ignored, so the original LASSERT() for cll_state checking is not correct.

The new patch set 4 has fixed the issue. If you have chance, please try such new patch. Thanks!
