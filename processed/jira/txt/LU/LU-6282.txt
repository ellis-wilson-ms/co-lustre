--- Comment (adilger, Thu, 26 Feb 2015 23:03:04 +0000) ---
Please do not use a blanket Jira ticket to land patches unless all of the patches are related to a specific feature or improvement.  Each change or series of related changes should have its own Jira ticket.  This ticket is fine if it is for tracking those other tickets from SDSC, but in that case the description should be changed.

Also, it is preferable if the emails used for patch commits reflect the actual organization submitting the patches (e.g. dmitry.mishin@sdsc.edu) so that SDSC can get credit for their contributions in presentations that describe code contributors.  However, that is not a requirement.  It is possible to link multiple email addresses to a single account in Gerrit under your account settings so that patch contributions and commit messages reflect a "primary" email address, even if you use a different email account for OpenID authentication.
