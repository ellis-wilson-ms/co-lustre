--- Comment (adilger, Thu, 9 Dec 2021 15:05:04 +0000) ---
-30 = -EROFS, so it looks like the filesystem was mounted read-only due to the previous error. That would have been posted in the logs before the filesystem turned read-only. 

The OST needs to be unmounted, run e2fsck, and remounted.
