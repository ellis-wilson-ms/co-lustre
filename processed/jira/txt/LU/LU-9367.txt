The thinking behind it is that ll_lov_setstripe() only uses the lum to set the file's stripe, while it shouldn't have the side effect to retrieve the instantiated stripe info back to the lum.

And lustre/tests/mpi/cascading_rw.c just exploits the side effect of stripe retrieving during the stripe setting call.
