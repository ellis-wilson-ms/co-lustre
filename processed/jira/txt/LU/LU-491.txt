--- Description ---
conf-sanity/50g fail on a local setup because wait_osc_import_state() in t-f finds two imports:

osc.lustre-OST0001-osc-MDT0000.ost_server_uuid=lustre-OST0001_UUID	FULL

osc.lustre-OST0001-osc-d973213c.ost_server_uuid=lustre-OST0001_UUID	FULL

and doesn't parse this correctly

orion is affected as well
