--- Comment (isaac, Mon, 13 Aug 2012 13:31:55 +0000) ---
Another problem with this error message is that it doesn't tell us how long the tx has been actually on the wire, e.g. the error message above told us a tx expired 60 seconds (10 + default ko2iblnd timeout) after it was queued BUT:

	It could have been sitting in the TX queue waiting for a credit for the most of the 60 seconds and just barely got onto the active queue, which indicates problems with flow control protocol. Or:

	It could have spent most of the 60 seconds on the wire without completion, which usually indicates problems in the IB fabric.

It'd be very useful to be able to distinguish the two cases.
