--- Description ---
The error message of "lfs setquota is not meaningful sometimes, e.g.

# lfs setquota -u usr2341 -i 16000000 /lustre/client/
# lfs setquota -u usr2341 -I 15000000 /lustre/client/
lfs setquota: quotactl failed: Invalid argument
setquota failed: Invalid argument

It should be more meaningful "the SoftLimit can't be larger than the HardLimit"
