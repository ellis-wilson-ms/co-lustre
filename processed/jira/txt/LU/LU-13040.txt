--- Comment (stancheff, Sat, 30 Nov 2019 22:20:27 +0000) ---
This KASAN indicates two different issues,

The root cause is indicated by lsm_md_dump() where the lsm_md_pool_name is not null terminated. This case appears to be rooted in a strncpy(). attempting to read the pool_name when lsm_md_magic == LMV_MAGIC_FOREIGN.

The second issue is the theoretical buffer overflow in libcfs_debug_msg() where the second pass of snprintf() reports number of bytes need, where the results is expected to be number of bytes actual. There are a couple of additional uses of snprintf that should also be changed to scnprintf() here.
