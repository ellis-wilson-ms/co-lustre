--- Description ---
Making Lustre start up messages more brief. For example, Perhaps the typical three lines of:

Lustre: OBD class driver, info@clusterfs.com

        Lustre Version: 1.6.5

        Build Version: 1.6.5-2.1.29HD-rsrel-RB-2.1-13122-2008-08-20-19:05

could be compressed down on to one line.
