--- Description ---
Commit 6697349ea92c2c61a40b8f23655f66de60ca1501 from LU-73 introduced a minor regression in the LC_SHRINKER_WANT_SHRINK_PTR test for pre-RHEL6.1.  Use of the uninitialized variable "struct shrinker tmp" is flagged as an error, making the test fail erroneously.

I will submit a fix.
