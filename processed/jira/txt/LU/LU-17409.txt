--- Description ---
ldev is incorrectly parsing the line specifying the hosts that can

run the MGS, when of the form

    gopher1  gopher2  MGS  gopher1/mgs

as it assumes every target specified includes a filesystem name,

like 'lustre3-MDT0000'.

As a result, ldev never reports MGS targets and cannot identify the hosts or NIDs of those hosts as it should
