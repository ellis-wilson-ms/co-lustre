--- Description ---
Fileset configuration parameter is lost if nodemap are unactivated and reactivated or defined before activating nodemap. Setting admin parameter also loose fileset config.

[root@mds1 lustre-release]# lctl nodemap_add  SG1
[root@mds1 lustre-release]# lctl nodemap_add_range --name SG1 --range 192.168.10.131@tcp
[root@mds1 lustre-release]# lctl nodemap_set_fileset --name SG1 --fileset /isolated
[root@mds1 lustre-release]# lctl nodemap_activate 1
[root@mds1 lustre-release]# cat /proc/fs/lustre/nodemap/SG1/fileset

[root@mds1 lustre-release]# lctl nodemap_set_fileset --name SG1 --fileset /isolated
[root@mds1 lustre-release]# cat /proc/fs/lustre/nodemap/SG1/fileset
/isolated
[root@mds1 lustre-release]# lctl nodemap_modify --name SG1 --property admin --value 1
[root@mds1 lustre-release]# cat /proc/fs/lustre/nodemap/SG1/fileset

[root@mds1 lustre-release]# lctl nodemap_set_fileset --name SG1 --fileset /isolated
[root@mds1 lustre-release]# cat /proc/fs/lustre/nodemap/SG1/fileset

[root@mds1 lustre-release]# lctl nodemap_set_fileset --name SG1 --fileset /isolated
[root@mds1 lustre-release]# cat /proc/fs/lustre/nodemap/SG1/fileset
/isolated
[root@mds1 lustre-release]# lctl nodemap_activate 0
[root@mds1 lustre-release]# lctl nodemap_activate 1
[root@mds1 lustre-release]# cat /proc/fs/lustre/nodemap/SG1/fileset

[root@mds1 lustre-release]#

--- Comment (JIRAUSER19288, Tue, 19 Aug 2025 15:13:31 +0000) ---
This issue was resolved in the context of LU-18357. Previously, lctl nodemap_set_fileset was only a local operation and not persistent. This was fixed and further deprecated in favor of the commands lctl nodemap_fileset_add and lctl nodemap_fileset_del.
