I agree with Oleg that "more safe" doesn't provide justification for landing the patch.  I can imagine that there is some problem with these bitfields in the code, though it seems lq_rw_sem is held when lq_dirty and lq_same_space are accessed and modified, except in lod_qos_priofree_seq_write() and lod_qos_thresholdrr_seq_write().  Since there is already a lock protecting all of these accesses, adding atomic bit operations is just overhead unless there is a reason to do so.
