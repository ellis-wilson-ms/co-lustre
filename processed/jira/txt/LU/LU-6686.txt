--- Description ---
Attached is a tcpdump for an experiment running an 'fstat' ('multiop OSc') from the client. Look for the LDLM_ENQUEUE (intent : getattr) request and reply, then go on to the next LDLM_ENQUEUE request and reply. The latter is a request from the client to the OST for an extent lock. The reply is listed in the header as having three buffers:

  Lm Bufflens: 152 - ptlrpc_body

  Lm Bufflens: 112 - ldlm_reply

  Lm Bufflens:  56 - ?what?

The third buffer is not included in the output at all. This is after an update with http://review.whamcloud.com/#/c/14961/ . Prior to that update the verbose output to tshark said "generic data". Now it doesn't even say that. I am pretty sure this is supposed to be a 'struct ost_lvb' (which is 56 bytes), but can't be absolutely sure.
